TT/Lipo, breast lift and lower eyelids – Is It too much for my body to absorb or should I have them do
Combined procedures
Jan 14, 2020
Combining all those procedures into a single surgery can be done and can be done safely, but then key is finding a surgeon who is comfortable performing all those procedures at once and who can do the procedures in an efficient manner so the length of surgery is not excessive.
What is the suggested time in between BBL and rhinoplasty surgeries?
BBL and rhinoplasty
Jan 14, 2020
I think it would be okay to have both procedures performed at the same time, although it would make the rhinoplasty recovery more difficult. Rhinoplasty patients are usually more comfortable laying in bed upright after surgery, but following BBL you would need to be on your belly or on your sides.
If you do separate the procedures I would recommend doing them at least 3 months apart.
If you suffer with endometriosis, can you still get a BBL done?
Endometriosis
Jan 14, 2020
Having endometriosis should not impact your candidacy for elective plastic surgery procedures. Your surgeon should do a thorough evaluation and history and physical to look for anything that may increase your risk of having a complication.
What would be more beneficial for me; Liposuction or Tummy Tuck?
Lipo versus tummy tuck
Jan 14, 2020
Given the stretch marks I seen in your photos that I imagine come along with some skin laxity, I think you would be better served with a tummy tuck. I would need make the ultimate assessment in person following a thorough evaluation at which point we could discuss the pros and cons of each procedure.

Prenancy after surgery

What are the pros or cons of breast massage after surgery?
Breast massage
Jan 14, 2020
I do not have my patients perform breast massage after surgery. I think the utility of massage is controversial. One recent study looking at risks of capsular contracture actually found that breast massage increased the risk of capsular contracture. Ultimately I would go with your plastic surgeon’s recommendation.
What would be the best procedure to fix my uneven breasts and larger nipple?
Breast asymmetry
Jan 14, 2020
I think a bilateral breast augmentation with a lift or small reduction on the right side may be a good option. A thorough examination by a board-certified plastic surgeon would be needed to come up with a definitive plan.
Can doxycycline be used in place of the traditional prophylactic liposuction treatment?
Doxycycline
Jan 14, 2020
Doxycycline should be just fine (given your allergies) to protect against the types of bacteria for which we give antibiotics before and after surgery.
Hi! I’m 24 years young and I have PCOS (Polycystic ovary syndrome). (Hormone imbalance) Do I still qua
PCOS
Apr 25, 2019
PCOS itself you not preclude you from being a candidate for BBL surgery. I would require medical clearance from your primary care doctor or endocrinologist to make sure there aren’t any other risk factors that would need to be addressed.
Will CO2 laser help remove under eye bags?
Under eye bags
Apr 25, 2019
Under eye bags can be caused by a variety of factors. CO2 laser resurfacing will tighten and skin and possibly improve the texture, tone, and quality. If those are the issues contributing to your bags then the laser treatment can help. The laser won’t do anything to address any bulging fat, but if the skin is tightened over the area then the fat may appear to bulge less.
I’m getting my implants removed after 3 months. Would I be able to get en bloc capsulectomy?
Capsulectomy
Apr 25, 2019
At 3 months following surgery you will have a capsule formed. While it may be possible to remove it, I think it is unlikely that removing it would provide any health benefit.
2 weeks post op and still pretty sore with muscle spasms frequently, is that normal?
Breast augmentation recovery
Apr 25, 2019
Having pain and spasms just 2 weeks following breast augmentation surgery is completely normal and pretty much expected. You will find that bot the frequency and intensity of these feelings decrease over time.
