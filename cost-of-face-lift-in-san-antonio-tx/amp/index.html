Cost Of Face Lift In San Antonio, TX

A facelift in San Antonio TX is a major operation to eliminate the age-related changes of the skin of the face and neck, and is becoming more and more popular today.

What is the reason for the increased attention to these operations, both from the side of surgeons and from patients? The very first signs of aging are reflected, above all, on the face.

The skin and muscles of the face are in constant motion, reflecting our emotions, feelings, thoughts.

This is typical only for humans, animals do not have so many facial muscles and skin mobility.

However, high mobility and constant movement of tissues also have a reverse side.

Ptosis of the soft tissues of the face occurs under the influence of gravity, deep folds around the mouth are formed, cheeks and chin hang, wrinkles appear … A well-groomed appearance, a youthful face is increasingly today not only a simple desire of the patient, but also a professional necessity. It is these reasons that make patients contact a plastic surgeon.

The first surgery to correct age-related changes in face surgeons began to perform as early as the early 1900s. The result of the long work was the creation of highly effective and safe techniques for surgical rejuvenation of the face and neck.

Currently, the following main types of facelift and neck lift are distinguished:

  • Simple (cutaneous) cervico-facial – without interference on deep muscular structures;
  • SMAS facelift – with intervention on deep muscular structures;
  • Facelift the upper third of the face;
  • Sectoral mini facelift (lifting of the cheekbone orbital area, eyebrows, temporal lifting)
  • A simple (cutaneous) cervico-facial facelift.

Cervico-facial lifting is a common operation, the purpose of which is to surgically correct the signs of aging of the neck and lower third of the face. The essence of this operation is to lift the skin only.

The advantage of this operation is the relatively low traumatism and ease of execution. Unfortunately, the results after such a facelift are rarely long-term, since the surgeon does not pick up the main array of soft facial tissues, so this operation is performed strictly according to the indications to patients with thin skin and a fine-wrinkled type of its aging.

SMAS facelift.

About this technique of facelift I will tell in more detail, as it is the basis for correction of age-related changes in the face. The incision passes in front of the auricle, behind the tragus bends around the earlobe and then goes in the posterolateral fold. Skin and subcutaneous tissue exfoliate in volume individually for each patient. During the operation, using special techniques, the surgeon assesses how much it is necessary to move the muscular structures.

The superficial muscular-fascial layer rises to the top and is fixed in a new position in the so-called “anchor” zones.

In a number of cases, the surgeon not only excludes surplus of the superficial muscular-fascial layer, but also removes the fatty body of the cheek, hangs the soft tissues on special threads.

This technique allows to lift and fix in the new position the entire complex of soft facial tissues. Thanks to this, the rejuvenating effect of the operation is maintained for a long time. The facelift is completed by applying a cosmetic suture and a sterile pressure bandage.

Doctor Richard A. Levine Lower SMAS Facelift

Lifting the upper third of the face (endoscopic face lift).

Endoscopic facelift in San Antonio is carried out with the help of endoscopic technique and thanks to this the operation is performed from minimal incisions. The essence of the operation is to tighten the skin of the forehead, eyebrows, and the temporal region.

Two incisions of 0.5-1.5 cm long are made in the scalp. Special instruments are introduced through them and under the control of the endoscope (video camera) soft tissues exfoliate and move upward to a new position.

Fixation of the complex of displaced tissues is carried out with polypropylene threads or special biological glue.

Endoscopic facelift

This technique can also be called an endoscopic facelift. This is a low-traumatic technique for correcting local zones of skin sagging on the face and neck. It is shown to patients at a relatively young age and is performed, as a rule, by an endoscopic method.

The length of the incisions does not exceed 0.5-4 cm. There are incisions in the parotid, temporal regions, along the ciliary edge of the lower eyelid, and, if necessary, in the oral cavity. The advantage of the operation is low-trauma and, as a consequence, rapid rehabilitation. The effect of the operation is clearly visible in the coming days and lasts for a long time.

Anesthesia is endotracheal anesthesia.

Time of stay in a hospital is one day.

The time for full rehabilitation is from two weeks to two months, and sometimes longer, depending on the volume of the operation performed, the individual characteristics of the patient’s tissues, the course of rehabilitation and the observance of the doctor’s recommendations.
