How long does a facelift usually take?
Facelift
Dec 27, 2017
While a facelift sounds like a “standard” procedure, it is not. It is a generic description that may encompass a variety of separate manuveurs or steps. There are even many variations on each of these components. As such, what may be referred to as a “facelift” may vary considerably in operating time from an hour or 2 to up to 6 to 8 hours. Your surgeon may customize your “facelift” more if there is a concern about total operating time. Robin T.W. Yuan, M.D.
