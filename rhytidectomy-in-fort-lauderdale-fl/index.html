Plastic surgery of the face and neck, whose purpose is to eliminate age-related changes, is called a facelift. It is relevant after 45 years of age. At this age, the skin loses its natural elasticity, becomes flabby and sagging.

When it is necessary to do a facelift and neck lift?

If there is a sagging skin on the cheekbones, cheeks, on the lower contour of the face, deep vertical wrinkles, nasolabial folds, double chin have long appeared, and all this causes psychological discomfort, it makes sense to seek the help of a plastic surgeon.

Modern facelift in Fort Lauderdale will help to effectively eliminate this problem, to restore the attractiveness of the exterior.

Contraindications to facelift and neck lift surgeries

Before And After Liquid Facelift Fort Lauderdale

Facelift is contraindicated if ithere are: infectious diseases, severe illnesses, blood clotting problems. It should be noted that in patients with diabetes mellitus, the healing of scars can be slowed down. Smoking worsens the healing process of scars, and in some cases can lead to necrosis. If the patient is a smoker, he should give up this bad habit for at least 2-3 weeks.

What is important in planning a facelift and neck lift surgery?

When planning an operation, try to find out about all the details of the procedure. Ask questions until then, until you understand what results you will be able to achieve. The surgeon should be aware of all your diseases and all the medications that you are taking to give you recommendations that will help you better prepare for the operation.

Terms of preparation for facelift

With a fat type of skin and enlarged pores, it is necessary to clean the face for 1-2 weeks. Preliminary cleaning will significantly improve the skin condition after surgery. Before the operation, you need to give up drinking alcohol, because they increase the risk of bleeding. The use of anticoagulants, such as aspirin and the like, should also be discontinued.

Lift Plastic Surgery With Doctor Daniel Man

Before the operation, wash your head and dry your hair. The surgeon will perform a special marking, your hair will be divided into strands and fixed with the help of rubber bands. After the operation, the length of the hair will remain the same.

Anesthesia during facelift and neck lift surgery

Facelifting is performed under general anesthesia. The advantages of general anesthesia are that the patient does not feel anything, he just sleeps. And you wakes up only when the operation is over.

How is the facelift and neck lift performed?

The surgeon makes incisions in the temporal region of the scalp, as well as near the ears and around the occiput. This removes unnecessary areas of soft tissue.

The skin on the cheeks tightens, the facial contour improves, the sagging on the chin and neck is removed. The double chin is corrected by liposuction. If the face has a deficiency of adipose tissue, a problem with sunken cheeks or deep folds of the face is possible. It is eliminated by lipofilling. That is, fat taken from other parts of the body, for example, from the abdomen and thighs, is transferred to problem areas. At the end of operation, the doctor superimposes cosmetic seams and fixing dressings. The surgeon installs drains in the wounds, which removes the next day.

Features of the recovery period after facelift surgery

After the facelift in Fort Lauderdale, the patient should be in the clinic for 2-3 days. The doctor prescribes antibiotics, anesthetics and medications that reduce edema. 10-14 days will have to wear a special bandage.

That bruises and edema completely disappear, it will take 3-4 weeks. On the 4th-5th day the doctor prescribes a number of cosmetic procedures that are aimed at reducing bruising and swelling. Such procedures are mandatory, because they speed up the recovery process. During recovery period, physical activities are not recommended. Go to work and return to your normal regime can only after 3-4 weeks.

At first the face will not be beautiful. Edema and bruises will distort the appearance, the face will seem to you too tense.

Some patients are even upset, and believe that they did not get the expected result. Most often, disappointment is premature. On the second and third week, facial features change for the better, the edema and bruises disappear, the well-being improves, and the sensitivity of the skin and face as a whole is restored.

Time passes and the skin becomes soft, without tension, seals disappear. After 2 months, the final result of facelift is seen: the scars are noticeably white, and after six months they become completely invisible. Positive result of a facelift is quite long-term. It persists for 7-10 years. Facelift can be combined with blepharoplasty or rhinoplasty

What can not be achieved by a facelift?

Facelift and neck lift in Fort Lauderdale is an effective operation to improve the appearance of the face. However, one method does not solve all the problems of aging.

Facelift does not remove all the wrinkles on the face, does not eliminate the deep nasolabial folds, glabellar wrinkles, not fighting with the “crow’s feet”, does not alter the anatomical features of the face and neck.
