Can PRP increase growth of hair that has always been short and thin?
PRP can show significant improvement to ALL hair.
Apr 29, 2021
It’s not uncommon for our patients to come back noting improvement in the caliber of ALL their hair with PRP. In many cases the results can be stunning. Obviously, outcomes can vary from person to person, but given the minimal side-effect profile of PRP, I think it would be worthwhile for you assuming there are no contraindications. Keep in mind, most patients will require multiple injections of PRP to see improvements and we recommend ongoing PRP therapy as maintenance if you like your result.
