Thomas Buonassisi, MD

Name: Thomas Buonassisi, MD
Statement: Dr. Thomas Buonassisi, a board-certified Facial Plastic and reconstructive surgeon located in Vancouver, BC, brings more than two decades of expertise in cosmetic surgery to his practice. Alongside his proficient team of specialists, he offers a comprehensive range of surgical and non-surgical treatments aimed at rejuvenating and reshaping the face and skin.

Dr. Buonassisi's journey in medicine commenced at McGill University, where he completed his Undergraduate Degree in Classical Piano. He then pursued his medical education at the University of British Columbia, followed by a five-year surgical residency in Otolaryngology–Head and Neck Surgery. Upon concluding his residency, Dr. Buonassisi furthered his training by relocating to Australia, where he completed a fellowship in Facial Plastic and reconstructive surgery under the auspices of the Australian Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery.

Continuously dedicated to refining his skills, Dr. Buonassisi achieved certification from the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (ABFPRS). The ABFPRS is a unique medical board in North America, exclusively certifying physicians in cosmetic and reconstructive plastic surgery of the face and neck. Earning this certification necessitates rigorous training and extensive experience in facial plastic surgery, making Board Certification by the ABFPRS a globally recognized credential in Facial Plastic Surgery.

Dr. Buonassisi serves as the leader of the team at 8 West Cosmetic Surgery & Skin Clinic, drawing on his extensive experience, which spans over two decades of performing facial cosmetic surgery. Dr. Buonassisi's commitment to excellence has led him to travel worldwide, collaborating with esteemed experts in the field and working closely with renowned surgeons in both the United States and Australia. His portfolio includes thousands of successful facial cosmetic procedures, a testament to his surgical expertise honed to the level of artistry.
