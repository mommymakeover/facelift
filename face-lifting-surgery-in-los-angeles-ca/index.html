Varieties of facelift.

The easiest method of facelift is rhytidectomy or skin tightening. With rhytidectomy, skin excesses are removed, the remaining skin is stretched, cosmetic sutures are superimposed, so the usual skin tension causes wrinkles to be smoothed. But a significant disadvantage of rhytidectomy is the distortion effect when facial features usually do not change for the better: the eyelids narrow down, the smile can become a kind of grin, and the facial expression becomes unnatural.

A Wide Range Of Non-surgical Facial Rejuvenation Options

To keep the usual facial expression and do not lose the individuality of the features during rejuvenate procedures today is possible with the help of SMAS facelift is one of the most demanded plastic surgeries. SMAS facelift is the so-called deep lift with the capture of dense tissue. During surgery, the entire facial muscle layer with aponeuroses (SMAS stands for “superficial muscular aponeurotic layer”) is lifted, excess skin and muscular tissue are excised, and the skin is then stretched. Thus, the muscular frame of the face is strengthened, so the effect of SMAS facelift lasts for many years.

Many patients want to achieve rejuvenation, using an endoscopic facelift surgery is a method that allows the surgeon to dispense with large incisions on the skin. During the operation, an endoscope is used (micro-video camera), and instruments are introduced through a very small incision (about 1 cm) in the scalp area.

The surgeon monitors the progress of the operation using an image that is displayed on the screen through the optical system. In the necessary places, the muscles are neatly stratified and then stretched. This technology of rejuvenation leaves almost no traces – post-operative scars due to hair can not be seen.

Features of the operation.

Facelifting in Los Angeles is performed under general anesthesia, and the duration of such surgery varies greatly depending on the complexity of the technique used and the end goals – from 40 minutes to 4-5 hours. During the facelift surgery, the whole face (complex facelift) and its individual parts can be affected.

The incisions are performed in the natural folds of the skin (in the ears, on the scalp), which makes the postoperative scars invisible to the outside view. The plastic surgeon chooses the method of facelift based on the patient’s age, the desired effect and the individual characteristics of the organism. In some cases, the best results are given by a combination of a facelift with blepharoplasty or liposuction of the chin area.

Facelift in men has some nuances: the plastic surgeon must take into account the property of hair growth on the cheeks and chin. If you ignore this problem, the results can be unpredictable – perhaps a visible shift in the growth of the whiskers and beard behind the ears.

Recovery period

Within 1-2 days after the facelift in Los Angeles, it is recommended to stay in the hospital. The first day the patient will have to suffer a tight gauze bandage on his face. This dressing is necessary to prevent the spread of swelling of the tissues. Sutures are removed after about 7-10 days.

For some time the patient may disturb an itch, pain, feeling of tightness of the skin on the face and a violation of its sensitivity. After 15-20 days the patient can do his daily business. You will need to take care of the face with extreme caution for several months. In addition, it is undesirable to play sports, go swimming, bath or sauna and sunbathe on the sun or in a tanning bed for six weeks after surgery. The preliminary result of a facelift is visible after 2-3 weeks, and the final effect is estimated after 2-3 months.

Contraindications to facelift surgery.

This plastic surgery is contraindicated for people who have a history of the following health problems:

  • oncological diseases;
  • acute gastric diseases;
  • diabetes;
  • diseases with violation of blood coagulability.
