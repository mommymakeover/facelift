Faith feels as strongly about ongoing education as she does about the demographically-driven, growing need for aesthetic care.

Biography: Geri graduated in 1996 from Central Missouri State University in Warrensburg Missouri with a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Nursing. Geri enjoys being a nurse, caring and helping others feel and look their best. Her aesthetics’ training includes injection expertise in Botox, Dysport, Juvederm, Restylane, Perlane and Voluma XC.
