Donor area redness
Apr 23, 2020
Some redness, or erythema, of the skin after an FUE procedure is normal, both in the donor and recipient areas. This can last from a few weeks up to 3-4 months after an FUE procedure. Continue with your post-op care and over time this redness improves significantly! The larger the case, the more likely redness is to occur.
