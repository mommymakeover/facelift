Can you have capsule removal, breast lift and fat grafting at the same time in one surgery?
Great question.
May 26, 2023
In my practice, many patients do elect to have a breast lift when undergoing breast explant surgery. I do recommend waiting at least 6 months post explant surgery before having a fat graft to the breast. That allows for adequate healing time, increased patient safely, and a greater probability for a successful transfer.
Does Breast Implant Removal Require Breast Lift Surgery?
You often do need a lift at the time of explant surgery
May 19, 2020
I often recommend a lift at the same time as explant surgery. As the tissues become stretched and expanded with an implant over time, they often do not contract sufficiently post-operatively and need some skin removal/lifting. This will depend on many factors including the size of your implant, elasticity of your skin, amount of breast tissue, etc. 
Can I play golf 6 weeks after the surgery?
Activity at 6 weeks
Apr 19, 2018
Yes. Most procedures require a recovery of 6 weeks prior to engaging in any activity that could be considered strenuous. Always be sure to discuss with your surgeon, at your post operative appointments, to make sure you are healing well, with no complications that would prolong activity.
Is it normal to have a 400cc implant completely removed and drains placed under local anesthesia?
Implant removal is possible under local anesthesia but not with a capsulectomy
Aug 16, 2017
If you are simply removing the implants and no capsule tissue, then it is very easy to do under local. If however you are having the capsule removed at the same time (which is standard of care in my practice), local would be painful for you to tolerate. Drains are usually used when the capsule is removed and not necessary when only the implants are removed. Good luck!

Aug 15, 2017
