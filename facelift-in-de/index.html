Facelift In Delaware

Facelift is conventionally divided into three zones:

The upper part of the face. Eliminates shortcomings in the forehead and eyebrows area. The skin incision made at the hairline;
Middle part of the face. The problems in the zone of the nose, lips and cheeks are corrected. An incision passes near the temples and in front of the ear;
Lower part of the face. Corrected chin and neck defects. The incision is carried out under the chin along the jaw line. Often surgeons combine these stages with each other.

Indications and contraindications

Facelift surgery in Delaware is appropriate after the age of 35 years. At this age, there is a worsening of the contours (they seem to swim), fatty tissue becomes larger. The following changes are also noticeable:

  • Ptosis of soft tissues;
  • creases in the neck area;
  • Sagging of the muscle fibers;
  • Deep wrinkles;
  • Double chin;
  • jawls.

You will be denied in the conduct of operations, if there are such restrictions:

  • Neoplasms of a different nature;
  • Diseases of the blood flow;
  • Exacerbation of somatic diseases;
  • Inflammatory, infectious and viral processes in the body;
  • Autoimmune diseases;
  • Dermatological diseases of the skin of the face.

Recent chemical peelings or laser resurfacing are relative contraindications. The operation is delayed until the dermis is completely restored. Methods of facelift

Space Lift

The operation consists in restoring the tissues under the mimic muscles. The angle of the neck and chin, the outer corner of the eye, the cheekbones, and the nasolabial folds are adjusted.

The scalpel is from the ear to the temporal region. In the main moments an endoscope is involved. The device finds fat spaces and tightens them. At the end, a fixing mask is applied.

The main advantages are the effectiveness lasts about 15-20 years, the facial expression does not change, the derma is not peeling, the recovery takes 10 days, after which the woman can start the habitual way of life for her.

SMAS Facelift

Not only dermal layers are tightened, but also muscular. A good result when combined with liposuction of the chin and neck. A classic incision is made: from the temple along the fibrous line to the anterior part of the lobe, going behind the auricle.

Then sagging layers of the dermis are pulled along with the muscle tissues, after which the muscles exfoliate from the tendon part. The flap is attached to the fascia of the temporal bone with a pair of sutures. Excess skin is removed. The disadvantage is the possibility of damage to nerve fibers, which is fraught with hematomas, infection and displacement of facial features. The effect of rejuvenation persists for 10 years.

The advantage of this method is small incisions of the skin, as well as work on the deep structure of the face. It turns out the most natural rejuvenation.

Endoscopic facelift

Small incisions (1-2cm) pass discontinuously along the hairline, leaving behind the ears. Also a couple of punctures are carried out in the mouth. With the help of hardware tubes, the skin separates from the periosteum, moves and is fixed to a new position.

Within a week the woman can start working duties, but the final result can be estimated only after six months. The method is widely used, because does not leave scars, the risks of complications are minimized. In addition, the volume of cheekbones increases, and the deepest wrinkles are eliminated. The downside is a low efficiency with excess epidermis. The effect lasts about 10 years.

Platysmaplasty

Patients after 50 years of age need surgery. The procedure involves tighting the platysm (subcutaneous muscle of the neck) to the center and to the sides and fixing it in a new position. As a result, the transverse folds and the lowering of the double chin are eliminated, the cervical-chin angle is sharpened. A four-centimeter incision passes along the natural cervical furrow. The result persists for 5-10 years.

Cheek Lift

Intervention changes the shape of the cheekbones to a greater or lesser extent. There are three ways of access – through the mouth, the temporal region and behind the ears. The seam is not more than a centimeter.

Corrected insufficient expression, broad cheek bone, the proportions of the face are corrected. When the volume is increased, silicone implants of oval or ellipsoidal type are set, with decreasing bone is actively ground.

The operation is carried out and the ladies and young girls. A specialty of recovery period is the intake of only liquid food through the tube for two weeks and rinsing the mouth with a special disinfectant solution.

Cervicoplasty

It is an action to eliminate the sagging of the chin zone by excising the excess fat layer. The main difference from platysmoplasty is the movement of only the skin, not the muscles.

Often combined with liposuction and facelift. The operation requires an incision in the ears and in the projection of the thyroid gland. The dermis exfoliates from the connective tissue, stretches and is fixed.

Excess skin is resected. Scars remain invisible. The age of patients for surgical intervention must be at least 45 years.
