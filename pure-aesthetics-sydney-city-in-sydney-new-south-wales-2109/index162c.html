Doctors

Steve Merten, FRACS

Name: Steve Merten, FRACS
Statement: Dr. Steve Merten, FRACS is a specialist plastic surgeon in Sydney, Australia, with over 20 years of surgical experience. A recognized leader in the field of cosmetic plastic surgery, Dr. Merten trained at the esteemed Royal Australian College of Surgeons. He specializes in reconstructive and cosmetic plastic surgery.

At his Sydney practice, Pure Aesthetics, Dr. Merten specializes in all aspects of body work, including cosmetic breast enhancement surgery and body-contouring procedures such as tummy tucks and body lifts following major weight loss.

After receiving his medical degree from Sydney University, Dr. Merten completed fellowships in cosmetic surgery at Melbourne Institute of Plastic Surgery and Monash University in Australia, and in breast reconstruction at the University of British Columbia, Canada.

Dr. Merten is board-certified by the Medical Board of Australia in plastic surgery and by the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons in plastic and reconstructive surgery.

A leader in plastic surgery education and training, Dr. Merten is frequently invited to speak at plastic surgery conferences. Additionally, he supervises plastic surgery training at one of Sydney’s major public hospitals. In his role directing fellows in plastic surgery, he has personally trained over 30 local and international plastic surgeons. Dr. Merten has been widely published in international peer-recognized medical and plastic surgery journals, and he is active in plastic surgery research.
