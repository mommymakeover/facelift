Kathleen Waldorf, MD, FACS

Name: Kathleen Waldorf, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Kathleen Waldorf, MD, FACS is a board-certified plastic surgeon with 28 years of experience. The founder of the Waldorf Center for Plastic Surgery in Portland, Oregon, she specializes in a range of surgical procedures including facial rejuvenation, body contouring, breast augmentation, and mommy makeover.

“Plastic surgery should be empowering for the patient, and it is my privilege to provide that for people,” says Dr. Waldorf. “I enjoy the artistry of plastic surgery and the individualized approach to each patient.”

Dr. Waldorf received her MD at Georgetown University in Washington, D.C., where she also completed her general surgery residency. She went on to complete a plastic and reconstructive surgery residency at Oregon Health & Science University in Portland.

She is an active member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, as well as a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.
