Fillers after rhino and before revision. Is this dangerous?
Fillers before Revision Rhinoplasty
Aug 16, 2019
Fillers can be safe after a rhinoplasty should they be done by a skilled and experienced surgeon following a physical examination and thorough assessment. Do bear in mind that it is advisable to get any filler that is injected dissolved before the revision procedure to allow clear visualisation of what areas need to be addressed. Best of luck.
GB
Is this a Pollybeak Deformity?
Rhinoplasty and Swelling
Aug 16, 2019
It is quite common to feel concerned with the appearance of the nose in the first few months following your rhinoplasty. It is still very early stages of your recovery and there is still swelling present which can alter the shape of the nose. I would advise allowing time for the swelling to go down before reassessing. You should also ensure you are having follow ups with your surgeon to keep an eye on your progress.
Kind regards,
GB
