Submuscular vs Subglandular? 7 years ago I had silicone implants put above the muscle.
redo breast augment for capsular contracture
Apr 20, 2018
You will need capsulectomies and new textured implants placed under the muscle. Placing the implants under the muscle will increase your chances of staying soft long term.
48 hrs post op. Gauze sticking to anchor incision
sticking gauze to breast wound
Apr 12, 2018
I would let my surgeon remove this as it may pull the incision open. Saline on the gauze will eventually allow it's removal. Better to wait a week so the wound can heal more before taking off with less chance of pulling it open.
Blister from steri strip?
blister following breast surgery
Apr 12, 2018
You have a very small blister which results from swelling and a shearing force on the steri strip. Just use antibiotic ointment on it twice daily and it should heal without any complications.

nipple sensation
