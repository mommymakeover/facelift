Mar 27, 2019
Hi! In view of your moderate skin laxity, the lack of any muscle banding in the front of your neck, and the presence of jowls, I think that a limited necklift should work very well for you. Can fat transfer really help me or is the only solution a full surgical arm lift?
Full Arm Lift
Mar 11, 2019
A full surgical arm lift will remove excess skin, but will not improve the quality of the remaining skin. Fat injection would, I believe, be equally ineffective. With fat transfer the skin envelope would be filled out more, but there would still be the "crepiness" of the remaining skin.

Options
