Best Face Lift In Toronto, ON

The technique of plastic operation always includes a cut, excision of excess skin, its tightening and fixing (or, according to a number of techniques, exfoliation of the skin and some soft tissues to reduce the pulling load on the seam). This technique is not very appetizing, so we will not dwell on it here, it has been described many times. We will only mention that sometimes during the facelift, liposuction is also done – excess fat (from the front surface of the cheeks, from the chin zone) is removed, which significantly improves the facial contours.

The main types of facelift in Toronto ON:

– Deep facelift;
– Lifting the middle third of the face;
– Facelift with the help of threads (for details about the filament lifting).

Types of face lift differ according to the following features:

  • Type of incision;
  • The number of layers of tissue exposed to the intervention;
  • The area of the face that undergoes the lift procedure;

The degree of invasiveness surgical intervention.

Deep facelift, affecting, respectively, deep layers of the skin, as well as muscles, allows you to get the maximum effect. This type of face lift is most suitable for patients aged who want to get the “effect on the face” in the shortest possible time.

The next, less invasive facelift is the subperiostal method, when the lifting of tissue is performed over the bone. The third kind is a skin-lift. It is used for patients with small changes that require correction.

The term mini-facelift designates the operation with the least degree of surgical intervention. This method is most suitable for young patients who do not need a traditional facelift. Temporal facelift is very popular now – the facelift method is performed in the upper third of the face.

A small incision is made on the temples in the hair and the fascia of the temporal muscle is pulled up, and the excess skin is excised. There are several “secrets” in carrying out a temporal facelift, that help to keep the hair. The main one is that the tissues do not coagulate in the area of hair growth and the skin is not sewn in tension, all the tension is in the deep tissues.

The effect is felt right away: the corners of the eyebrows and the outer corners of the upper eyelids are lifted, wrinkles are smoothed out in the corners of the eyes, nasolabial folds become less pronounced. Most patients can go to work on the second day. The effect of rejuvenation on the facelift remains from 5 to, according to some sources, 15 years.

Years pass, the skin again undergoes age-related changes. Usually, the need for a secondary facelift arises after the previous procedure, when parts that have not undergone surgical intervention begin to protrude.

The volume of secondary facelift depends on the amount of previous interventions and age changes at the time of treatment. Facelift allows you to eliminate wrinkles in the neck, face, near the eyes, while ensuring a lasting effect that persists for several years. Today facelift is considered the most effective way of surgical rejuvenation.

Laser Skin Tightening To Stimulate Collagen Remodeling

The age-related changes is to reduce of the amount of elastin and collagen in the skin, sooner or later cause the appearance of wrinkles on the face and neck, which in turn are some of the most noticeable signs of an aging person. In the plastic surgery clinic, a facelift in Toronto ON is performed by the classical method: excess skin is removed, tissues that form irregularities and wrinkles. Surgery is performed under general anesthesia.

Through the incisions of the skin that are performed in the behind-the-ear area, in the region of the scalp, tools are introduced into the subcutaneous tissue by which the skin is separated. After the skin of the face and neck becomes mobile, it is tightened, thereby smoothing wrinkles.

Excess skin is removed, after which cosmetic sutures are applied.

Sutures are usually removed on the 7-10th day after the operation. The preliminary result can be assessed after 2-3 weeks, the final result after 1.5-2 months, which are necessary for complete healing. Complications are extremely rare and, as a rule, depend on the qualification of the plastic surgeon and the technical equipment of the operating room.

The effect of facelift in Toronto lasts from 5 to 7 years. Age-related changes in the skin, which inevitably lead to the appearance of wrinkles, reduce over time the effect of facelift, but subsequently a reoperative operation is possible, which will ensure rejuvenation for another 5-7 years.

At the disposal of experienced plastic surgeons are Operating rooms fully equipped with modern operating equipment, as well as its own hospital for monitoring the patient’s condition in the early postoperative period. Long-term practice in the field of surgical facial rejuvenation, a full range of supporting cosmetic procedures and attention to the wishes of patients help to achieve the maximum effect from the operation.
