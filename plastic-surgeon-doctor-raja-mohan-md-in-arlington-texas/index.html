Can I have Breast Augmentation without undergoing HRT?
Breast Augmentation
May 14, 2020
The short answer is yes but it is worth considering the pros and cons. HRT can increase nipple and breast size and potentially accommodate a larger implant. Of course HRT has its own side effects that some patients are not fond of. When undergoing breast augmentation without HRT, I recommend smaller implants since the pocket is tighter and the nipple size is smaller. Overall breast augmentation is a procedure with a high satisfaction rate.
What FFS procedures should I get to help give my face a more feminine look?
Facial Feminization
May 14, 2020
In facial feminization, I believe it is important to analyze each part of the face to determine a treatment plan. Most patients benefit from cheek augmentation, chin augmentation, rhinoplasty, forehead reduction, and lip augmentation. Some patients are good candidates for a subset of the procedures whereas others undergo all of them. It really depends on a case by case basis. Hope this helps.
Facial feminization and Botox fillers?
Facial Feminization
May 14, 2020
Botox in my opinion is meant to treat wrinkles and does not really feminize the face. There are a series of procedures under the category of Facial Feminization that can help feminize the face. Some patients desire doing one at a time but other have multiple procedures done at once. The key is the procedures should be done safely in an efficient manner by a board certified plastic surgeon.

Is there a rule of thumb or tips for choosing breast augmentation sizes?
Breast Implant Sizing
Feb 22, 2019
Great question. There is no formula or hard and fast rule for picking an implant size. I always tell my patients to look at pictures online and try to find sizes that they desire.
