Where is PRP injected in vaginal area during O-shot?
O-shot injection
Aug 31, 2017
Thanks for your interest in the O-Shot. Typically two areas are injected with prp in the O-shot. One area is near the urethra inside the vagina called the O-spot (or G-spot) and the other area is around the clitoris. How long do dissolvable stitches from labiaplasty take to dissolve?
Labiaplasty sutures
Aug 24, 2017
Feeling the sutures at one end of the labial incision is not uncommon. The length of time for the suture to dissolve depends on the type of suture material used and the rate at which your body is able to dissolve the suture. Most sutures dissolve within 6-8 weeks. If you notice signs of infection notify your surgeon.
