Do I need a free nipple graft?
Free Nipple Graft is not required!
Jan 25, 2019
I disagree. I do not believe you will require a “free nipple graft” because the distance from your nipple to the fold underneath your breast looks relatively short. The shorter the distance, the less risk of vascular compromise of the nipple/areola complex. Also, it appears that you may be a candidate for a no vertical scar technique because the distance between your new nipple position and the top of your current areola position is sufficient enough to avoid the vertical scar.
Do I qualify for the mini tummy tuck?
A more dramatic result with a full tummy tuck!
Jan 24, 2019
I believe that you will get a more dramatic result with a full tummy tuck. The risk of limiting your surgery to a mini tummy tuck is that you will be disappointed with the contrast between a tight lower abdomen and less tight upper abdomen.
Downtime & recovery of liposuction vs. tummy tuck w/ lipo: is the recovery similar?
Tummy tuck is a more intense procedure than Liposuction alone!
Jan 24, 2019
Tummy tuck with or without liposuction is a more intense procedure than Liposuction alone. The typical liposuction patient should be back to normal daily activities with in 4 to 7 days. A Tummy tuck usually requires 11 to 14 days before a patient is ready to return to normal daily activities.

Juvederm vs Restylane
Find a skilled Injector and leave the decision to them!
Jan 24, 2019
Juvederm and Restylane are excellent products and in fact each is a family of products. Juvederm family includes Ultra, Ultra Plus, Voluma, Vollure, and Volbella. Restylane family includes Lyft, Silk, Refyne, and Defyne. Each product has its own unique qualities and attributes. I recommend that you find a skilled injector and trust his or her judgement which product would be correct choice for you.

I would not recommend a facelift for you and in fact if you came to my office for a consultation I would refuse to do the surgery. In my opinion you have volume loss in the prejowl/lateral chin area. How effective would lipo on my stomach be for me?
Liposuction is the recommendation!
Jan 23, 2019
I believe you are a good candidate for liposuction. Your skin quality looks good. I do not see any surgical scars or significant stretch marks that may limit the results. Based upon the photos you provided, I would recommend liposuction.
