What is a short scar facelift?
Facelift Incisions
Apr 1, 2018
A short scar facelift refers to a variety of incision types that reduce the total length of incisions during a facial rejuvenation surgery. Classically this was described as having all of the incisions in front of the ear, occasionally going behind the crease of the ear. In general this procedure is an option for younger patients with minimal or no skin laxity in the neck. If there is a lot of loose skin in the neck, then a hidden hairline incision behind the ear will be required.
