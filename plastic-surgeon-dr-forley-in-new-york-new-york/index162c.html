Time between Morpheus8 treatments
Morpheus8 Treatment Timing
Jun 25, 2023
A minimum of 4 weeks is the recommended interval between Morpheus8 treatments. This is felt to give the skin a chance to recover from the previous treatment before delivering additional
radiofrequency energy in the subsequent session. However, there is no study that has specifically looked at 3 versus 4 week intervals.

Treatment of Marionette Lines
Oct 30, 2022

Can I have liposuction without Tummy Tuck?
Tummy Tuck and Liposuction
Jul 31, 2022
There are 3 primary contributors to the contour of the abdomen: skin, fat, and muscle. Each one needs to be evaluated by a plastic surgeon to determine the procedure that will result in the best outcome. In some cases liposuction alone will be indicated especially if the skin is not lax or redundant. However, should convexity of the rectus abdominis muscles be present, this will need to be addressed as fat removal alone will not result in a flat abdomen.

Fortunately, it can usually be successfully treated with hyaluronidase which is an enzyme that selectively dissolves the filler.
