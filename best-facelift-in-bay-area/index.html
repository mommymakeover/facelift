Best Facelift In Bay Area

Facelift in Bay Area allows to change the shape of the face, outline a beautiful contour, getting rid of wrinkles, sagging skin and other age-related changes.

The operation is performed in our clinic, facial rejuvenation is one of the most demanded procedures. We guarantee you a “fresh” and attractive look after the operation.

Indications for facelift

– Flabbiness of the skin
– Lowered corners of the eyes and lips, eyebrows
– “Crow’s feet” at the eyes
– Decrease in the volume of the cheekbones area
– Folds in the lower jaw area
– Wrinkles on the face and neck
– Nasolabial folds

Preparation for facelift

Preparation for facelift begins with consultation with a surgeon. The specialist evaluates the structure of the skin and the degree of age-related changes. Then the patient gives the necessary medical tests.

Before the procedure, it is recommended to rest more, to refrain from drinking alcohol and taking medicines without consulting a doctor. For 2 weeks before surgery, refrain from smoking. The operation is performed under general anesthesia, always on an empty stomach.

Contraindications to facelift

  • Diseases of the cardiovascular system
  • Diseases of some internal organs
  • Diabetes mellitus in decompensation stage
  • oncological diseases
  • Infectious diseases in the acute stage
  • Blood diseases

After facelift

After the procedure, the patient is recommended not to stay for a long time in the hospital (on average – a day). On the first day after surgery, a bandage is applied to the face, antibiotics and pain medications are prescribed.

Appears swelling, which significantly decreasing after 4 days. In the next two months, it is recommended to abandon the solarium, sauna and heavy physical exertion. Lymph drainage massage can be prescribed to accelerate the healing process. The first results can be seen after 2-3 weeks. The final results of the facelift surgery can be judged after 1.5-2 months. The duration of the effect is 5 to 10 years.
