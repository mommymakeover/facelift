Will a brow lift help vertical lines on forehead?
Forehead lines
Jun 10, 2018
The short answer is "yes": a browlift *could* help reduce both vertical and horizontal forehead lines. It depends on the technique that is used. You should discuss this in detail with your surgeon to see if he or she performs these types procedures. In addition, adjunct/post-surgery use of Botox injections can be used to further enhance the end result. The most important thing is to find an experienced surgeon who is comfortable with these treatments.

What is it about my eyes that makes them look so 'dead'?
Tired look
Mar 21, 2018
The "tired" look you describe is related to the downward slant of the outer corner of your eyes where the upper and lower lids come together - this is called the lateral canthus. Repositioning/tightening of this structure can be done surgically and could be a good options for your problem. I would recommend seeing an experienced oculoplastic surgeon for an in-person consultation in order to discuss these options. Good luck!
