Wrinkles, crow’s feet, sagging cheeks, day after day, leaves traces on our face. The first wrinkles in the corners of the eyes appear already at the age of 25, by the age of 35 we are already using anti-aging creams, and after 55 years the skin has been cut with deep wrinkles.

The face betrays your true age, but after all, we feel something and behave like a dozen years younger!

With age, the elasticity of the skin decreases and muscles weaken.

The skin and fatty layer fall down under the action of gravity.

Surface procedures, such as peeling or laser treatment, help in about half the cases that have not been cleared. Facelift is effective one hundred percent – especially when the tissues are sagging in the forehead, cheeks and neck.

So, if you are embarrassed by hanging eyebrows, deep wrinkles on the forehead, deepening of the infraorbital areas or nasolabial folds, “bags” under the eyes, a general “blurring” of the contour of the face, then you are the ideal candidate for facelift.

Each person is faced with these age-related manifestations in the 40-55 years of age. That’s when people often turn to plastic surgeons: the skin of the face and neck still retains the necessary elasticity, but the first bells of aging start to sound noticeable.

Facial plastic surgery in Indianapolis is the excision of excess skin with the subsequent return of soft tissue to its original position. In addition, facelift can strengthen the subcutaneous fat and muscle layers.

Most often, during facelift surgery, a general anesthesia is used with a duration of surgery of 2 to 6 hours.

During surgery, the surgeon removes excess skin and strengthens the facial muscles. Sutures or a surgical patch are applied to the incisions. Depending on the amount of intervention, the surgeon can supply a drainage tube that will prevent the formation of edema and bruising.

Methods of facelift surgery

The volume of necessary changes also affects the choice of facelift techniques, which are now developed a great variety. During the consultation or preliminary modeling, the surgeon will inquire about the characteristics of the patient’s body and take the final decision taking into account all the necessary indications.

Classical facelift

Facelift can only affect the face and neck. The traditional procedure is performed through an incision that runs along the hairline, around the ears and under the chin in the natural folds of the skin. In the future, thanks to this, the scars will be almost invisible.

Face Lift Mask Homemade

Such a facelift gives an effect for many years. If you are confused by heavy, hanging eyelids and bags under the eyes, then you can combine facelift with blepharoplasty.

SMAS facelift

This is a more complex and modern technology. With this method, the surgeon lifts and sutures the muscular aponeurotic layer (SMAS) in the new position, which is under the skin and subcutaneous tissue and serves as a framework for the soft tissues of the face. If you are about 45 years old and the skin of the face and neck is not much flabby yet, then this technique is for you.

Mini facelift or facelift without scars

This technique is more correctly called “small-scars” – an S-shaped incision performs in the field of the temple or in front of the ear, without entering the area behind the ear (as with traditional facelift).

Face Lift Mask

This method of suspenders refers to modifications of SMAS facelift in Indianapolis, but performed under local anesthesia. If you are between 30 and 40 years old and you want to slightly tighten the skin with minimal incisions, then select a surgeon who works in this technique. The mini facelift is also beneficial in that it provides excellent results, but it takes much less time to restore. A mini facelift is also much cheaper than a conventional facelift.

Deep facelift

If the age changes have left a significant mark in the area of the cheekbones and the middle part of the face, the cardinal method of correction will be deep facelift. Unlike the SMAS-technique, the surgeon corrects deeper layers of facial tissues in this way.

Also this technique is indicated for smokers: Plastic surgeons believe that such a face lift has less effect on microcirculation of tissues. This allows to minimize the risk of complications among smokers, who are most often disturbed by blood circulation. If in combination with the skin the fascia and the chewing muscle are fragmentarily corrected, then we can expect the preservation of the effect for 4-6 years.

Thread Facelift

The method developed by Russian specialists in 1999 is now widely used throughout the world. This is not surprising, since the technique does not require cuts and does not leave scars. The surgeon inserts under the skin from 4 to 12 special Aptos threads, which tighten the soft tissues of the face, which have lost their tone.

This is due to micro cuts applied on the thread, which, clinging to the subcutaneous soft tissues of the face, allow to return to a natural location. The result of this method is often not inferior to classical facelift, but it threatens that threads will be visible through the skin and relapse of omission.

Brow Lift & Forehead Lift Surgery

Facelift is often supplemented or even replaced by lifting the forehead and eyebrows. Forehead Lift allows not only to remove deep wrinkles on the forehead or nose, but also to correct hereditary signs: heavy eyelids and low eyebrows. According to the existing standard of beautiful balance of eyes and eyebrows, the distance between the center of the pupil and the eyebrow should be about 2-2.5 centimeters.

Lifting Face With Doctor Richard Zeph

If it is less, low eyebrows, even at a young age, give a stern and gloomy expression. There are 2 methods of eyebrow and forehead lift. With the classical facelift of the forehead, a continuous “coronary” incision is made in the scalp from ear to ear. With endoscopic facelift, 3-5 short incisions are made in the scalp through which the basic operation on deep tissues is performed under the control of the video camera, which makes it possible to smooth out the wrinkles of the forehead and raise the eyebrows.

Endoscopic facelift

The most common endoscopic facelift makes it possible to correct isolated defects in the tissues of the neck and face. The advantage of this technique is the absence of long incisions. The entire operation is carried out through 2-3 small incisions using a thin probe with a camera and a light bulb at the end.

Recovery After Face Lift Photo

This allows to apply facelift surgery to balding men, who find it difficult to hide scars with cosmetics or hair. Plus of the endoscopic facelift is an easier recovery period and fewer possible complications. But, there is also the reverse side of the coin – the effect lasts about half as much as after the traditional facelift and is about 5 years.

Recovery period after a facelift

On average, recovery after surgery takes about 2 weeks. During this time, you will need to sleep on your back and apply cold compresses to reduce swelling. Sunbathing, baths and saunas will be contraindicated for this period.

On the 7-10th day, seams are removed, until this point, it is advised to avoid any facial strain, for example, active chewing or laughing. So temporarily protect yourself from both negative and positive emotions.

Facelift: possible complications

Thanks to modern achievements of surgery complications after any plastic surgery are becoming increasingly rare. The main risks after facelift are circulatory disorders and microcirculation in the operated tissues.

If the doctor has the slightest suspicion of this complication, he immediately prescribes the necessary medications that will neutralize this risk. Numbness and a sense of tension, especially in the ears, are quite frequent companions of the recovery period after a face lift. They are not afraid of them, because this is a temporary phenomenon.
