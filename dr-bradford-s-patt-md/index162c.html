Statement: A leading physician in Houston, Texas, Dr. Bradford Patt is a board-certified facial plastic surgeon specializing in facial cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries. For 11 consecutive years, Dr. Patt has been listed as one of the “Top Docs®” and “Super Doctors®” in the Houston, Texas area.

With over 28 years in practice, Dr. Patt has become especially well-known for his exemplary results from rhinoplasty, facelifts, and eyelid lifts, as well as nonsurgical facial rejuvenation procedures such as Botox, dermal fillers, Ultherapy skin tightening, and nonsurgical rhinoplasty. How long does it take for crown hair to grow after hair transplant?
Hair growth after grafting
Jan 31, 2019
Hair grafting with Follicular unit extraction is a very reliable procedure and has excellent results. The time period for hair growth is variable depending on your age amount of grafts and follow up treatment(ie biotin, Platelet rich plasma, laser cap) you are completing. Most patients should start seeing results within 6 months with continued growth up to a year.  The crown area is probably the most reliable and stable areas after hair grafting.
