What is the minimum period before I can see 100% results of a FUE transplant? Can it vary based upon transplant region?
Jan 29, 2018
Growth is usually complete in a year, but there is quite a bit of variability with growth up to 18 months not unusual. Growth is also slower in subsequent procedures and in older patients.
Can I smoke after 15 days of hair transplant?
Can I smoke after 15 days of hair transplant?
Jan 29, 2018
Smoking can constrict the small blood vessels that enter the scalp and impede growth. Smoking after a hair transplant is not a good idea.
11 months post FUE, hair is frizzy and hard to manage. Why?
11 months post FUE, hair is frizzy and hard to manage. Why?
Jan 10, 2018
Usually, it takes 12-18 months for the hair to regain its full previous luster/appearance following a hair transplant. If the follicles were damaged or the sebaceous glands removed in the harvesting/dissection, some of the characteristics of the hair may not completely return, but this is uncommon.
Could rubbing grafts 17 days post-FUE cause any damage?
Could rubbing grafts 17 days post-FUE cause any damage? (Photo)
Jan 10, 2018
At 17 days following your procedure, the grafts are permanently in place and will not be dislodged. Rubbing your scabs gently to allow them to come off at this point is fine and should not damage them.
