I want to know if my scars are treatable?
Scar Treatments
Feb 16, 2018
Scars are a deep skin injury. While you will never completely resolve the scar, you can make it much less prominent. A series of laser treatments is probably the best option after 18 years. By creating micro-injury, new cell growth can improve the appearance of the scar. I am also a fan of a product called BioCorneum. It is a silicone gel that remodels the scar over time. Laser and silicone gel are complimentary! Good luck.
