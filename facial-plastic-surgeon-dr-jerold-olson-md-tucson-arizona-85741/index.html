Does the scalp reattach to the skull after hairline lowering procedure?
Scalp healing
Aug 6, 2022
Hello,
The scalp is lifted in between 2 structures, the periosteum is the layer just over the bone and the galea. After the forehead advancement these 2 areas heal down to one another again. There is some numbness associated with this procedure but once this improves the scalp feels completely normal to patients.
How Should You Sleep and Take Care of Your Skin After a Facelift?
Best Sleeping Position & Best Skin Care Post Facelift
Mar 3, 2021
For the first 2 nights it is recommended you sleep with your head elevated to minimize swelling. You may use a travel pillow to support your neck or a soft towel. This will help decrease the amount of post-operative swelling while healing. You may shower approximately 48 hours after a facelift but it should be kept brief. Using a gentle face wash once a day will keep your skin clean until you are able to resume your skin care regimen.
