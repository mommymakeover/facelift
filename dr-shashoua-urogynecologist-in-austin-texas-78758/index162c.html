Can I have ThermiVa done while pregnant? I'm 6 weeks pregnant.
Can I have ThermiVa done while pregnant? I'm 6 weeks pregnant.
May 28, 2021
Thank you for sharing. ThermiVa is contraindicated in patients who are currently pregnant. We recommend waiting until after delivery for treatment. This would likely give you the most benefit as well if you are planning a vaginal delivery which can further relax the tissues. We do recommend that if you do decide to proceed with treatments scheduling a series of three about 6 weeks apart and from there you can typically maintain with one or two a year.

Can ThermiVa safely be done while using Clobetasol?

Thank you for sharing. It depends on what/where you are using the clobetasol treatment.
