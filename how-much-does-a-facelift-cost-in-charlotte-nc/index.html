Unfortunately, time runs with inexorable speed, and age makes itself felt, drawing on the face the first wrinkles.

Age is not the only reason! External factors such as stress, constant lack of sleep, improper facial skin care, improper diet and bad habits, also harm the face skin and promote wrinkles, muscle weakness and sagging of the skin.

Aging soft tissues and weakening the muscles is inevitable, but modern medicine knows how to maintain beauty and youth.

An excellent way to remove the shortcomings today is a facelift in Charlotte, with its help the facial contours are restored, the cheekbones acquire the former volume.

Rhytidectomy

One of the oldest methods of facial rejuvenation is to make incisions along the edge of hair growth on the forehead and temples, then along the edge of the auricle with the transition to the behind-the-ear area. The skin is lifted while the excess fatty tissue can be removed then it is stretched and sutured.

Simultaneously with this operation, neck lift and, as a rule, lower blepharoplasty can be performed. The effectiveness of such an operation usually does not exceed 5 years, because the skin is easily stretched.

SMAS facelift (deep facelift)

Deep facelift or SMAS facelift (a superficial musculo-aponeurotic system), during this operation not only the skin is pulled as with a classical facelift, but also the deep structures of the musculo-neural system (SMAS).

The main advantage of this operation is not only in rejuvenation but also the preservation of the individuality of the face without becoming a mask. The effect of such operations is more pronounced and protracted than with a conventional facelift.

The implementation of this operation is associated with greater risks, since directly under the SMAS are located salivary ducts and branches of the facial nerve, the damage of which can lead to irreversible consequences, which requires the surgeon to make jewelry accurate in performing this operation. The recovery period after such facelift is the longest up to 4 weeks.

Endoscopic facelift

This operation refers to a less traumatic and high-tech types of operations. Requires the presence in the clinic of special endoscopic equipment and the knowledge of the surgeon’s methods of working on it, and by the methods of endoscopic facelift. With the help of this equipment, it is possible to perform isolated operations such as forehead lift, midface lift. Advantages are that the incisions do not exceed 2-2.5 cm and are in the small areas (usually in the scalp). The rehabilitation period is 1-2 weeks.

Facelift with the use of endotins

One of the latest developments in the field of facial rejuvenation (facelift) involves the use of such tissue retainers, as endotins:

  • Endotine is an absorbing material used to hook tissues in place onto bone.
  • They were very fashionable about 8 years ago.
  • They absorb slowly and can form lumps.

They are used with endoscopic surgery to secure tissue to bone but they offer little benefit for soft tissue surgery. These operations are related to low-traumatic and highly effective methods of rejuvenation (facelift), the recovery period is about 10 days.
