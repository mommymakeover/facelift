How much fat is lost after injected and how long does it take to go away?
Fat Transfer
Nov 25, 2013
Fat transfer is a great way to add volume. How much fat survives after injection depends on the way it is harvested, how it is prepared, and how it is injected. It does best when removed in a gentle fashion, prepared in a similar fashion, and injected in small aliquots, into small tunnels. This technique gives the body a chance to grow blood vessels into the fat to keep it alive. Typically, one can expect anywhere from 40 to 60% of the fat to “take”. The rest is absorbed by the body.
