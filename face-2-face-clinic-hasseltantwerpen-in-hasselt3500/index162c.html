Olivier Beckers, MD

Statement: Olivier Beckers is a Facial Plastic surgeon, specialist in Cranio-Maxillo-Facial surgery – Head and Neck Surgery ” at “Ziekenhuis Oost-Limburg campus St-Jan (Genk, Belgium)” and in his private practice in Hasselt.
