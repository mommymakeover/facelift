Can I use Emsculpt if I have a bladder sling?
EMSculpt with bladder sling
Apr 18, 2019
Absolutely.
Both EMSculpt and Emsella are safe with with a bladder sling in place.
I have used EMSculpt on many women with bladder slings.
There is no metal in a bladder sling.
We are getting great results with EMSculpt, by the way.

SculpSure and bruising
Feb 23, 2019
The lumps are collections of fat that should resolve over time- in fact in my experience they always do. It takes the body some time to break down the long chain fatty acids that are released from the ruptured fat cells.

Is Emsculpt safe with Skyla IUD?
EMSculpt with a Skyla IUD
Jan 30, 2019
The Skyla IUD is not metal, therefore EMSculpt should be safe. The Paragard IUD does contain copper and would not be safe.
