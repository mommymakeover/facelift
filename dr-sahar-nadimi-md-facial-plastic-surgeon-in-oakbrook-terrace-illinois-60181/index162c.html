Are doctors supposed to minimize fat removal during an upper bleph?
fat removal during blepharoplasty
Mar 27, 2019
The amount of fat, skin, and muscle removal varies from patient to patient, depending on their anatomy. In general, it is important to avoid a hollow appearance which may result from removing too much fat and/or muscle. When removing fat, using a cautery is nice to minimize the bleeding. I recommend a proper evaluation with a board certified facial plastic or oculoplastic surgeon. Good luck!

I have sinusitis. Post nasal drip. Take Flucticason daily. Headache, burning sensation. large nostrils
rhinoplasty and sinus surgery
Mar 27, 2019
A septorhinoplasty can improve breathing if there is a structural issue (i.e. deviated septum, weak nasal sidewalls, etc) and improve cosmetic appearance, but may not help with sinusitis. I recommend a thorough evaluation of your sinuses by an ENT; sinusitis can occasionally be treated with medications alone but may also require sinus surgery. Some ENT surgeons are also fellowship-trained in facial plastic surgery and can perform the rhinoplasty as well. Best of luck!

I recommend a consultation with a board certified facial plastic surgeon or oculoplastic surgeon. 
Could weight lifting prevent grafts to grow better?

Mar 27, 2019
Weight lifting will not effect your graft yield. If you had high-quality surgery then your grafts will grow. Grafts usually begin to grow at around 3-6 months with full results by 1 year postop. Best of luck!
