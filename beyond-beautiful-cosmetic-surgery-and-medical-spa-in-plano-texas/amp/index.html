Doctors

Vu Ho, MD

Name: Vu Ho, MD
Statement: Dr. Vu Ho is a board-certified plastic surgeon based in Plano, Texas. The founder of Beyond Beautiful Plastic Surgery and Medical Spa, Dr. Ho is a leader in cosmetic surgical procedures in the North Texas area, offering a range of services that includes neck lifts, facelifts, cheek and chin enhancement, and more.

Dr. Ho received his medical degree at the University of Minnesota Medical School, then completed a General Surgery internship at Hennepin County Medical Center in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This was followed by a residency in Otolaryngology and the University of Minnesota and a fellowship in Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the University of Michigan.

He operates out of a state-of-the-art, on-site surgical center and is an expert in local anaesthesia. This allows patients the option to have many procedures done on site, which can mean a significant cost savings over surgery in a hospital under general anaesthesia. In addition to surgical treatments, he also offers a variety of non-invasive options, including laser resurfacing and skin tightening, microneedling, and injectables.

Dr. Ho is certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology Head and Neck Surgery, as well as a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery. He’s the winner of numerous awards, including a 2019 RealSelf 100 and 500 Hall of Fame Inductee, Addison Magazine’s 2013 and 2014 Reader’s Choice Award for Favorite Cosmetic Surgeon, and Living Magazine’s Best of Readers' Choice Award, Best Plastic Surgeon for 2013-2019.

Dr. Ho is nationally recognized as a physician trainer for Galderma Aesthetics and The American Institute of Aesthetic Medicine.
