What can be done about my face that is sagging?
Facelift and Bell’s Palsy
Mar 16, 2018
You would benefit from a face and neck lift and brow lift.  The first facelift I performed 20 years ago was on a patient similar to you.  It is unlikely that you will achieve perfect symmetry, however, by differentially lifting the right vs the left side, a significant improvement in balance could be achieved.
5 days post-op of Facelift, my right eye won’t blink and right upper lip is lazy/immobile. Is this
Facelift and Eyelid
Jun 10, 2014
I would agree that this is not what would be considered “normal” 5 days after facelift surgery. I would strongly recommend a visit with your surgeon to discuss your concerns and observations. Damage to a nerve during a facelift is usually, but not always, temporary. If your eye is not closing and remains exposed while you are asleep, you need to ensure that it is being protected and not being allowed to dry out.
What is the worst part of Rhinoplasty/ Septoplasty regarding pain?
Post op rhinoplasty pain
Jun 10, 2014
It is unusual to have any significant discomfort following rhinoplasty surgery. Make sure to follow all of your surgeons instructions carefully both before and after the surgery.
Juvederm Voluma vs Sculptra
Volume Restoration
Mar 19, 2014
What your physician said makes no sense. Juvederm Ultra, Ultra Plus, Voluma and Volbella are made of naturally occuring hyaluronic acid that is then cross-linked to provide stability and longevity. They are safe, soft, natural and, if necessary, reversible. As mentioned, Sculptra has its uses, however, in the vast majority of cases where volume fillers are indicated, a hyaluronic acid based filler would be the product of choice.
Do you think my nasal asymmetry will even out over time?
Early Post Rhinoplasty Assymetry
Mar 18, 2014
Nostril assymetry is common this early after surgery. Be patient but also be sure to discuss your concerns with your surgeon during your next follow up appointment.
When can I smile, laugh, eat solid foods, and wash my face after alar base reduction?
Alar base reduction
Mar 18, 2014
Although you should check with your surgeon, I instruct my patients to limit facial expressions that put tension on the base of the nose for 10 days after surgery – this includes wide smiling and laughter. Keep the area dry and apply a small amount of the recommended antibiotic ointment twice daily for 1 week. Solid foods are okay immediately but perhaps best not to have anything that requires alot of chewing for the first week or so.
Can I Wear Contacts After Rhino?
Rhinoplasty and Contact Lenses
Sep 30, 2013
Contact lenses are fine to wear immediately after your rhinoplasty. You are correct about glasses. I request that my patients not wear glasses for 6 weeks following their surgery.
