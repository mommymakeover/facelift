Statement: Dr. Daniela Vaca Grisales is a plastic, reconstructive and aesthetic surgeon. She provides a very special care to her patients since the first moment they come to her office, supporting them in their transformation process and during the journey afterwards to maintain their beauty and health.

The doctor graduated from the Universidad de los Andes in Colombia and completed her plastic surgery training at the Fundación Universitaria de Ciencias de la Salud (University Foundation of Health Sciences). In addition, she has a sub-specialization in Breast Surgery and Microsurgery from the University of Toronto in Canada.

Furthermore, she completed the full course of Functional Medicine, obtaining a certificate from the Universidad de los Andes. Thanks to this, she provides her patients with a comprehensive management to maximize the results in their aesthetic and health changes.

Specialties

Dr. Daniela Vaca Grisales is an expert in body contouring (liposuction) which she complements with a special diet plan to maintain the obtained results. She performs breast augmentation on women seeking a better cleavage and improvement of their self-esteem.

Helping moms in the recovery of their figure after pregnancy, both at the abdominal and breast level, is one of her greatest professional interests. Likewise, she performs breast surgeries in patients with ASIA syndrome or with the desire to remove their implants.

She provides non-surgical procedures to revitalize and improve skin imperfections such as: Botox, hyaluronic acid (lips, dark circles, nose), Radiesse, platelet-rich plasma, nanopore and Fixer. As for facial rejuvenation she performs procedures in the areas of eyelids, forehead and cheeks. She also offers nonsurgical nose job with hyaluronic acid, which as she herself says, focuses on “giving her patients the nose they have always wanted without the need for surgery”. The doctor performs surgical rhinoplasty as well, which allows her fix imperfections of the nose and have the beautiful face they want.

Location

Her office is located in the Usaquén district of Bogotá, an area known for its modern and cosmopolitan atmosphere. There you will find a friendly team that cares about your well-being.
