How long will it take for me to get my facial expressions back?
Facelift recovery
Jul 27, 2015
It is not unusual to have some residual numbness as well as swelling that alters the movement of your face 2 weeks after this type of procedure. Icing can be continued, and I generally recommend head elevation, gentle massage, a healthy diet, and treatments like manual lymphatic drainage (MLD) and ultrasound to speed up the healing process. Make sure to follow your surgeon's postoperative instructions, and follow up with your surgeon to make sure you are healing properly. ...
