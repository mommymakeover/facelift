How much time is usually enough for liposuction recovery?
recovery is a matter of a few days
Mar 11, 2020
Recovery from liposuction is pretty quick. Most patients take Advil for a few days. I have my patients up and walking that day. They can return to work or school in one or two days. I recommend compression garments for 3-4 weeks
Liposuction swelling - how long until it goes down?
you are still early in the recovery
Mar 11, 2020
I tell my patients that 60% of the swelling is gone in 3 weeks,but the other 40% may take 3 months. I have my patients wear compression garments for 4 weeks. You may have some areas that are lumpy,from fibrosis(thickening of tissue due to the trauma from liposuction). That will resolve. If there are some residual fat pockets,your plastic surgeon can revise them,usually with a syringe or a chemical such as lipodissolve or Aqualyx

Feb 25, 2020

What is the specific purpose of wearing the compression garment after Facetite?
compression helps limit fluid accumulation in the layer beneath the skin
Feb 25, 2020
When treating with Fcaetite,the procedure is done in the subcutaneous layer,which is the fat layer beneath the skin and on top of the muscle layer.The radiofrequency heat melts fat and tightens skin.This causes mild fluid accumulation and compression limits that.It speeds up the recovery time. I have my patients wear compression for 3 days
