Sutures are used to secure the newly positioned upper lateral cartilages to the spreader graft and septum so it won't move out of place.

Is there a limit to how much you can narrow the bridge in an osteotomy?

Nov 16, 2020

We don't normally remove any nasal bridge skin, but plan on having it shrink wrap over the new underlying bone position.
