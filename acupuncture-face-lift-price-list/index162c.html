What is acupuncture facelift

Acupuncture theory is based on the belief that the human body has 20 channels of vital energy flow. There are numerous branches to these 20 main channels. If there are any disruptions to these channels, the acupuncture theory believe that it will lead to illness and other health issues.

How does acupuncture facelift work

Each session of acupuncture face lift takes around one hour and involved 60 needles being poke into your face. While this is not painful, it will definitely lead to a feeling of uneasiness as the face is the most sensitive part of the skin. Some doctors might apply a mild anesthesia to improve the comfort level.

Is acupuncture facelift safe?

For this treatment, there is no cutting for any sort, thus reducing any risk of scarring as well as operational complications. That being said, you should make sure that the doctor has sufficient experience and expertise by asking for any before and after photos of his or her ex patients.
