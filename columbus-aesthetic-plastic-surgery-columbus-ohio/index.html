Can I get CoolSculpting if I have had a shingles before?
Shingles after CoolSculpting
Aug 2, 2020
We have been doing CoolSculpting over 6 years and we have not had this issue nor do I think it will happen again. However you can always do a round of antivirals beforehand just to be sure.
