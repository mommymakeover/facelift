How do you remove scar tissue from liposuction in the abdomen?
Lipo scar tissue
Apr 6, 2017
Scar tissue from liposuction typically represents adherence of the skin down to the deeper layers of the abdomen from the prior liposuction. Sometimes this can result from too much fat being removed. One option is to break up that scar tissue with cannulas under no suction, followed by injection of a small amount of fat just under the skin to give you that natural/unscarred look to the tissue.
Gummy Bear Breast Implants vs Saline or Silicone?
Implant differences
Feb 22, 2017
The "gummy bear" term should be applied to all silicone implants available in the USA after 2006. The gel is like a gummy bear with much less tendency to leak. Saline implants aren't bad devices, just relatively older technology.

Aug 21, 2015
