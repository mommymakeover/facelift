What is a suitable treatment suggestion for my acne scars?
acne scar treatment
Oct 28, 2019
Normally for these kind of acne scars, it is advised to actually undergo a combination of treatments including microneedling, subcision, lasers, TCA cross, PRP and fillers. I usually like to combine these procedures according to the type of acne scars a person has.  I would advise you to consult a certified dermatologist to have an in person consultation to stop your existing acne issue and then work up with combination treatment.

Does 0.5 mm Derma Roller work on this chicken pox scar?
chicken pox scars
Oct 3, 2019
I don’t recommend using 0.5 mm for scar treatment. chicken pox scars are more resistant to the usual treatment and they would require subcision along with PRP  & microneedling with more depth (say 1.5-2mm). also, in some resistant, cases excision may be required.I would advise you to consult a dermatologist to know your best optionsAll the best 
Dr Jyoti Gupta
