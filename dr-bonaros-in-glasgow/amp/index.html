If I am prone to male pattern baldness, will hair loss on Accutane be more probable?
Oct 21, 2022
Isotretinoin does not cause hair loss very often. In a meta-analysis published in 2022, hair loss was observed in 3–6% of patients taking low doses of isotretinoin. The more Accutane is taken and the longer it is taken, the more hair loss may occur.Hair thinning or loss can be caused by Accutane (isotretinoin) due to its effect on the hair cycle, the increase in vitamin A, as well as the suppression of sebum production.
Does metformin treatment effect dihydrotestosterone (DHT) and the (male pattern) hair loss?
Does metformin cause hair loss?
Oct 21, 2022
Metformin does not cause hair loss, according to significant scientific research. Taking metformin for a long time may cause hair loss indirectly due to anaemia, according to a study conducted in 2016.Metformin treats diabetes type 2 and PCOS, both known causes of hair loss. In addition, it appears to lower testosterone, thus preventing male pattern baldness.
