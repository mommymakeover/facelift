Is my end goal achievable for a thick skinned, ethnic nose?
Rhinoplasty and thick skin outcomes
Sep 21, 2022
The important thing to keep in mind is that when performing rhinoplasty on thick-skinned patients, the nose cannot be overly shortened or reduced- otherwise it will loose shape and definition. I typically recommend maintaining tip projection in these patients to keep the skin well-fitted around the underlying nasal structures. I think the edited photo you made is a reasonable goal given that you did not overly deproject your nose.

Is loss of smell common post Rhinoplasty?
Anosmia and rhinoplasty
Sep 16, 2022
Any nasal surgery has the chance to impact olfaction (smell) postoperatively. Most often this is related to swelling and should resolve over a month. It is unlikely for smell to be permanently lost after rhinoplasty unless other procedures were performed with it such as endoscopic sinus surgery.
Any preparation tips to get best results from Rhinoplasty for a very oily nose?
Sebaceous skin and rhinoplasty
Sep 16, 2022
This skin type can be the most challenging for surgeons and patients. I would discuss with your surgeon and aesthetician about an appropriate regimen. Some surgeons recommend accutane postoperatively to help with the healing process as this can shrink the sebaceous glands and assist the skin in contracting over the newly designed nasal shape.
Is it possible to achieve this rhinoplasty result while maintaining better breathing?
Rhinoplasty and breathing
Sep 16, 2022
With any nasal surgery there is a risk of compromising breathing, especially when one is making the nose smaller. Fortunately, if done carefully, breathing can be maintained if not improved during the surgery. A thorough examination is necessary to see which procedures would be be performed with rhinoplasty to improve breathing.
Is my radix too high?
Radix height
Sep 15, 2022
Radix height is best evaluated from the profile view; not the frontal view. In men, the radix ideally sits at or above the lid crease. 5 1/2 weeks is quite early to judge the result. Whenever the radix is altered it tends to hold swelling longer.
