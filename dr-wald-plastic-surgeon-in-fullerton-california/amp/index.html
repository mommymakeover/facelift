Dog ear revision

Tummy tuck vs no tummy tuck when getting lipo?
Tummy Tuck
Nov 6, 2023
Most of the time, we do liposuction in conjunction with a tummy tuck. Liposuction can also be done without a tummy tuck if there is good skin tone and not a lot of loose skin. The surgeon will help you make the decision which option is best for you.
Pre op cold meds recommendations?
Cold
Nov 6, 2023
I would call your surgeon immediately and let them know. It sounds viral so over the counter cold remedies will help with the symptoms. They may prescribe a z pack as well. If you are sick, they will cancel the surgery.

Is it important to have a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon?
Board certification
Nov 6, 2023
As a board certified plastic surgeon, I would most definitley recommend you see one for your facelift. These individuals are the most qualified and will give you the best results. Go to the person who has at least 15 years of experience and your most comfortable with.
Are surgically implanted breast implant shells labeled?
Breast implants
Oct 23, 2023
Depending on the manufacturer, there is labeling for the company and the volume on the device. The older implants did not add the labeling. Saline implants also do not always label their devices.
How many cc's do you recommend?
Breast Implants
Oct 23, 2023
I am sorry I can not give you a very accurate answer since I don't have a picture of your entire chest. I also need to know your height and weight and your desires of size. A breast lift also complicates the procedure and implant size will be affected. A ballpark estimate would be a 300 to 400cc moderate profile implant. Please see your surgeon and go over your options.
If I had a breast reduction with 400g removed & want old breast size back, can I get 400cc impl
Breast Implants
Oct 23, 2023
Yes, you can place 400 to 450cc implants to replace the 400gm removal. These implants will definitely replace your lost volume. CC's and Grams are not exact but are close. The surgeon will be able to help you with implant size.
Leaking silicone breast implant, how fast does it need to come out?
Breast Implant removal
Oct 23, 2023
I would highly recommend that you move up your surgery. You don't want to keep this fluid for several months. There is a chance of infection and the fluid potentially could cause bleeding. There is also a rare potential for a lymphoma with the textured implants.
Dropping +1 years post op?
Breast Implants
Oct 18, 2023
Yes, your implants will drop even more over time but not significantly. The position of the implants appear to be in the correct position. At this time, you can redo the breasts with smaller implant and possibly a different dimension. I would recommend you see your surgeon and go over your options.
Can I get my implants removed if I have BRCA2?
BRCA 2
Oct 18, 2023
Yes, you can have your implants removed. However, you need to see an oncologist to discuss your genetics and determine if your breasts need to be removed as well. As far as a nipple sparing vs a complete mastectomy, this needs to be discussed with a general surgeon. The key now is your safety and the risks of breast cancer now and in the future.
Will Infini RF or Fraxel be helpful for my scarring?
Infini vs Laser
Oct 10, 2023
With acne scarring, the infini works better in my opinion. It's important to understand there will be only a 50% improvement in your appearance and usually 3 treatments are necessary for the best results.
5'4 125 lbs 34AA - how can I choose between 475cc and 525cc?
Breast Implants
Oct 10, 2023
You can use either implant. The difference between the 2 devices is approximately 1/2 cup size. You are quite petite and dimensionally I think you would be better with the 475cc device. You will get cleavage with the 500cc moderate plus implant. You will bulge more to the sides with the larger implant.
I'm looking to go as large as I can, can I get implants without a lift?
Breast Implants
Oct 10, 2023
You can definitely go large since your chest dimensions and volume of your breasts will accommodate a large implant. You can easily use a 500 to 600cc implant. Your breast are droopy to begin with and if you add an implant, this will contribute to more droopiness in the future. You may be required to get a breast lift in the future.
350cc high profile or 325cc moderate profile for natural look?
Breast Implants
Oct 10, 2023
The volume of the 2 sizes your surgeon recommended is essentially the same. The difference is in the dimensions. If your chest is narrow, the high dimension fits better. The moderate profile does not project as much and some women prefer this look. I think you can choose either implant, it really depends on what shape and projection you desire.
