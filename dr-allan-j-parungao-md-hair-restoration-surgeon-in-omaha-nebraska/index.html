I had a 1500 graft FUE done yesterday and I’m kind of freaking out right now about density and a
Density issues
Jul 11, 2019
It is likely that you were informed that density that can be achieved surgically is not the same as your natural density.  There is always a discrepancy.  The key is to stay on hair saving techniques (like finasteride, Rogaine, PRP, laser therapy, etc) which will enhance your surgical results. Go back to your surgeon and discuss all of your options.  Best of luck to you.

Overharvested?
Jul 11, 2019
Maybe. You need to give it time to heal/recover.  As you know, there is certainly a risk of being over-harvested with this procedure, but you will have a better idea of where you stand around month 6.  In the meantime, explore different hairstyles to enhance what you have .  Best of luck to you.
Is the hairline too conservative?
What exactly makes you look younger?
Jul 11, 2019
Oftentimes, a more youthful appearance is not achieved by lowering the hairline. This can be done by making the current hairline fuller at its current position. Many surgeons use the current hairline to construct the new one. Keep in mind, you want a hairline that will age gracefully. This and many more factors are taken into account when the plan is made. If needed, get a second opinion, even a 3rd one if that makes you feel more confident in your choice. Best of luck to you.
