Trang Vo-Nguyen, MD

Name: Trang Vo-Nguyen, MD
Statement: Dr. Trang Thuy Vo-Nguyen, M.D., known to her patients as “Dr. V” is a double board certified facial plastic surgeon and founder and Medical Director of The Virginia Institute for Surgical Arts located in Chantilly, Virginia. She is board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology/Head and Neck Surgery.

Due to her vast experience with Rhinoplasty, Blepharoplasty and Facelift procedures, Dr V. is often sought by colleagues and patients for some of the most complex cosmetic cases in the DC metro area.

Dr. V is considered a premier Injector in Washington D.C for her experience with hyaluronic acid fillers, like Restylane and Juvederm, as well her exceptional reputation as an industry leader for Sculptra, an injectable collagen stimulator.

Dr. V has received many honors, including Best of DC Washington’s Best of City Winner for 2022: Best Facial Plastic Surgeon and is among a select group of board certified facial plastic surgeons that have attained the highest level of achievement in cosmetic surgical training, continued education, and clinical experience.

Dr. V graduated summa cum laude in chemistry and obtained her medical degree from the University of Minnesota in the Twin Cities. She continued her training in Internal Medicine at the University of California and returned to the University of Minnesota for her residency training in Otolaryngology (Head and Neck Surgery). Dr. V was then selected for a Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery Fellowship at Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center, Shreveport, LA.
