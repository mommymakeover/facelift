My earring split my earlobe. What can I do?
Torn earlobe
Dec 7, 2015
Hi, You have a very common problem - once the earlobe have healed from the tear, it is very simple to trim the scarred edges, and repair the earlobe with fine sutures. The best results will be obtained by a surgeon who is careful to align the edges perfectly, and evert the edges, to obtain a fine, imperceptible scar. Once you have healed from this, you can re-pierce the earlobe in a different location, i.e. not near the scar - otherwise it will tear through again.
