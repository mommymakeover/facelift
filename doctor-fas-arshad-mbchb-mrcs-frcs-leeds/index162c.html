I had my hair transplant 6month ago with 4350 grafts. Will I get more coverage or will
Waiting game until 12 to 18 months
Dec 26, 2019
Following a hair transplant growth in the recipient site can occur up to 12 to 18 months post procedure. However in my experience the growth after 8/9 months is little and the majority of the result can be seen. In you case I suggest waiting for another 3 months or so before jumping to any conclusions. All the best.

Pictures taken at various stages post procedure look different.
Dec 26, 2019
Immediately after the surgery it is usual to see some skin (epithelium) surrounding the hair/s that are implanted. After a while this crusts over and subsequently falls off to leave the hair. Depending on when the picture was taken post procedure the look of the implanted hair grafts may vary. This does not mean the surgery was done incorrectly. Is it harmful to use oil after 1 months and 1 week after hair transplant?
Oil is good...
Dec 26, 2019
Given the stage your at after hair transplant i.e. 6 weeks there is no issue with using oil. Both organ and coconut oil are fine to use. In fact a lot of my clients at this stage prefer to use oil in order to reduce the itch and dryness that may occur. It helps sooth the scalp. All the best.
