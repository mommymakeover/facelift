Statement: Dr. Mark Scheinberg is a board-certified cosmetic gynecologist practicing in Deerfield Beach, Florida. The founder of The Center for Cosmetic and Reconstructive Gynecology, Dr. Scheinberg specializes in female genital aesthetic surgery, including labiaplasty and vaginoplasty. He developed the natural edge labiaplasty technique and lectures to surgeons around the world on CPG techniques.

Dr. Scheinberg works with patients internationally, throughout the country, and especially in South Florida (Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, Tampa, and West Palm Beach) who are looking for a highly skilled surgeon who combines a warm, empathetic bedside manner with the kind of expertise that only comes from having advanced training and performing thousands of genital surgeries.

Dr. Scheinberg learned advanced techniques in vaginal reconstruction and transformation from the well-known gynecologic surgeon Dr. Adam Ostrzenski.

Throughout his career, Dr. Scheinberg has kept up with the latest advancements in cosmetic and reconstructive gynecology. He is part of the international research team that discovered the G-spot and is a clinical scientific reviewer for several peer review scientific publications.

After completing undergraduate studies at Syracuse University, Dr. Scheinberg earned his medical degree from the State University of New York at Buffalo. He later completed an internship and residency in anesthesia at Charity Hospital New Orleans, Louisiana. He then completed a residency in obstetrics and gynecology at the George Washington University Medical Center in Washington, D.C.

Board certified by the American Board of Obstetrics & Gynecology, Dr. Scheinberg is a Fellow of the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists and a Diplomat of the American Board of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

Dr. Scheinberg also offers nonsurgical vaginal rejuvenation treatments like ThermiVa, as well as bio-identical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT).
