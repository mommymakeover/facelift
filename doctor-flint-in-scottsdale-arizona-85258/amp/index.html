Can a new mom get implants?
Recovery following breast augment
Nov 26, 2013
Lifting following breast augmentation should be avoided for two to three weeks in most cases. It can be difficult to do that with a newborn. Also, the breast can change fairly significantly in size for a few months following breast feeding. You may be better off waiting a bit.
If Facelift Results Are the Same Why Would I Invest More Recovery Time and Money?
Type of facelift
Nov 20, 2013
The different facelift procedures suit different patients and different surgeons differently. Sometimes the surgeon is convinced that one technique provides better results than others. In some instances, patients are better candidates or would benefit from one procedure more than another. This can be dependent upon their goals and their exam. Without an examination, it is impossible to determine which technique would benefit you the most.
One or two procedures?
Anesthesia
Nov 20, 2013
If you are healthy and a board certified anesthesiologist will be in attendance at your procedure, doing these procedures at one time is safe. Length of surgery in the 4-6 hour range is not considered risky.
Would a breast lift be recommended?
Lift with implants
Nov 20, 2013
To correct deflation and descent, a lift with implants is your best option. The type of lift depends on many factors that can only be determined during and in person exam.
Do I need a breast lift?
To lift or not to lift
Nov 20, 2013
The answer to this question depends very much on your goals for the aesthetic outcome. Your breasts are low set, more than they are droopy. Discuss this with any consultant you see and then decide which procedure will accomplish your goals.
I need a breast lift, but I’m petrified of scars. What can I do?
Scars after breast lift
Nov 20, 2013
There are many different types of breast lifts, and they come with different scar patterns. Common terms include donut, lollipop and anchor. Your surgeon should determine which breast lift scar pattern would provide the result you desire. If you prefer a different scar pattern, there will be shape and lift trade-offs that you should be made aware of. Scar after breast lift
Nov 20, 2013

How many cc’s should I do?
Weight loss and breast surgery
Nov 20, 2013
A combination of a breast lift and implants may be in your best interest. Without a photo, it is hard to determine implant size or type of lift you would do best with.
Silicone moderate plus implants smooth under muscle for athlete?
Dimensional approach to implant sizing
Nov 20, 2013
The width of your chest wall and quality of your tissues is the best guide to appropriate implant size. In athletes with low body fat, I would avoid implants on top of the muscle, as visibility and palpability of the implants can be a concern.

How do you fix a significantly lowered inframmamary fold during explantation?
Inframammary folds
Nov 19, 2013
Folds can be adjusted up or down at the time of augmentatin or implant removal. If the capsules are removed, the fold usually re-establishes itself, but a few internal sutures can be placed to stabilize it at the desired level.
How many cc’s would you say these are?
Implant size
Nov 19, 2013
It is not possible to estimate the size, but when patients change from over the muscle to under the muscle, they often feel less projected.
How do I find out if my implants are underfilled?

Nov 19, 2013
This is a very common problem with saline. If your tissues are thin, it is even more common. Changing to silicone with possible use of Strattice or Alloderm for better coverage are reasonable options.
When Does Swelling After Breast Augmentation Begin to Subside?
Swelling after breast augmentation
Nov 18, 2013
Most of the swelling will be resolved by three weeks, but do not buy expensive new bras for three to four months as there will be subtle softening and shape changes during this time that will makes some bras fit better than others.
Which Breast Implant Placement Gives a More Natural Appearance?
Implant position
Nov 18, 2013
Surgeons tend to differ in their opinions on this issue. I find imlants under the muscle look and feel more natural. More importantly, implants under the muscle are much less likely to interfere with mammograms.
