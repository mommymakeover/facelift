How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Cincinnati

Mankind has been concerned for a long time with the question of the return of the youth, and to this day scientists are in search of the treasured “The Holy Grail”.

In surgery, this question is offered a fairly pragmatic answer. Operations to rejuvenate of the face began to be carried out as early as the beginning of the twentieth century.

During this time they underwent certain metamorphosis, but today we can safely say that in plastic surgery there are techniques, that guarantee the return of youth and reduce the age of an average of 10 years.

At the moment, there are two main ways to carry out facelift surgery is classical open access and an endoscopic facelift through mini-incisions.

Different Types Of Facelifts With Dr Devinder S. Mangat

The technique of modern facelift is a synergy of endoscopic and open method of face rejuvenation, which involves the tightening of different areas of the face, including endoscopic lift of the middle third of the face. The technique of authorial facelift is a deep facelift in Cincinnati.

During the operation, the doctor combines open and endoscopic approaches, corrects not only the skin but also the more deeply muscular-aponeurotic layer (SMAS), as well as adipose tissue. All this for the sake of achieving the most pronounced anti-age result.

The unique advantages of total facelift:

  • correction of the skin, musculo-aponeurotic structures and lipid tissue gives not only a pronounced, but stable effect of rejuvenation, on average, for 10 years;
  • does not require additional cosmetic procedures (injections of fillers, hardware techniques);
  • pronounced rejuvenation not only of the face, but also of the neck;
  • eliminates the need for a repeat operation in a few years.

Total facelift is a large-scale operation, including a number of consecutive stages.

Doctor Allison M. Holzapfel Half Face Lift

Endoscopic lifting of the upper third of the face – the first of the stages. It includes an endoscopic lift of the forehead, eyebrows, and the temporal region. This technique allows you to avoid big incisions in the scalp, limited to small accesses about 15-20 mm, in which a thin tube with a camera at the end (endoscope) is placed.

The enlarged image is transmitted to the screen, so it is more convenient for the expert to control his work and the duration of the operation is reduced.

With endoscopic lift of the upper third of the face, the surgeon gently separates the skin from the bone and fixes it in a new place, while the nervous and vascular tissues are not damaged, which is extremely important.

As a result of endoscopic lifting of the upper zone of the face, horizontal wrinkles of the forehead, wrinkles on the bridge of the nose, “crow’s feet” disappear, eyebrows rise slightly upward.

Facial Face Lift

The plastic surgeon works particularly neatly around the eyes. The tissues in this area are tightened so that the eye does not change its natural shape. Often, you can see that the face loses its former features, especially noticeable in the eye area after a circular facelift.

The sensation, that the skin around the eyes literally was pulled on the temples, the eyes acquire an unnaturally almond-shaped.

Endoscopic lift of the upper third of the face includes the upper blepharoplasty: excess tissue is carefully excised, the thinnest seam is hidden in the fold of the upper eyelid.

After the operation, the sight becomes more open. It should be noted that the endoscopic method of facelift is today the safest and the least traumatic.

But, the lower blepharoplasty already refers to the endoscopic lift of the middle zone of the face. Fatty herniations of the lower eyelid and excess tissue are removed, the skin is sewn to the lash line of the lower eyelid. Thus, the issue with bags under the eyes, a nasolacose and a groove is solved forever.

Full Face Lift With Dr Daniel G. Kuy

The next stage is the rejuvenation of the cheek area, cheekbones, removal of the nasolabial fold, ptosis of the middle zone of the face by the endoscopic method. Access is through a small incision on the temple (in the hair) or intraoral access.

Further, the doctor proceeds to SMAS-lift, rejuvenation at the level of the musculo-aponeurotic layer.

It is worth noting, that a procedure of this kind can only be performed by a specialist with a lot of experience, since at this level there are nerve endings everywhere.

During the operation, many shortcomings are corrected: deep wrinkles in the cheek area, nasolabial folds, and also fat deposits. The main advantage of SMAS-lifting is that the surgeon has the opportunity to work complex on the muscular, and lipid tissue. As a result, the facial contours becomes clear, the furrows and deep folds disappear, while the naturalness persist. It should be added that SMAS-lift is also one of low traumatic procedures

Hyaluronic Acid Face Injections Before And After

During facelift in Cincinnati, the surgeon often installs silicone endoprostheses in the cheek and chin areas.

Facial implants are installed in order to give the person femininity, restore asymmetry, and also add volume to the soft tissues of the face. Implants are installed through a tiny incision that is not noticeable at all.

Recovery period can be longer and take from 6 to 12 weeks. However, at the end of the recovery, when all swelling disappear, and the scars heal, the result exceeds even the most daring expectations of patients.

The face looks young and radiant, while the naturalness is preserved. Total facelift is ideal for patients with severe age-related skin changes (ptosis of soft tissues, loss of volume, bags under the eyes).
