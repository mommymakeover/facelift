Hi I don’t think lift is the right answer for you. Volume loss is a major factor in your case rather than tissue descent. Any suggestions for congenital ptosis?
Congenital ptosis
Jan 28, 2016
The severity of the ptosis is significant as such can obstruct the visual axis and cause visual impairment. I agree that survey should be done early on; in cases like this when amblyopia is at stake, (risk of visual impairment from the eyelid occluding the pupil) I recommend frontalis suspension using a proline suture.
