Can someone with a heart murmur get liposuction?
Safety of liposuction with a heart condition
Dec 29, 2021
Liposuction can be performed safely in patients with a variety of heart conditions including a “heart murmur”. However, safety depends on a variety of factors including nature of the heart condition, amount of liposuction, and type of anesthesia. A full assessment of the safety and risk of a procedure requires a conversation between the person managing your heart murmur, plastic surgeon and anesthesiologist.
