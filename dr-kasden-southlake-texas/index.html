Statement: Dr. Scott E. Kasden is a board-certified plastic surgeon with nearly three decades of experience, serving patients in the Dallas, Fort Worth and Greater Metroplex areas of Texas. Using the newest technology and techniques, Dr. Kasden specializes in breast augmentation, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers, as well as cosmetic rehabilitation for patients after massive weight loss. He also has expertise in both surgical and nonsurgical facial rejuvenation options, from full facelifts to Botox and dermal fillers like Juvéderm.

“My staff and I take the time to educate our patients on all of their options, answer questions, and create the right expectations,” says Dr. Kasden. “My number one goal is to perform a safe and successful procedure, and we work hard to make sure that our patients are as comfortable as possible so they can enjoy this transformative experience.”

(Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education) general surgery residency program at Western Pennsylvania Hospital in Pittsburgh, followed by a plastic surgery residency at the Medical College of Ohio in Toledo.
