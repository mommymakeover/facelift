Cost Of Facelift In Portland Oregon

Are you ready for a facelift surgery? Thinking of having a facelift surgery to finally smoothen out all those unwanted wrinkles?

Before you decide to go under the knife, it is important to first know the facts and risks facial surgery brings.

Facial surgery facts

Take note that facelifts could be done on the whole face or just in one facial area such as the brows, neck or cheeks.

Though facelift effects could last for almost a decade, the most excellent facelift surgery results are brought on by having a body weight that is stable, a well-built bone structure and good skin elasticity.

Believe it or not, people who spend a lot of time soaking in the sun have surgery results that do not last as long as it should.

Be aware, prepare

It is best to discuss your facial surgery plans with a general practitioner you trust. He or she may be able to recommend a good surgeon or offer you helpful advice on which is the best hospital for your pre and post-care surgery needs.

Though generally safe, side effects and complications still exist in a facelift surgery. As much as possible, gather all the information you need to be able to make an informed decision.

Prepare yourself for the usual effects surgery brings such as a sick feeling brought on by general anesthetic. Other side effects of facelift surgery are swelling, soreness and bruising which could last for a month. Scarring may also occur. Though it fades after a year, it doesn’t really disappear.

Dr James Chan Face Lift Treatment

Complications also occur either after or during the facelift surgery, albeit rarely. These include an unexpected and adverse reaction to the anesthetic, blood clotting, excessive bleeding and even infection.

Other complications include hematoma, bleeding underneath the skin; facial nerve damage, muscle weakness and numbness in the face; keloids or raised red scars; and loss of hair around the scar area.

Another notable risk is that the earlobes have a possibility to hang at an angle that is irregular making the ears appear unbalanced.

Cases have also occurred where after going through facelift surgery, patients were not satisfied with how they look.

All in all, the specific facelift surgery risks are unique for each person. It is best to ask your chosen surgeon how each risk applies to your case and needs. More importantly, know what you want and what you expect from the operation. Doing so helps you face your surgery courageously – before, during and after.

What to expect from face lift surgery in Portland OR ?

As it has been mentioned earlier that traditional facelift surgery is a right choice for those who want to get rid of wrinkles and sagging at their entire face. Contrarily the mini facelift is handy for those who want to reduce sagging and droopiness only at some specific area of their face.

Areas like forehead or brows can’t be addressed under Mini facelift procedure. While traditional facelift procedures often seem to produce good results even in these difficult areas. You can consult a plastic surgeon to know the merits of each face lift surgery procedure and to know which one is suitable for you.

Mini facelift procedure can be carried out without using the undoscopic scope, but in this procedure more incisions are made around the face lift.

In face lift surgery in Portland OR, the targeted areas are relatively minor and the incisions that are made under the low-stress approach are quite small. Because of these small incisions, mini facelift surgery is considered the least invasive procedure.

Short Scar Facelift Portland Oregon

The use of an endoscopic or a non-endoscopic approach largely depends on the situation.

Under an endoscopic procedure, the surgeon made three or more small cuts to insert surgical instruments around the area for the treatment.

These instruments help to remove excess fat and reshape muscles in the face. The whole procedure is observed by the surgeons through a small camera that is also inserted below the skin.

Under non-endoscopic procedure continuous incisions are made and the skin is turned over to expose the area for treatment.

In both these approaches of the mini facelift minimal cutting of the skin is made and that’s why the risk of scarring and other possible facelift complications are quite low.
