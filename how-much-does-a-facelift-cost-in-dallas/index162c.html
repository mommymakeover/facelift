Recently, the number of men and women who are seeking help from plastic surgeons to improve theirs appearance has increased noticeably.

A young and well-groomed face in our time is the key to success in almost all spheres of activity, therefore, the face and neck lift gradually becomes one of the most widespread plastic surgeries in the world.

Unfortunately, the aging of the soft tissues of the face is inevitable and is part of the natural general biological changes in the body.

First of all, the following anatomical structures are subject to involutionary restructuring: skin, cheeks ( sucking pads), superficial muscular-aponeurotic system (SMAS system).

Age-related changes are largely due to a decrease in supporting collagen structures in soft tissues and the impact of gravity on them. As a result, there is a sagging of the tissues down – the eyebrows, upper and lower eyelids, cheeks.

Are formed and deepened infraorbital and nasolabial furrows. Age changes also affect the neck. The skin becomes flabby, horizontal wrinkles are formed, the cervical-chin angle disappears.

The process of aging of soft tissues of the face can accelerate various somatic diseases, exposure to harmful environmental factors and bad habits.

Thus, under the influence of a complex of causes, the wrinklesin the forehead area, eyes, nasolabial, infraorbital furrows are formed, the face oval changes and then it becomes clear that even professional cosmetology care is not enough.

What is the best age for facelift?

As a rule, the skin of the face and neck are largely susceptible to stretching and sagging at the age of 40-50 years. Sometimes, a serious correction of appearance is required at an earlier age due to individual characteristics (premature aging 30-35 years old).

Indications for Facelift surgery.

Indication for the operation:

  • The sagging of the skin of the forehead and eyebrows;
  • Formation of wrinkles along the nose;
  • Ptosis of the eyelids;
  • Omitted corners of the eyes;
  • Deep vertical wrinkles on cheeks;
  • Deep nasolabial folds;
  • Ptosis of soft tissues cheeks below the edge of the lower jaw (“flew”);
  • “Double chin”;

Contraindications.

  • Severe diseases of internal organs;

  • Oncological diseases;
  • Violations of blood clotting

Preoperative preparation of the patient.

For 2 weeks before surgery, do not take medications, that affect blood clotting (eg, aspirin, cough suppressant).The day before the operation is necessary to conduct a day of limited intake of food(light foods in small quantities).

A circular face and neck lift is a long operation (4-5 hours), requiring general anesthesia (inhalation anesthesia). Lifting individual areas of the face, such as the forehead and eyebrows, can be performed under local anesthesia or intravenous anesthesia.

During surgery, it is possible to eliminate large wrinkles, improve the face oval and correct age-related changes in the neck area by removing excess skin and tightening it, strengthening deeply lying muscular-aponeurotic structures (SMAS lifting), applying the liposuction method. It should be noted, that an isolated facelift without affecting SMAS structures gives a short aesthetic result.

Endoscopic facelift

Endoscopic lift in Dallas is a modern facelift technique. All surgical manipulations during endoscopic lifting are performed by thin optical-fiber endoscopes. Endoscopes are conducted under the skin through micro-incision 4-5 (up to 1 cm) placed on the scalp.

Endoscopic facelift, as a rule, is used for lifting the skin of the upper third of the face. It can be used for mid face lift for patients with minimal sagging of soft tissues.

Postoperative period

Stay in the hospital is 2-3 days. In the postoperative period, a special compression mask should be worn for a week. Postoperative swelling reaches a peak at 2 – 3 days after surgery and disappears by the end of the first week. The sutures in front of the auricles are removed for 6-7 days (to form a flat inconspicuous scar), in the scalp on the 10th day after the operation.

The head can be washed a week after the operation, dye your hair after a month. A week after the operation, you can use makeup. After 10-14 days the patient can return to workplace. You can not play sports (heavy loads) for 1.5 months.

Sunbathing and visiting the sauna can be in 1 month. After 3-4 weeks to achieve better results, it is advisable to conduct a course of lymphatic drainage massage, before this time, facial massage is contraindicated.

The final result of the operation is formed by the end of the second month, when the puffiness of the skin finally disappears

Complications

The most likely complications are:

Edema of the facial skin and bruises are weakly expressed when using fibrin glue. Skin pigmentation occurs in patients with sensitive and delicate skin with intradermal hemorrhages, disappears on its own within six months after the operation. Temporary hair loss around the skin incisions in the scalp, in the future their growth is restored independently.

Rarely occurring complications:

  • Bleeding in the postoperative period is 1%.
  • Necrosis of the edges of skin grafts is less than 1% (the risk of occurrence increases in chronic smokers and elderly patients).
  • Damage to the branches of the facial nerve.
  • Weakness of individual facial muscles is less than 1% (disappears within 3-6 months).
  • Damage to the branches of the ear nerve, a temporary disturbance of the sensitivity of the earlobe is less than 1%.

  • Infection of postoperative wounds.
  • Hypertrophic scars occur when the patient has an individual tendency to form rough scars.

Results

Facelift and neck lifting in Dallas will help patients look 7-8 years younger. The end result largely depends on the age of the patient, the initial condition of the skin, the presence of concomitant diseases and bad habits, the characteristics of the lifestyle (work, nutrition, etc.).

The same factors will determine the duration of the rejuvenating effect after the operation. Repeated facelifts can be performed at intervals of 5-10 years.

Therapeutic cosmetic procedures significantly delay the re-operation. Mesotherapy, photorejuvenation, lymph drainage massage and masks fix the result for many years and are the prevention of the re-occurrence of soft facial ptosis and deep wrinkles.
