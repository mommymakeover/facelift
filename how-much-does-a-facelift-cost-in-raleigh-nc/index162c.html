How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Raleigh NC?

Facelift is a very effective and quite affordable way of correcting such age-related changes in the face as wrinkles, reducing the elasticity of the skin, sagging the tissues of the neck and face.

The most characteristic signs that testify to the need for facelift surgery are:

– flabbiness of the muscles and skin of the neck and decolletage;
– sagging cheeks and chin;
– double chin;
– deep wrinkles on the forehead, sagging of the skin in this area and in the eyebrows;
– wrinkles on temples, cheekbones and in the nose area;
– omitted the outer corners of the eyes and eyelids ptosis;
– “jawls”;
– pronounced nasolabial folds and wrinkles on the cheeks.

Facelift is contraindicated in oncological diseases, severe diseases of internal organs, high blood pressure, patients with poor blood coagulability, during infectious diseases.

Planning and carrying out the operation facelift

When planning facelift surgery, you should definitely consult specialists on all issues. Firstly, we must clearly define our wishes and really assess the possibilities of achieving them in the process of tightening the contour of the face and tissues of the neck.

Secondly, at the stage of preparation for an operation to assess the risk factors during a facelift, the picture of your own health should be fully described to your doctor.

At the preparatory stage for the operation, a medical examination is carefully conducted, which makes it possible to identify all possible contraindications and limitations to the operation. After studying the state of health of the client, the doctor examines in detail the skin condition, muscle structure and subcutaneous fat, since the course of the operation depends on the anatomy of the face. Then the planned results are discussed. Different variants of the operation may be considered. This is an isolated forehead lift, and endoscopic facelift, and neck lift, and other.

Doctor Glenn M. Davis Quick Lift Pictures Results

Mini facelift is very popular. The incisions are performed in places of natural skin folds and hair growth lines in order to hide the traces of the operation. During the operation, excess skin is removed and the muscles of the face are strengthened.

When preparing for surgery, one and a half to two weeks before it, you should stop smoking, drinking alcohol and taking hormonal drugs. One day before the operation to be a fasting day, allowing only a liquid diet. The very operation is done on an empty stomach.

A facelift is performed under general anesthesia, and local facelift allows use intravenous or local anesthesia.

The operation lasts 1-3 hours, depending on the individual features of the structure of the operated parts of the body.

Postoperative period

The postoperative period assumes the patient is in the clinic for 1-3 days. At this time, antibiotics and pain medications are used. 3-5 days after the operation, the first dressing is performed. The sutures are removed after a week, but the edema partially passes within 2-3 weeks. Swelling subsides in about two months, but the effect of facelift can be fully assessed after six months or a year. It depends on the lifestyle of the client and the state of his health. Cosmetic procedures consolidate the effect of the operation.
