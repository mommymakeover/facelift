Patient satisfaction is Dr. Motykie’s number one goal. At his West Hollywood practice, he and his staff provide exceptional patient care and results, while ensuring the safety and well-being of his patients throughout their experience. Before he embarked on a medical career, Dr. Motykie was a personal trainer and fitness competitor, which led to his interest in learning about the human body and its functions. Dr. Motykie is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery Memberships. He is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, as well as a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.
