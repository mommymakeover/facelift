Would I benefit from breast liposuction on my bigger breast?
Breast liposuction on ptotic(sagging) breasts
Oct 10, 2019
Reducing the volume of breasts that already sag would further increase the sagging. Not a good idea unless you are willing to accept this.  Liposuction of the breast is really limited to the  area of fat between the skin and actual breast cells. Consider having a mastopexy to reshape the breasts and make them more symmetric

There are many " wanna be" physicians who make false claims of being board certified in plastic surgery  but are not.
