Facetite

I would not recommend doing this procedure before a lift. You want your surgeon to have the best possible result and messing with the tissue that close to surgery could be risky. Best of luck.
How to get rid of redness, pigmentation, and dry dull skin?
Skin
Dec 29, 2019
There are so many options on the market. You need to find a reputable provider that you can develop a plan with. Skin care is an on going commitment. There is no one stop treatment for skin. In our practice we love BBL by sciton for pigment issues in skin. Best of luck with your search!
Will Botox help these static wrinkles or will I need filler too?
Botox
Dec 29, 2019
Once the lines have etched in the dermis; Botox will not get rid of them. They will soften over time with consistent use of botox. Medical grade skin care, chemical peels, laser treatments will help with the lines that are etched in. Filler in this area is not recommended. Most of out patients do both Botox and ressurfacing to get the skin smooth and healthy. Then we maintain with botox 3-4 times per year.

Does saline injections cause blindness?
Saline
Dec 29, 2019
The reason that there is a risk of blindness is that the filler can occlude a vessel; saline can not do this. Therefore, there is no risk of blindness. Always see an experienced injector with the appropriate reversal agents in office when getting filler.
Do I need more filler in my lips to correct the unevenness?
Filler
Dec 29, 2019
Thank you for your question and photo. There is a lot of talk on symmetry in our line of work. No one will ever be perfectly symmetrical. In my practice, subtle assymmetry isn't worth chasing with filler. You end up bouncing back and forth. I'd allow them to settle out for a while. You could always dissolve and start over in a month or so as well. Best of luck.
