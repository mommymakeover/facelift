Ramtin Kassir, MD, FACS

Name: Ramtin Kassir, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Ramtin Kassir, MD, FACS is a triple board-certified facial plastic surgeon and founder of Kassir Plastic Surgery, which has offices in New York City as well as Ridgewood and Wayne, New Jersey. He is best known for his work performing facial surgeries and non-invasive treatments, including rhinoplasty, facelifts, neck lifts, and injectable fillers. Celebrities and international clientele travel to Dr. Kassir for his expertise in providing natural, “designer” results.

Board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery, and the American Board of Sleep Medicine, Dr. Kassir is a graduate of the University of Texas Medical Branch at Galveston and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons. He is frequently asked to share his expertise at conferences and seminars around the world.
