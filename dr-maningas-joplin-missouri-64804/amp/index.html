Lip lift and implants possible? I have very thin lips, a simple lip lift won’t be enough for me. (Ph
lip lift and lip implant
Feb 7, 2018
Yes, both of these can be performed at the same time if desired. However, I would make sure you understand the implications of each procedure. The lip implant will obviously add fullness and volume to the body of the lips. The lip lift with decrease the distance from your lips to your nose and roll the lip out – a byproduct is creating more dental show. Make sure to go over each procedure with your surgeon and look at plenty of before and after pics. Good luck!

Can seroma be avoided after face and neck lift surgery?
Seroma after facelift
Feb 7, 2018
Seromas after facelift are a rare occurrence but can happen. If they occur they typically need drained and may require serial draining over the course of days to weeks. They should not inhibit your final result if managed appropriately.
