How easy or difficult is it to accidentally move the wrong way and rupture internal stitches?
Popping Sutures Post Facelift
Nov 19, 2018
It is very difficult to pop a stitch after facelift surgery. After 6 months or so the tissues have healed in place and the sutures are not as necessary. Second, most surgeons use interrupted sutures so there may be more than one suture doing the job so the loss of a suture is not a problem. If you actually break a support suture you may get pain and a touch of swelling and bruising. Either way it is rare to have the need to replace the suture.

How long does numbness last after a facelift?
Numbness After Facelift
Nov 7, 2018
All facelifts have some degree of numbness and some have stinging, burning and shooting pains. These problems are all normal and more importantly-temporary. At 4-6 weeks the sensation begins to return but not yet totally normal. Over the next 6 months, normal sensation slowly begins to return. It happens so gradually that it is hard to put a distinct time period on the return of normal sensation.
