Fillers or Facelift?
Jun 2, 2016
Over time and/or for hereditary reasons, our face undergoes changes that include: loss of soft tissue volume, loss of bony volume, and sagging tissues. We use surgery (ie. mini-lift, facelift) to address sagging tissues. Can Asymmetric Eyes or Eyebrows Be Fixed?
Brow asymmetry
Apr 29, 2015
Close examination of the face reveals asymmetry is very common in the face. We inform patients that one side of the face is the sister (or brother) and not the twin of the other side. Asymmetries can be treated so that the asymmetry is less dramatic, but it is nearly impossible (if not outright impossible) to achieve perfect symmetry in facial plastic surgery.

How Long Should Swelling and Numbness Last After a Facelift?
Numbness after facelift
Apr 29, 2015
Numbness following a facelift can take up to 1 year to resolve. The majority of patients experience improvement of the numbness within 3-6 months after facelift. Patients typically experience tingling sensations as the nerves begin to regain sensation function. As for swelling, it improves at 2-3 weeks after facelift so that patients are able to return to work and social engagements within that time frame. Further resolution of the swelling occurs within 3 months after facelift.
