Statement: Dr. Troy Robbin Hailparn is a board-certified OB-GYN practicing in San Antonio, Texas. As the owner and medical director of the Cosmetic Gynecology Center of San Antonio, she focuses on vaginal rejuvenation, female genital cosmetic surgery, and sexual wellness.

The author of the book Beneath Your Pink Perfect: Everything You Always Wanted to Know About Labiaplasty, Dr. Hailparn has performed over 4,000 vaginal surgeries and over 900 labiaplasties. She was one of the first female OB-GYNs to be trained by the Laser Vaginal Rejuvenation Institute.

Dr. Hailparn offers a variety of cosmetic gynecological treatments, including labiaplasty, vaginal reconstruction (vaginoplasty), clitoral hood reduction, hymenoplasty, perineoplasty, mons pubis lifts and liposuction. She also offers nonsurgical treatment options like ThermiVA and the O shot, an innovative procedure that uses PRP (platelet-rich plasma) injections to enhance sexual sensitivity and response.

After obtaining her medical degree from the Albert Einstein College of Medicine in New York, Dr. Hailparn completed her obstetrics and gynecology residency at the Winthrop University Hospital in Mineola, New York. In 2003, she began focusing her practice on cosmetic gynecology, and underwent advanced training in vaginal rejuvenation, labiaplasty, and vaginal reconstruction.

Dr. Hailparn is board-certified in obstetrics and gynecology by the American Board of Obstetrics & Gynecology. Throughout her long career, Dr. Hailparn has become a respected figure in the field of cosmetic gynecology. She has traveled the world as a guest speaker at gynecology conferences and participated in the very first scientific session on cosmetic-plastic gynecology at the XX FIGO World Congress, which is considered the world’s most prestigious OB-GYN conference.

Patients from all over the world visit Dr. Hailparn’s San Antonio practice to experience her life-changing procedures. She is known by her patients for her warm and compassionate demeanor, as well as her surgical skills and expertise.
