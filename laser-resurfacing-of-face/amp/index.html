They look like themselves, but with a more attractive face with more youthful curves, and better facial balance and symmetry. If a patient has a problem with facial balance and symmetry, it will remain the same after laser resurfacing and often after facelift surgery too. Only LR/AFI, the facelift alternative, consistently balances facial symmetry problems, producing a more perfect and attractive face.

The procedure is gentle and does not require heavy sedation or general anesthesia. After mild sedation from an IM injection, the face and the fat donor sites are locally anesthetized with tumescent technique, the safe form of liposuction invented by Dermatologists.

Donor fat is then harvested with microcannulae from either the tummy, waist, or hip. Microinjection cannulae are then used to place the fat in a three dimensional meshwork of small cylinders, to build up the desired areas of the face.

Sometimes jowls of the lower face are reduced with liposuction at the same time. When the facial sculpting is completed, the UltraPulse CO2 laser resurfacing is then used to lift and tighten everything and to erase fine wrinkles and sun damage. A dressing is placed over the face that eliminates pain, and is not touched for five days. An ointment is used for the eyelids, and upper chin.

At five days the dressing is removed, and the skin will be 90-100% healed. Patients frequently use the ointment a bit longer for the areas just below the eyes and the upper chin. At 7-10 days we want patients using either a sunscreen that contains a makeup-like cover product, or using sunscreen containing make-up.

With laser resurfacing of face with autologous fat injection , LR/AFI, we obtain fantastic results without aggressive laser work. In five years of laser resurfacing we have never had a scar occur. We have also led the country in preventing post-op mild color problems like hypopigmentation.

Autologous Fat Injection

Laser Skin Resurfacing

Autologous Fat Injection is a procedure with no incisions by which a small amount of donor fat is taken by syringe liposuction technique from the tummy or hips and injected under the skin to buildup the lip and cheek areas.

Patients who do well with fat injection are those who need additional fat in the areas of the lips, deep smile lines, around the mouth, and cheekbones.

The results create a very pleasing, youthful appearance that lasts years. Fat injection also helps people who have acne scarring. Eyes come alive with improved light reflection from the new cheeks. Cheeks are smooth, rounded and sexy. Improves harsh angular facial features and makes a more attractive patient with more perfect facial symmetry.
