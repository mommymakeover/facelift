Can you raise your eyebrows after treatment for 11 lines?
Raising Brow After Botox
Sep 15, 2020
The answer is - yes! In fact, that is the precise goal of doing Botox to the 11s. If you target these muscles that are responsible for pulling the brow down, your forehead should get a nice chemical brow raise afterwards. This is because the muscles that pull the eyebrow upward are left alone and can function without an opposing force.
