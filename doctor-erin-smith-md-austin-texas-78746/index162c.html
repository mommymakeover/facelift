She also studied under some of the world’s leading oculoplastic surgeons and cosmetic dermatologists while in Beverly Hills and has extensive training in non-surgical treatments including: Botox, fillers, lasers, skin tightening technologies, and hair restoration.

Will using Botox for glabellar lines lead to smoother skin and less product usage over time?
Botox for glabellar lines
Jun 29, 2023
The use of neuromodulators such as Botox may lead to improvement in static and dynamic lines of the glabella due to weakening or decreased muscle movement. Some studies have suggested the rare possibility of decreased movement even when the neuromodulators have worn off. It is advised you discuss this with your treating provider.
Should I try Botox over Dysport?
Trying different neuromodulators
May 24, 2023
There are several different neuromodulators available for use to achieve improvement in the dynamic wrinkles of the face. Some patients have better reported outcomes with a particular brand, therefore it is reasonable to try a different brand of neuromodulator to assess how you respond. Be sure to discuss with your treating provider on different brands that they carry and have experience with.
Should I go to a dentist or cosmetic dermatologist for Botox to treat masseter muscle hypertrophy?
Neuromodulator use for bruxism
May 24, 2023
There are many different providers who may have the skill set to perform injections for bruxism. One of the more important aspects is seeing a provider who can make an assessment to help determine the cause of your issues prior to making treatment recommendations.
Best option to fix my deep mental creases in chin?
Deep mental crease
May 24, 2023
Volume augmentation of the mental crease utilizing intradermal filler would be a good starting point. An experienced injector could tailor the result to achieve your desired goals, and using a hyaluronic acid filler has the added benefit of being reversible if you decide you do not like the results. I recommend follow up with an experienced injector to discuss your options.
