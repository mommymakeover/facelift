Why do I need a facelift?

No one is insured against age-related skin changes.

In connection with the reduction in the necessary amount of elastin and collagen (substances responsible for normal elasticity and skin tone), wrinkles appear is the first signs of aging.

Over time, they become deep, the skin of the face fades. In order to significantly slow down the aging process and remove wrinkles (including deep wrinkles) and used surgical facelift.

How is a surgical face lift performed?

The surgeon makes incisions on the skin behind the ears. Through these incisions, special surgical instruments are introduced into the subcutaneous tissue for exfoliation and stretching of the skin folds.

The plastic surgeon tightens the skin in the desired direction, while smoothing wrinkles. Excess skin, which occurs while doing this, is removed with the help of surgical instruments. At the end of the operation, cosmetic sutures are applied.

IMPORTANT! Stitches after facelift surgery in Tulsa are usually removed 7-10 days after surgery. The effect of the surgery, the patient begins to notice after 2-3 weeks, the skin’s natural healing in areas of intervention. The final effect occurs one and a half to two months after the operation.
