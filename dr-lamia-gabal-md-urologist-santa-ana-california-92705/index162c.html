Does SculpSure permanently destroy fat cells in the treated area?
SculpSure and fat cells
Jul 13, 2020
Yes! The laser kills cells permanently by heating them, a process known as “lipolysis.” Your body then clears out those dead fat cells, thereby reducing the size of the area. Those particular cells are gone forever. The fat cells remaining in the area, however, can still enlarge if a patient continues to gain weight, so it is important to eat healthy and exercise in order to maintain the benefit of SculpSure warm sculpting.
Sculpture on outer chest for male – Is it safe for men to have on the outer chest area?
Sculpture on outer chest
Jul 13, 2020
Yes! SculpSure is a very effective treatment for the fat in the area at the outer chest, whether male or female. It is very dependent upon each individual’s anatomy, however. You should have a consultation with an experienced provider to decide if this would be a good treatment for you!

Cankle Treatment

The answer is “yes!” Labial asymmetry is a very common finding on exam. It bothers some women more than others.
