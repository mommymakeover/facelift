Facelift In Oakland

As we age, wrinkles, skin laxity, and jowls become increasingly prominent in the face and neck. A facelift in Oakland elevates the soft tissues of the lower half of the face.

Facelifts are no longer reserved for movie stars or narcissists. More individuals today are taking advantage of the available cosmetic procedures to look young, and many people you know have probably already had face lifts.

The Procedure

Face lift surgery in Oakland if usually done with general anesthesia and takes about four hours.

Inconspicuous incisions in skin folds or hair bearing areas allow the adjacent skin to be separated from its underlying muscle.

The skin and underlying muscles are tightened and then the excess skin is removed. The skin is sutured snugly in place, loose bandages are applied and the patient may return home the day of the surgery.

Doctor Trevor M. Born O Therapy Facelift Picture Results

Typically facelifts are performed using general anesthesia, in an outpatient facility or at a hospital. Twenty-three hour observation is available if the patient needs assistance the first day.

After Surgery

Pain is minimal and relieved with oral medications for a day or two after the procedure.

The night of the surgery patients may stay at an aftercare facility or have a friend stay with them at home. Patients usually feel woozy from the anesthesia.

There will be bulky bandages after the procedure which will be removed the day after the procedure.

Surgeon may also place small drains under the skin which are removed the day after surgery.

Dr R. Stephen Mulholland Plastic Surgery For Face

Doctor will remove some sutures at seven days and others at ten days after the surgery. Bruising and swelling subsides in seven to 14 days. The patient then will then be able to return to work and social situations but may be advised to avoid the sun for several weeks. It is important to limit your activities for several days, and not drive until your doctor gives you the go-ahead. It will probably be about two weeks before you can return to work.

Of course, as with any major medical procedure, there are certain risks, which should be discussed with a qualified physician.

Traditional facelift surgery in Oakland corrects wrinkles and sagging throughout the entire face.

This surgery provides the most dramatic and long-lasting results. However, recovery from traditional facelifts may take several weeks or months, and permanent scars by the hairline and in front of the ears may remain.

Endoscopic (or “scarless”) facelift surgery addresses saggy cheeks, which can create frown lines or creases around the nose and mouth.

Endoscopic facelifts are cheaper, quicker and less invasive than traditional surgeries, resulting in less recovery time and a lower risk of complications. This surgery is most effective for cheek sagging and cannot fix problems in the neck area.

Mid facelift surgery targets bags under the eyes, the cheeks, and wrinkles around the nose, eyes, and corners of the mouth. Mid facelifts require minimal recovery time and result in less bruising and swelling than traditional procedures. However, results are modest and subtle.

Dr. Leonard Harris Mini Facelift Surgery Result

Mini (or “weekend”) facelift surgery in Oakland reduces minor sagging around the cheeks, jawline, and neck. This procedure uses small incisions, and has a short recovery period. This procedure is only appropriate for individuals who need slight skin tightening.

Thread facelifts reduce minor sagging around the cheeks, jawline, and neck without anesthesia. The procedure is quick and any scarring is virtually invisible. This procedure is best for people seeking a remedy that delays the need for a more intensive facelift technique.

All types of facelifts provide more permanent changes than nonsurgical face reshaping techniques (that must be repeated after a few months). Also, facelift surgeries directly target the loss of skin elasticity due to aging, resulting in more dramatic results.
