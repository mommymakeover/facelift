What can I do to improve my breasts (I don’t want implants)?
Breast lift and fat transfer to the breasts
Dec 8, 2020
You would benefit from a breast lift procedure. If you are bothered by the bra bulge, performing liposuction in this area is a nice way to contour the outside border of the breast. If you would like to increase your breast volume as well, this can be done without implants using a fat transfer procedure.
Do you believe this would be an augmentation or a mastopexy also?
Augmentation vs Lift
Dec 6, 2020
The best way to know for sure is with an in-person consultation, but judging by your photos I would recommend an augmentation only. If you are dissatisfied with nipple position, you can consider adding a mastopexy at a second stage (3-6 months after your initial surgery).
What is wrong with my breasts and how can they be fixed?
Breast reduction procedures
Dec 6, 2020
You have large, saggy breasts and would benefit from a breast reduction procedure. This procedure will simultaneously lift the nipple and reduce the breasts in size.
Tuberous breast augmentation without a lift?
Tuberous breasts
Dec 6, 2020
You don’t need a lift in the traditional sense, but in tuberous breast cases, the breast fold needs to be lowered and a suturing technique is used to correct the shape of the areola. This places a scar circumferentially around the areola like a circumareolar lift does.
Best way to fix breast asymmetry?
Breast asymmetry and constriction
Dec 6, 2020
Both your breasts are constricted. The smaller breast is tuberous and the larger breast has some constriction near the center of your chest. To correct this, you will likely need some combination of asymmetrically sized implants and breast lift/breast reduction procedures. Because of your asymmetry, you should consider performing this in a staged fashion. In severe cases of breast constriction, insurance will cover surgery.
Are there any solutions for outward facing nipples/breasts?
Breast augmentation and nipple position
Dec 6, 2020
If your nipples are facing outwardly, unfortunately breast augmentation will not correct this. There is a possibility that this might be exaggerated by a breast augmentation. A procedure known as a circumareolar mastopexy can be added to medialize the nipples (move them in).
Facelift – if yes what type please?
Facelift
Dec 6, 2020
You would benefit from a brow lift, high SMAS face and neck lift, upper eyelid lift, and fat transfer to the face. Fillers and Botox will only create minor changes, and if you are interested in a more long lasting result, I would recommend surgical intervention.
From 32A to 325 cc ultra high profile?
Cup size
Dec 6, 2020
Cup size can be deceiving in breast augmentation, because it varies so much from one bra manufacturer to another. For example, a Victoria’s Secret D cup is very different from other manufacturers’ D cups. As a general rule of thumb, every 150-200 CCs is about one cup size, so you will likely be 1.5 – 2 cup sizes larger.

Periareolar incisions

Periareolar incisions are advantageous for patients who have a constricted breast or a tuberous shape, or for patients who are simultaneously undergoing a circumareolar breast lift for mild breast droop.
