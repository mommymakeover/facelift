Is it safe to perform ablative CO2 laser resurfacing on the neck and the face at the same time?
CO2 laser resurfacing with facelift
Mar 31, 2019
Great question, and the answer is yes in most cases. I perform face/neck lift surgery very frequently and combine the surgery with a fractional CO2 laser of the brow, eyelids, cheeks and peri-oral region (around the mouth). This improves overall skin quality as well as improving the appearance of the neck and jowls. The key is picking the right patient, and specifically avoiding patients with darker skin types.
Facelift and rhinoplasty 2 weeks post-op: Is this nerve damage and will it repair.
Asymmetry post-op facelift
Mar 26, 2017
Based on your photo you have some nerve weakness on one side. I see this sometimes after surgery and it nearly always resolves, though it may take a few weeks to begin looking normal. The nerves can be irritated from the surgery and are not likely damaged significantly. Thanks for the question!
I am 4 post facelift and necklift. Is this a bad scar? What would you recommend to improve this? (photo
Pre-tragal scar for facelift
Mar 13, 2017
I commonly put this scar behind the tragus in order to hide it better, but in most cases the scar will fade and be barely noticeable by 6 months after surgery. There are lots of scar therapies out there, but I have found the best to be silicone gel or tape and also laser treatments like fractional erbium and CO2 lasers. Do not worry as the appearance right now is just fine and will continue to improve.

I did a facelift 19 days ago. 7 days after surgery appeared in my face a skin necrosis?
Facelift healing issue
Mar 5, 2017
Unfortunately this can happen in facelift surgery as the skin flaps can be long and have decreased blood supply. The good news is that the face tends to heal very well in time and you are likely to only need a minor revision or laser treatment to make the scar fade. You do NOT need surgery now. Be patient and listen to your surgeon's advice and all will likely be just fine.
