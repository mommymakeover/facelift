Which filler would you recommend for cheek lines?
Dermal fillers
Sep 21, 2021
Hello,
Your perioral/cheek rhytids are not that deep, so I would recommend starting with a hyaluronic acid filler, such as Juvederm Ultra or Ultra Plus XC, may even one of the RHA fillers, which are better for treating dynamic lines and wrinkles. Radiesse is longer lasting and does not have a quick reversal if you want to dissolve it.
