How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Tampa Florida?

The term facelift unites a wide range of plastic surgeries, allowing to remove all age-related changes, restore the facial contours and attractiveness.

Surgical facelift in Tampa is recommended for men and women over 40 years of age, since at a younger age, facial skin tightening can be achieved with a number of non-surgical techniques.

With facelift surgery can successfully deal with the problem of soft tissue ptosis, so that a person can rejuvenate at 10-12 years.

Anti-aging facelift is recommended to eliminate the following age-related changes:

– Flabbiness and atony of the skin;
– Omission of muscles and fascia;
– Pronounced nasolabial folds;
– Double chin;
– Appearance of “bulldogs”;
– Sagging cheeks, etc.

After a competently performed plastic surgery, the contours of the face and its oval are restored, along with the age-related defects, external fatigue and “gloomy” facial expression disappear.

Types of surgical facelift:

Lift the lower two-thirds of the face allows you to remove wrinkles, make the removal of sagging cheeks, smooth nasolabial folds. Performed in this case, the correction of the cheeks also helps to get rid of the aesthetically unattractive “jawls” and “bulldogs.”

In elderly patients, this procedure is usually accompanied by a neck lift, eliminating wrinkles in the cervical-chin area and the “second” chin. Brow lift and upper eyelid “opens” the eyes, making look younger and clear, and forehead lift smoothes deep frown creases and transverse wrinkles.

In the course of an operation conducted under general anesthesia, the surgeon excises excess skin, removes excess fatty tissue, which as a whole allows you to return the soft tissues of the face to the initial position.

In order to obtain a more pronounced effect, the tension of the aponeurotic flap (SMAS technique) is often used. The surgery for facelift in Tampa lasts from 1.5 to 3 hours and, as a rule, requires the patient’s stay in the hospital.

Sutures are usually removed for 5-8 days, and a final evaluation of the result of facelift can be given after 2 weeks.

The technique of facelift procedure allows to minimize the rehabilitation period and achieve beautiful aesthetic results.

Qualified surgeons of the clinic have modern methods that allow to exclude negative postoperative results such as “stretching” of the lips, unnatural tension of the skin, the appearance of face asymmetry and so on.
