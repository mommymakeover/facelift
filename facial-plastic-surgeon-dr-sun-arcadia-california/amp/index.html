Is discoloration from Restylane permanent?
Four Ways to Minimize Discoloration After Restylane
May 30, 2010
1.  Minimize Brusing At All Cost:  relaxation, laughing gas, ice compresses, Arnica, avoidance of blood thinners

2.  Conservative Volume

3.  Deep Injection Rather than Superficial Placement

4.  Bleaching:  skin brighteners, bleachers, peels.  This does indeed take a while to work

Juvederm for lines and under-eye bags?
Fillers For Troughs, Not For Bags
May 30, 2010
Fillers work only for tear trough or nasojugal lines, not for bags themselves.

 

Shallow bags can improve with Thermage, and larger bags will need surgery.

Will Juvederm work to reduce marionette lines at corners of the mouth?
Degree of Marionette Lines Determines Type of Treatment
May 30, 2010
For shallow marionetter lines, fillers, especially hyaluronic acids such as Juvaderm, Restylane, Perlane, or even Hydrelle, alone or in combination, work well in this area.

For deep marioneet lines, however, surgical cheek lifting technique may be required.
