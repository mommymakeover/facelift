A Revision Rhinoplasty May Be Necessary For Alar Pinched Tip
Sep 28, 2020
Supra alar pinched tip is very common after old rhinoplasty where a large amount of cartilage was removed and therefore the nose collapses over time and pinches in. The solution for that is to replace that cartilage so it has the structural support and the cosmetic improvement at the same time.

Does revision rhinoplasty recovery take longer?
Revision Rhinoplasty May Take Longer
Sep 28, 2020
Revision rhinoplasty surgery always takes longer to recover, unless it’s a minor revision. The reason for a longer recovery time is because once you go back to an already operated nose and reposition the skin, cartilage and the bone, the second time around takes a lot longer for the nose to heal and come together. We always tell our patients that are considering revision rhinoplasty that they need to be more patient with the recovery time.
