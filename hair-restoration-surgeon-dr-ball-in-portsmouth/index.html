Care after hair transplant. Any suggestions?
Care after hair transplant
Feb 11, 2016
Now that you are over a month since surgery your grafts are well healed and safe. A healthy balanced diet is wise but supplements are largely unnecessary. It sounds like you have been on minoxidil & it would be wise to continue this, provided you haven’t been experiencing side effects. You may also be describing some increased shedding following the surgery (shock loss). The minoxidil can cause this in the early stages but once established should help to stabilise things. 
Propecia vs Minoxidil for women hair loss?
Minoxidil vs Propecia
Feb 11, 2016
Propecia is not licensed for use in women and most doctors would consider the side effect profile to be excessive in women. Minoxidil is very well established and has a much more acceptable side effect profile. Unfortunately, topical minoxidil can be rather an impractical treatment because patients can find it inconvenient and messy to apply. Its longterm compliance in women is therefore often poor.
