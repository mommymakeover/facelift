Hair Growth Timeline After Hair Transplant
May 24, 2021
The hair growth timeline after hair transplantation in Turkey is very simple. During the first two weeks, the transplanted hair will begin to shed. However, this is not a cause for worry. Regrowth will start shortly after and might cause folliculitis around the four-month mark. That too will fade away with time, and instead, brittle un-pigmented hair will replace them after four months of the surgery. The hair will begin to gradually strengthen and darken in color until around eight months…
How Long Does Hair Transplant Last?
Is Hair Transplant Permanent?
May 24, 2021
Yes, hair transplants are permanent. Some follicular units are resistant to hair loss. Hair transplants work by moving these resistant follicles to areas of hair loss. Transferring from one place to another does not affect their properties. Hence, growth in the regions that did not have it before will occur from there on.
