How many grafts would be required for my vertex hair loss on my crown?
Crown baldness and graft number to provide coverage
Mar 17, 2018
To estimate the number of grafts needed, we perform a high definition hair dermoscopy to better visualize the hair that surrounds the bald spot to see whether that area is thinning too (miniaturizing ). If so, then the graft count would reflect providing density in the area that is also continuing to thin. In general, 1250 to 1500 grafts would be needed to achieve a dense result for an area similar to your bald spot.
