If I go flat instead of getting implants is it just as easy to get an infection?
Infection risk with or without implant
Aug 13, 2021
If you decide to remove your implants, risk of infection will be lower than if you kept them. Risks of going flat could include increased risk of seroma (fluid collection) due to having a large open space that was once occupied with an implant. I only recommend you go flat if you are certain of your decision. Otherwise, if you were to later change your mind, starting over with reconstruction later may present further challenges. Best of luck with your decision.
