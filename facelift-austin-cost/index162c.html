Causes of aging face

Unfortunately, the age processes do not spare anyone, and over time significantly reduce the beauty of the human face, despite any physical exercises.

This is a very unpleasant event, because in our society the person’s face plays a very important role in communicative and social aspects. The facial skin begins to age rapidly after 45 years old (and in some cases even earlier).

And the first forerunners of problems appear much earlier, but for many years, small wrinkles can be successfully suppressed with light cosmetic procedures and good make-up.

Acupuncture Facelift Austin Tx

However, sooner or later the moment comes when the number of changes can not be easily corrected and requires surgical intervention, such as a facelift. What is the reason for such rapid biological processes?

Time promotes the overextension of elastin and collagen fibers, which are the basis of the dermis. The destruction of intercellular and intermolecular bonds in the deep muscular and neurological layer leads to the appearance of an excess volume that extends far outside, forming folds.

This is especially noticeable after 45 years of age. At first, the folds are barely noticeable, however they have a clear tendency to proliferation, and are not removed with the help of exercises. In parallel with this, there is an increase of the wrinkling on the skin, and a weakening of the normal trophism of the bulging sections of the dermis.

In turn, the imbalance in the nutrition of the tissues causes the appearance of unpleasant shades and a general flabbiness of the skin. All these problems can be solved only in a complex medical way, for example, with the help of facelift in Austin. They can not be eliminated at home. The reviews show, that facelift has significantly improved in the area of rejuvenation, while at a relatively low price.

Indications for surgical facelift, and patient preparation

Women and men begin to age intensely after 45 years of age, which is manifests in the appearance of numerous wrinkles and folds. In addition, the contours of the forms are smoothed out, which is especially noticeable in areas where the dermis are naturally thin enough – for example, on the face.

All these changes are accompanied by abnormalities of an aesthetic nature, which can only be eliminated by facelift.

Initially, various exercises from the field of physiotherapy, carried out at home, can partially quell the problem, but over the years the flabbiness of the skin is all the more increasing. In some cases of premature aging, similar cosmetic troubles can overtake a person in 35-40 years.

Features of the procedure

If no secrets of beauty, realized at home, did not help to rejuvenate the face, then the patient is recommended surgery, for example facelift. It should be noted that modern surgical facelift is a whole complex of various techniques that use different technologies to achieve a specific effect.

Dr Adam Bryce Weinfeld Lower Face Lift

It is not necessary to apply the entire set of cosmetic tools to a specific patient, available in facelift – everything depends on the operated area and on its aesthetic state.

As a rule, during the facelift, the following actions are performed:

  • Excision of excess volume of skin;
  • Tension of the upper layers of the dermis;
  • Strengthening the skeleton of deep muscle fibers by thermocoagulation;
  • removing excess fat.

If the patient’s age exceeds 45 years, then it is necessary to perform step-by-step all the elements of the facelift complex in order to achieve the desired effect. The total time of surgery can be up to 4-5 hours, while the patient is under the influence of general and local anesthesia, therefore, does not experience any significant painful sensations either during the procedure or during subsequent physiotherapy exercises.

Possible complications, and side effects

Facelift has flaws, which can be displayed in the form of negative consequences, especially if you perform a procedure at the age of 45 years. +
The most common complications of facelift are:

  • Increased swelling of the face in the first days after surgery;
  • Multiple small bruises;
  • Pigment spots (occur at the site of internal hemorrhages, or in patients with particularly sensitive skin);
  • hair loss in the areas of the circular incision.

As evidenced by the reviews, all of the above processes that develop during the facelift are reversible and under proper conditions pass by themselves, even at home.

The main thing is to follow all the recommendations that were prescribed by the specialist. Physiotherapy exercises, in the case of such conditions, should be performed with caution.

At the age of 45 years old or in the presence of problem skin, more serious facelift complications are possible, which, fortunately, are quite rare:

  • Occurrence of bleeding after surgery;
  • Necrosis of tissue in the zone of incisions;
  • Damage to the facial nerves;
  • Weakening of facial muscles.

The development of such conditions in such a procedure as facelift is associated with various factors – first of all, with the problems with the patient’s overall health and with the professional mistakes of the doctor.
