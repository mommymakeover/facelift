Facelift surgery

A facelift in New York is performed to rejuvenate the face and correct age-related changes. To achieve a complex rejuvenating effect, facelift is often performed simultaneously with blepharoplasty and liposuction of the chin. Aging of the face is an inevitable and constantly progressing process. Over the years, the skin of the face and neck sag, the contour is blurred, forming a “jawls” and a second chin.

Eyebrow line is lowered, there are excess skin on the upper eyelids, wrinkles and hernial bags, the corners of the mouth are omitted, expressed nasolabial folds are formed. The cause of facial aging is a change in the age structure of the skin – reduction in elasticity due to degradation of collagen, a slowdown in the division of skin cells, a decrease in water content and the accumulation of free radicals that damage cells. The impact of unfavorable external factors, primarily solar radiation, aggravates the age-related skin changes.

A good cosmetic skin care can slow but not stop the aging process. Aesthetic operations on the face, including facelift, are now effective and safe if performed by a qualified specialist. There is a tightening of the lower and middle third of the face (neck, chin, cheeks, cheekbones), and a tightening of the upper third of the face (eyebrows, forehead). Facelift can be superficial and deep. With a superficial facelift, only the skin separates and tightens. This operation is indicated for minor age changes of the face and the effect from it lasts for several years.

Deep facelift, the so-called SMAS facelift, was developed and implemented about 30 years ago and produced a real revolution in aesthetic facial surgery. In the process of SMAS facelift, apart from the skin, the muscular aponeurotic layer is allocated, which is then moved and fixed in a new position, providing a young facial contours. Deep facelift makes a noticeable and lasting results – even after 10 – 15 years, people will look much younger than he looked without surgery.

Indications:

  • Omission of the face contour, double chin, pronounced nasolabial folds;
  • Ptosis of eyebrows, eye corners, transverse wrinkles on the forehead;
  • Flabby skin on face and neck.

Contraindications:

  • Chronic diseases in the stage of decompensation;
  • Diabetes;
  • Thrombophlebitis of deep veins of lower extremities;
  • Diseases of the blood;
  • Infectious diseases.

Features of the facelift surgery

A facelift in New York state is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation is from 1.5 to 3.5 hours, depending on the volume (surface or deep facelift). The skin, and with deep facelift and the musculo-aponeurotic layer, are separated, moved and fixed in a new position. Excess skin is removed and neatly sutured. Techniques used for facelift, blepharoplasty and other aesthetic operations, and modern suture materials make it possible to make the postoperative scar absolutely imperceptible.

The postoperative period and the terms of recovery period.

Stay in the hospital lasts 1 – 2 days. Sutures are removed after 7 to 10 days. Edema and bruising take place within three to four weeks. During this time, you should limit physical activity, stay in the sun. Microcurrent physiotherapy is indicated for better rehabilitation. The sensitivity of the skin in the region of the temples and near the ears is restored throughout the year. The final results of the operation can be evaluated after 6 to 8 months.

Complications

Complications are almost absent when performing facelift surgery by an experienced surgeon. Any possible risks are assessed by the surgeon at the consultation and examination stage in each specific case.
