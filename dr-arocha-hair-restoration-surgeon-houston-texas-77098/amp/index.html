PRP after transplant: Is it possible to damage transplanted follicles with PRP injections
PRP after FUE
Aug 22, 2019
It is unlikely that PRP done properly would damage transplanted grafts through a prior FUE procedure. The needle is small and injected to a subdermal or superficial depth. The PRP may induce a temporary shed or effluvium. Recovery should be within a few months and hair may come back stronger than it was originally. Good luck!
