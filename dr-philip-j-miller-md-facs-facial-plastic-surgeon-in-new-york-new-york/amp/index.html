A deep plane facelift will last over 10 years
Jul 12, 2022
Hi, there! Judging from your photos, I don’t believe you are the best candidate for a deep plane facelift.
The early signs of aging are sagging of the brows, hooding of the upper lids, drooping of the cheeks, deepening of the nasolabial folds, and formation of jowls. If lifting is desired, do a lift. But for a young person, a traditional deep plane facelift with cutting in front of the ear and around the temple hairline is not worth the trade-off. You may be a good candidate for a micro…
