How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Oregon?

Facelift in Oregon is one of the most popular and effective plastic surgery, aimed at rejuvenating the face. Typically, facelift is combined with endoscopic forehead lift, blepharoplasty, platysmoplasty and liposuction to achieve a pronounced “young face” effect.

Experiments on surgical tightening of the skin of cheeks doctors began to carry out at the beginning of the twentieth century.

Then the whole operation was reduced to “cutting wrinkles” (rhytidectomy) at the skin level. Modern methods of facelift appeared in the 1970s and 1980s.

In the 21st century, aesthetic results after facelift surgery meet the requirements and wishes of both professional doctors and even the most demanding patients.

The purpose of a facelift is to eliminate the age-related deformations of the middle and lower third of the face and make the face look like it was in the youth of a man.

Facelift In Portland Oregon

Indications for facelift:

  • Sagging skin in the lower third of the face (“jawls”);
  • Nasolabial folds;
  • Deep wrinkles in the middle and lower parts of the face.

Facelift in Oregon is not an alternative to mesotherapy, laser resurfacing, injection contour plastics, peeling and other ways to rejuvenate and refresh the skin. These cosmetic procedures are an excellent addition to the results of facelift and serve as an excellent remedy for removing fine wrinkles on the face, pigmentation, rosacea and various skin irregularities.

Young face relief: the essence of the facelift technique

Face lift in Oregon is a major plastic surgery that can be performed only in a specialized clinic, in the operating room with a full set of necessary equipment and a hospital for 3-7 days of the patient’s stay. A plastic surgeon must have experience and even a talent for performing such operations.

Plastic Surgery Of Face By Doctor Richard H. Bensimon

Modern plastic surgeons performed facelift surgery by the method of SMAS lift. The operation is under general anesthesia and lasts about 3.5-5 hours.
SMAS facelift is performed at muscular level: tightened musculoaponeurotic layer (abbreviated SMAS, hence the name of the method), located under the skin of the patient face.

The surgeon makes the incision that starts a few centimeters above the ears, then continues in the direction of the lobule along the natural contour of the front edge of the ear, passes under the lobe and ends under the hair behind the ear. After this “mobilized” (i.e., separated from the skin), tightened and fixed in a new position SMAS, excess fat removed (method of liposuction) are excised and excess skin. At the final stage, the skin is straightened, and seams are applied without tension (in the future this guarantees a neat, unobtrusive scar, hidden in the hair and natural folds of the skin).

Recovery After Facelift In Medford Oregon

It is worth noting that a facelift can be preventive (that is, ahead of the age-related changes in the facial contours). In this case, a facelift is performed after the appearance of a slight sagging of the skin. Taking into account the indications, there is not one continuous, but several small incisions (in the hair and near the ears).

At the same time, the amount of work of a plastic surgeon (SMAS facelift) is not less. After the preventive facelift, the face of the patient will noticeably freshen, but there will be no bright difference (“Before” and “After” the operation). The effect will be evaluated after years, during which the face will retain his youth and beauty.

Features of the rehabilitation period after facelift.

Face lift surgery requires a long period of postoperative recovery. In the first week on the face there are multiple bruises. Asymmetric swelling can distort facial features and make the patient’s facial expression difficult. However, bruising and swelling are a normal part of the recovery process after surgery.

Sutures are removed gradually: on days 4-5 – the seams located in front of the ears, on 9-10 days remove the seams behind the ears, and the seams under the hair – for 11-12 days. The healing process lasts for several weeks, and for a full recovery it will take 1.5-2 months. Scars after facelift take about six months to become less noticeable to outsiders. During the recovery period, plastic surgeons and cosmetologists recommend to patients undergo cosmetic procedures aimed at solving three problems:

  • Restoration of normal blood circulation;
  • Normalization of lymphatic circulation;
  • Improvement of the quality of scars.

For this purpose, magnetotherapy, ultrasound therapy, microcurrent therapy, laser therapy, mesotherapy, SPA-capsule for face “FaceSystem” and others are recommended. Correctly selected complex of cosmetic procedures for skin care during this period will shorten the terms of recovery.

The results of facelift:

Facelift effectively restores the “young” contours and face volume, as well as clear, relief and expressive outlines. At the same time, the natural facial expression is not violated and the patient’s habitual expression does not change. A decade after surgery, the patient will still look 10-15 years younger than his biological age.

A professional approach to the facelift, the original focus on the final aesthetic result is a guarantee of success and safety of plastic surgery!
