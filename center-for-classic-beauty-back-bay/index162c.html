How is SmartGraft different from regular FUE?
SmartGraft FUE Transplant
Apr 23, 2021
Thank you for your inquiry! SmartGraft is an FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) Transplant. SmartGraft uses a small round punch to extract follicular hair units one at a time. The hair is taken from an area of the scalp that is resistant to balding and placed in the areas that are experiencing hair loss. The old method of performing hair transplants is the strip method, which tends to leave patients with a large linear scar on their head. With SmartGraft’s FUE Transplant there is no linear scar.
Can you have EMsculpt with a Kyleena IUD?
EmSculpt with an IUD
Apr 23, 2021
Thank you for your question! EmSculpt uses highly focused electromagnetic energy, similar to an MRI, so it is not recommended for anyone that has any metal implants or copper IUDs. Based on our research, Kyleena is a plastic IUD with nylon threads and therefore you would be able to receive an EmSculpt treatment with a Kyleena IUD.
