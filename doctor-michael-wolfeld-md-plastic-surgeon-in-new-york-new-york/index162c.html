Are there some people that have a reaction to hair grafts and their skin will not accept them? Is there a way to test for this?
Apr 19, 2018
Since a hair transplant is an autograft (transplanting follicles from one part of the body to another) it will not lead to grafts being rejected. However, if you have a type of alopecia called scarring alopecia (uncommon scalp disorders which can permanently damage the hair follicle and replace it with scar tissue) then transplanting hair in this scenario can lead to loss of grafts. Does every clinic use the same method to prepare PRP?
Does every clinic use the same method to prepare PRP?
Apr 19, 2018
There are a wide variety of protocols when it comes to PRP treatments. Some physicians combine the PRP with additives, such as Acell, or combine the treatment with microneedling and dermarollers. The type of PRP centrifuge and the frequency of injections also vary among practices. At Bernstein Medical, we use the Emcyte Pure PRP System which provides a double centrifuge technique which seems to optimize the concentration of the platelets.
How effective is PRP?
How effective is PRP?
Apr 19, 2018
With telogen effluvium the shed hair should return on its own, so PRP would most likely be a waste of time and money. I guess it is possible that PRP may stimulate quicker regrowth but, to my knowledge, there are no scientific studies to support this. PRP seems to have its greatest effectiveness in reversing the miniaturization due to androgenetic alopecia but even in these cases the response is modest.
