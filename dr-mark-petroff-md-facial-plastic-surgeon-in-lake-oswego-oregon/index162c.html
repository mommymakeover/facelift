Any suggestions on the color in mustache area after Juvederm?
Bruising after Juvederm injection
Aug 18, 2015
It is very common to bruise after a Juvederm injection and it should subside shortly. Before your next injection – try discontinuing products such as aspirin, fish oil, etc that are known to thin the blood and increase the chance of bruising. A product called Arnica Montana can be taken immediately following injections as well to help with decreasing the chance of bruising. Try to stay patient as the bruising subsides.Mark Petroff, M.D., FACS
