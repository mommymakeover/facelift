How is performing breast surgery on tuberous breast different to a regular breast augmentation?
BAM in tuberous breasts
May 27, 2021
Thank you for your question. A tuberous breast has a narrow base. It can variate
from mild to severe. For an optimal result, an effort should be made to widen the base of the breast during augmentation. This will depend on the degree of tuberosity. Also, the type of implants to be
chosen must be considered accordingly. Seek expert consultations with board-certified plastic
surgeons in your area. Best wishes.
