What is the least scarring face lift? Can it all be hidden in the scalp?
Facelift scars.
May 31, 2016
Great question! The best place to place the scars are behind the ears in the retro auricular groove, and behind the tragus of the ear, since this will hide 100% of the scar. Some surgeons make it anterior to the tragus and this is a mistake because this scar will be very visible. A small portion if the incisions are made in the scalp and this is done in a way that it does not destroy the hair folicules, so it will not kill those hairs, and it will hide perfectly this portion of the scar….

What is the best (and longest-lasting) facelift technique to lift heavy jowls?
Best Facelift technique
May 31, 2016
The Best and more lasting Facelift technique is the SMAS Facelift because it lifts the SMAS and with it all the muscles, skin, and tissue that falls down with age. An extended SMAS or deep plane facelift is dangerous because it can damage the Facial nerve that moves all the muscles in the face, so I would avoid that if I were you. With the regular SMAS acelift you can rejuvenate your face from 15 to 20 years in my experience. Hope this answers your question. Have a good day.
