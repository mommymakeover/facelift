How long should I wait between CoolSculpting?
CoolSculpting Same Area
Jul 22, 2019
The protocol is always different in each office but I would check with your provider. Typically, you can treat the same area of CoolSculpting as soon as 1 month after your first treatment. Congratulations!
CoolSculpting- What is the most sessions you have witnessed/heard about and was it successfu
CoolSculpting Sessions
Jul 22, 2019
CoolSculpting is a great body contouring treatment. Patients typically get 2-3 treatments in the same area over time. Not everyone is a candidate for the procedure so the best thing is to have an evaluation to see how many attachments are needed.
Coolsculpting or Clatuu? I have stubborn decade fat on lower abdomen and upper arms.
Upper Arms
Jul 22, 2019
You would have to have an in office consultation to see if you are a candidate for the procedure. Arms could be a tricky area because CoolSculpting does not tighten the skin in the treated areas. Pro-lipo and/or brachioplasty have better results. 
IF you only do CoolSculpting on the lower half of your stomach will it also help the top?
CoolSculpting Abdomen
Jul 22, 2019
This depends if your upper and lower stomach is appropriate for CoolSculpting. Some patients have more volume on the lower abdomen than the uppers. Which would make sense why your doctor would recommend treating the bottom. After 3-4 months when the fat is reduced the stomach may be more even. It's hard to tell without any photos or evaluation the area. If you have questions regarding your treatment plan, you should follow up with your doctor about treating your whole abdomen.
What treatment would you suggest to tighten my neck?
Tighten Neck
Jul 22, 2019
CoolSculpting will not work on your neck. CoolSculpting targets fat cells and does not tighten the skin. You would benefit from a necklift. Please schedule a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon to talk about both non-surgical and surgical options to see which procedure suits you. 
Will resurfacing create keloids?
Keloids and Laser
Jul 19, 2019
If you have history of keloids, you may not be a candidate for resufacing. I would recommend having a consultation with a laser expert to evaluate if you can have resurfacing. There are alternatives to lasers. 
Why did Juvederm filler under eyes produce irregular contour and how can I fix it?
Filler
Jul 19, 2019
You should have a consult with the doctor first before dissolving anything. The treatment plan could always change depending on the evaluation. You could also benefit from some more filler in your mid face, that's where we lose fat in the face first. 
How to get my whole face smooth using Dysport?
Laser Treatments
Jul 18, 2019
Dysport is injected in the muscles. These injections will not work for all of the areas of your concern. Lasers may be an option if you are looking for a non-surgical treatment. Please schedule a consultation with a laser expert to talk about different treatments to improve your skin. 
Aside from Botox, which cosmetic treatments/procedures would you recommend for a 50 year old
Lasers
Jul 18, 2019
Have you tried lasers to improve your skin tone and texture? You should schedule a consultation with a laser provider to talk about different laser treatments. Lasers could improve your collagen and soften fine lines. Chemical Peels are great as well. They are more superficial compared to lasers so it's common to have a series of treatments. There is a little downtime with this treatment depending on the intensity.
Am I a good candidate for mommy makeover with breast lift/augmentation, tummy lipo, and new
Mommy makeover
Jul 8, 2019
You are a great candidate for a mommy makeover. You could have a large enough implant to avoid getting a lift. However, if you don't want to bigger than a breast lift would be better. A tummy tuck will take care of the excess skin and the belly button could be revised at the same time. You're petite already, you may not need liposuction of the flanks.

Halo Laser
Jul 8, 2019
The Halo laser is safer for dark skin types but the the best thing is to have an in office evaluation with a laser expert to discuss expectations and treatment plan. Treatments for acne scarring normally takes more than one treatment.

Surgery vs CoolSculpting

How to get rid of redness, pigmentation, and dry dull skin?
Dull Skin
Jul 1, 2019
It's very important to establish a good skin routine at home. You should  start on retinol/retin A which is applied at nighttime only. This will be part of your anti-aging routine. Redness in the skin is common and could be a genetic component. BBL or photofacials treatments could help redness redness in the skin. This is not a permanent treatment and will need maintenance. The sun and free radicals contribute to aging quicker. Wearing sunblock everyday will prevent pigmentation.
