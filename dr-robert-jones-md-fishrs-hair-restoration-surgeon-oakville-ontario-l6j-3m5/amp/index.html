How many grafts do I need for my crown?
How many grafts for crown?
May 7, 2021
A crown area typically takes a lot of grafts to create coverage, usually 40 to 50 grafts per square cm.
Measure the area and multiply by 45. My guess is that you will need in the range of 2500- 3000 grafts.

Can a hair transplant be done in this scar area ?
Hair Transplant into scar
Feb 24, 2021
Hi,
Hair can be transplanted into scar tissue the same as it can be planted into normal tissue. Occasionally you may see incomplete growth if the blood supply is less than adequate, but usually patients are very happy with results. Visit any experienced hair transplant physician for further information,
Regards,
Dr. Robert Jones

Should I be shedding small hairs on finasteride?
Shedding on Finasteride
Feb 10, 2021
Hello,
It is normal to shed 50 to 100 hairs daily even if you don’t have Male Pattern Baldness. After 5 months on finasteride, your hair loss should stabilize and no further hair loss should occur. I would suggest you continue the finasteride, benefits will continue for years to come.

Will I need to take finasteride to keep my own hair I have left or should I just leave it?
May 9, 2019
I recommend that patients take finasteride around the time of their transplant even if they don’t want to take it long term. This will help prevent the shock loss of their existing hair.
