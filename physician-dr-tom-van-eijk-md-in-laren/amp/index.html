Botox Spock brows I am going back for another dose tomorrow and was wondering should I ask for the inh
Those extra injections are not needed when the frown is injected correctly
Feb 11, 2019
The ‘Spock’ Sign comes from placing the medial injections too high, resulting in a relative relaxation of the medial fibers of the muscle that raises the eyebrows, As a result, the fibers on the side will work harder when you try to lift your eyebrows. Correct placement will prevent this from happening.

Since the level of the eyebrows is determined by muscles pulling them up and others pulling them down, correction of drooping eyebrows can be possible by weakening downward pulling muscles. Please find an experienced practitioner to do this.
