Will female hair transplant give me more hair overall?
Hair Transplant Does Not Produce More Hair.
Aug 28, 2023
Not many women are a candidate for hair transplant surgery. Depending on certain type of hair loss, some women can be a candidate for hair surgery. Hair transplant in the frontal are tend to be more successful than the crown area, especially with women hair loss. Once the hair have been harvested from the donor area, it will not grow back. There will not be more overall hair after hair transplant surgery. All we do is relocating the donor hair to the recipient area.
Currently on Finasteride, how many hair grafts would I need?
Combining both Medication and Surgery will give you the best hair result
Aug 28, 2023
Medication like Finasteride or Rogaine can be effective for hair loss treatment like in your case. From your photos, I believe you may need around 2,000-3,000 hair grafts depending on how much balding you have underneath the canopy of long hair. The best hair result is from combining both Medication and surgery. Doing a consultation with the hair transplant surgeon is the best option to determine how much hair you needed.
FUE / Neotech hair transplant after sinus artery thrombosis, complications?
Hair Transplant after Sinus Artery Thrombosis
Aug 28, 2023
Hair transplant whether by FUE or FUT does not associate with increase arterial thrombosis. You should be a candidate for hair transplant surgery.
