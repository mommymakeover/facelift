How much tention is placed on the inguinal space during a body lift? Can a hernia happen in you are pul
Can a lower body lift cause a hernia?
Jun 3, 2019
Great question. If the abdominal wall is intact, without any pre-existing weaknesses, it would be unlikely that a lower body lift would cause a hernia. The most important consideration is how much visceral fat is present. If there is significant intra-abdominal fat, tightening of the abdominal muscles could create enough intra-abdominal pressure to create a hernia in a weakened area. I would discuss these concerns with your plastic surgeon.

2 weeks post op and still pretty sore with muscle spasms frequently, is that normal?
Is it normal to have muscle spasms and pain 2 weeks post-op?
Apr 23, 2019
Thanks for your question. This not unusual at all. Patients may have varying amounts of pain in the immediate post op period. This usually resolves fairly quickly. However, as the initial pain resolves, patients become more active and some will develop muscle spasms as the result. Decreased use of the pectoralis muscle will help and sometimes muscle relaxants may help as well. This usually resolves after a few weeks.

Can a Tummy Tuck incidentally fix a slight tilt uterus?
Can a Tummy Tuck fix a tilted uterus?
Mar 1, 2019
A Tummy Tuck will tighten the abdominal muscles and loose abdominal skin. It may improve posture and core strength. The lower abdominal bulge we see after having children is due to thinning and separation of the rectus muscles. When you stand up, the intra-abdominal contents exert the greatest pressure in the pelvis and lower abdomen, creating a bulge. A tummy tuck probably has no effect on the position of the uterus.
