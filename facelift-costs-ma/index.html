Facelift Costs Massachusetts

Facelift in Massachusetts is one of the most popular aesthetic operations to date. After all, the face is the part of the body, open to the views of others, and therefore it is most difficult to hide defects on it.

Many negative factors postpone their imprint on the face, including heredity, fluctuations in body weight, eating habits and lifestyle in general, bad habits, stresses, ultraviolet irradiation, and many others.

And this, in addition to the processes of natural aging and the action of gravity, as a result of which the tissues gradually descend.

As a result of all these influences, the cheeks sag, the eyelids become heavier, the nasolabial folds become deeper, the face oval is pertest to be clearly delineated.

Not least they are interested in the cost of facelift, as well as new methods of conducting such operations. Our clinic is ready to offer you a full range of procedures included in the extensive concept of “facelift”, conducted by experienced surgeons.

A special place among them is the Aptos thread lift, with the help of which the specialist moves and tightens the facial tissues without serious surgical intervention. For today it is the least traumatic and at the same time highly effective method of tightening soft tissues.

Massachusetts facelift is aimed at removing excess skin from the face, and also, when combined with liposuction, adipose tissue. Deep facelift means tightening of the muscle frame of the face through the plastic surgery of the superficial muscular-aponeurotic layer (SMAS facelift), due to which the face acquires a new, improved contour.

Indications for facelift:

  • “double chin”;
  • Strongly deepened nasolabial folds;
  • omited outer corners of the eyes;
  • Sagging skin on cheeks, eyebrows, forehead.

It should be noted that surgical facelift is less often performed as a circular one. Most often, 2/3 face lifts are used, which includes the middle and lower third of the face, as well as the neck.

Today there are three types of facelift:

  • Lifting the upper part of the face (forehead, and brow lift, as well as the temporal region);
  • Middle part of the face (tightening of the buccal-mandibular area);
  • The lower part of the face (neck lift).

These operations can be performed separately or together. Recently, endoscopic facelift is becoming more common, implying correction of facial defects through small incisions, which makes the result more natural, reduces the risk of complications and shortens the rehabilitation period.

Do not forget about the contraindications to the operation. They include severe diseases of internal organs, infectious and oncological diseases, and clotting disorders.

The cost of a face lift in Massachusetts depends on the prepared facial area and the technique used. Approximate prices for facelift and other plastic surgery you can see in our price list or check with our administrators, but the final amount will be available only for consultation with a doctor.

Facelift surgery technique.

During facelift, the location of the incisions and the subsequent technique of the operation depends on the condition of the patient’s face, as well as on the surgeon’s preferences. When lifting the middle and lower part of the face (facelift the lower 2/3) of the skin incision begins in the temporal region, extends in front of the ear and the natural pleats, rounding the earlobe finishes in retroauricular region in the scalp, that allows virtually do postoperative scars imperceptible.

Liquid Facelift Massachusetts

Further, the facial skin is separated from the underlying tissues and the plastic surgery (SMAS facelift) of the superficial muscular-aponeurotic layer is performed, followed by excision of excess skin.

With the facelift of the upper face area, the skin incision begins in the temporal region, continues up the scalp and ends in the opposite temporal region, which makes the incision invisible to the eye.

Next, a detachment of facial tissues is made in the temples and forehead areas (more often subperiosteal), and after stretching the skin, its excess is excised. Endoscopic facelift performed with inconspicuous incisions, most of which is hidden in the scalp. The operation is performed using special (endoscopic) equipment.

Anesthesia.

Facelift is usually performed under general anesthesia (endotracheal anesthesia). In some cases, local anesthesia with intravenous sedation is used.

Duration of the operation

Lifting of several zones of the face usually lasts 3-5 hours. Isolated forehead lift or neck lift last 1-1.5 hours.

Postoperative period and recovery.

The early postoperative period after facelift is accompanied by temporary painful sensations and edema of the face, usually passing for several days. Maximum swelling appears at 2-3 days. You must follow all the recommendations of your doctor to accelerate the recovery period and reduce the risks of complications in the postoperative period. You can return to everyday life with some restrictions 10-12 days after surgery.

It is necessary to wear special compression underwear for 5-7 days. The final physical activity is recommended after 4 weeks. It is necessary to refrain from visiting the bath and solarium, drinking alcohol and smoking. We do not recommend sunbathing for several months. The result of a face lift can be assessed on average after 3 months.
