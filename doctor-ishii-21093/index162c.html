Can you have PRP for hair loss and Botox at the same time?

Thank you for your question. Yes, you may have PRP Injections as well as Botox injected at the same time. You may experience some swelling after your PRP injections but that should not affect your Botox injections.

Necklift-Platysmaplasty
Nov 15, 2018
The bruising from a Neck lift (Platysmaplasty) varies per patient but typically the bruising last around 2-3 weeks. With this procedure we would make a small incision under your chin as well as around the ears.
