Insecure about the bump on my nose.
Nasal bump/hump removal
Dec 18, 2016
Thank you for your question. Surgery would be the most conventional and long-lasting way to correct the bump on your nose. That said, a non-surgical option would be to use injectable fillers to camouflage the bump (i.e. juvaderm, radiesse, etc.). That method has some drawbacks. The result would not be permanent as the fillers will absorb with time and it may have the undesired affect of making your nose look a little bigger overall.
