Indents bulbous tip TRICKY CASE ( my surgeon was great). Should I consider a revision or filler?
Revision rhinoplasty
Jun 18, 2021
The “indents” are a normal anatomic area of the nose referred to as the soft tissue triangle. The size and shape of this area is determined by the size, shape and orientation of the lower lateral cartilages. The lower lateral cartilages can be controlled with a combination of sutures, grafts and partial excision in rhinoplasty. Filler would not improve this much. You are better off having revision surgery to correct the domal angle, caudal excess and hanging columella.
