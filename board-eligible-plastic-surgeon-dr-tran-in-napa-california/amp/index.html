Thin skin?

You have very thin skin with very little elasticity That means your breasts will "sag" faster than most peopleThe heavier the implants the more they will pull on the skin and cause sagginess heavier When you lay down, the weight of the implants will fall to the side and down, that's normal gravityThis constant pull enlarged the pocket in which the implants sitSO this is normal but will only get worse You will need a breast lift at some point in the near future
