an answer to your question
Dec 4, 2018
There are risks with all surgery. However, your lipoma (if indeed that’s what it is) will not go away on its
own. Often they get larger with time. Risks are infrequent but exist. They include the most common
such as, bleeding, scarring, continued pain and on and on. I suggest you Google this and have an in
person consultation. While rare, virtually anything you can imagine can happen at the time of surgery.
