Pulling under chin after Corset Platysmaplasty. When will it loosen up?
Swelling, lumps and tightness after corset platysmaplasty
Nov 3, 2014
Different surgeons have varying approaches to performing a Corset Platysmaplasty. It is not unusual to notice some firm areas, swelling and pulling for several months after surgery on the plastysma. Your concerns are understandable, and most of the time they will resolve as the area continues to heal and settle. Your surgeon is your best resource as far as what to expect for your particular situation, and there may be some options to help things settle a little sooner. Best regards.
