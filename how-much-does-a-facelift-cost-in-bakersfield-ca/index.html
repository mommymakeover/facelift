A simple skin-cervical-facelift does not affect the deep muscular structures, because consists only in tightening the skin tissue.

The operation is fairly simple and provides an easy and fast recovery period, but the result is not stable, because tightening the main body tissues occurs of the main array of tissues do not occur. This operation is indicated for patients with thin skin and in the presence of many fine wrinkles.

The SMAS facelift technique in BakersfieldÂ involves intervention in the muscular structure and is the most popular facelift surgery. SMAS facelift involves incisions in the area of the auricle: in front of it and then along the antilobium, the lobe and the behind-the-back fold. During the operation, the surgeon exfoliates the subcutaneous fatty tissue, moves the muscular structures, lifts the muscular-fascial layer and fixes it in a new position.

Full facelift allows you to fix all the soft tissues of the face in a new position, which gives a strong anti-aging effect.

If the age changes affect only the upper part of the face, then the upper third of the face is lifted: the forehead, eyebrows and the temporal region. The operation is performed through two small incisions in the scalp. With the help of endoscopic technique and special tools, soft tissues separate and fix in a different position.

For local correction of the sagging zones of the skin, sectoral mini facelifts are indicated, which are performed endoscopically through incisions in the region of the ears, temples, along the edge of the lower eyelid or in the oral cavity.

These operations are characterized by low traumatism and rapid rehabilitation, as well as a lasting result.

In preparation for the operation, I always ask the patient to quit smoking at least 2 weeks before the operation and within 2 weeks after it. Smoking significantly slows down and worsens recovery because it makes it difficult for blood to enter the blood vessels.

Facelift is accompanied by combined anesthesia or endotracheal anesthesia. After facelift surgery, you need to stay in the clinic for one day, and full recovery will take from two weeks to two months, depending on the method of performing the operation.
