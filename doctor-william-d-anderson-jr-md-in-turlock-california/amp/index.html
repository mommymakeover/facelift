Is it normal for my eye to bleed after eyelid operation? 4 days post op.
Postoperative bleeding
Apr 10, 2018
Persistent bleeding after surgery is uncommon. Bleeding after four days without swelling is likely from the surface of the incision or wound edge. There is usually no concern if the bleeding stops and does not lead to pain and swelling. You should seek medical attention right away if there is rapidly developing pain, swelling and bleeding or bruising. Bleeding into the wound could lead to pressure on the eye and possible vision problems.
I have a fractured nose and I was told to go back to remove the cast within 10 days?
Fractured Nose
Apr 25, 2017
It takes time for the bones to mend and gain strength. Bone healing can take two weeks for good stability and 6 weeks for the full strength. I usually leave the cast on for at least 6 days, then re-tape the nose for another four days. Most likely you will have the cast removed at your ten day visit. Hope this helps.

Botox and Microdermabrasion
Apr 25, 2017
I usually recommend avoiding massage or pressure to the area for several hours. However, some authors of published articles recommend waiting one to two weeks before getting facial or facial massage. Applying pressure or massaging the area may cause an undesirable spread of Botox.
