Traveling and Recovery Time for Mommy Makeover
Jun 7, 2017
Thank you for your question.  Following a mommy makeover most people can return to normal work functions after seven to ten days.  However, you may still have drains that will need to be dealt with.  We tell all of our patients that the full recovery will take approximately three months.  We discourage cosmetic surgical tourism if the patient is not going to remain in the area for at least two weeks post-operatively.

Thanks for your question. I do not recommend ice or heat to the abdomen, as the dissection often causes a decrease in sensibility and the skin can be damaged.  Ice to the breast can help with swelling the first couple of days after surgery but at this point is unnecessary.
