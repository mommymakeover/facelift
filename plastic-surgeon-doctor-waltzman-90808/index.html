I’m breast feeding my 3 month old, how long do I need to wait before I can get my breast done?
Breast Feeding before surgery
Feb 20, 2018
My rule is that patients need to be completed with breast feeding about 6 months before having breast surgery.  The reason for this is that the breast undergo a lot of changes after you stop breastfeeding.  It makes sense that you would want your breasts to completely settle so that you get the best results after your surgery.  If you have surgery too soon, your breasts may undergo normal post breastfeeding changes and you may end up with a sub optimal result.  Best of luck!

How to find the best supportive bra while still healing after a BA?
Bra after augmentation
Nov 12, 2017
I actually prefer that my patients wear a soft bra after augmentation.  I recommend a soft post op bra, a loose fitting sports bra, or a camisole top.  I typically don’t recommend anything very tight because we want the implants to be able to “drop” into place and not stay too high on the chest.  Post op protocols vary between surgeons, so the best advise is to call your surgeons office to ask specifically what they would like you to do after surgery.  Best of luck!
