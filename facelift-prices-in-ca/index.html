Time for a facelift procedure?

A facelift procedure is the best choice when the patient and/or the plastic surgeon feel as if they have exhausted the non-surgical techniques available including Botox and Filler Injections. It is also when the patient feels they look tired and their face and/or neck is sagging.

This usually happens in the early to mid-fifties depending on the individual patient. However, everyone ages differently depends on how the individual patient ages chronologically as well as social indicators such as smoking , stress and sun exposure, etc.

When Facelift Is A Good Choice For You

A facelift procedure in California is a great choice for a healthy individual who is seeking to have the lower face and upper neck repositioned to look younger and more rested. Many patients first consider non-surgical options such as injectable fillers and botox to achieve a more youthful appearance.

When these treatment methods fall short of the desired goal, one might consider facelift surgery to help turn back the hands of time.

Facelift Recovery

Believe it or not, facelift recovery is easy and hassle-free as long as you follow doctor’s recommendations as dutifully as you possibly can. Usually, after the surgery is done and the anesthesia has worn off, that is the time where you will be appropriately discharged from the hospital. It is important to note that you do need someone to drive you home.

It is usual and normal for patients to feel a bit of discomfort after a cosmetic surgery. But do know that this slight discomfort is insignificant and will wear off in a few days or weeks. Plastic surgeons usually prescribe medication to help patients deal with any surgery-related pain. Make sure though that if you are prescribed with any medicine that you take careful and cautious look on the meds’ side effects or of any adverse reactions.

As much as possible, be extra vigilant. If you do notice any slight allergic reactions, it is best to notify your doctor as soon as possible so a different and non-allergic med will be prescribed.

The doctor may also recommend to elevate your head days after the cosmetic surgery. This is to assist in keeping the swelling significantly down.

You may also notice that your body feels a lot more comfortable if your movements are minimized for at least the first few days or weeks after the surgery. This is your body telling you that it needs to recuperate and save energy to concentrate on healing.

Any numbing feeling is also normal. The general feelings usually come back after a few weeks and complete numbness disappears in a few months.

Take note that you should also be vigilant for any complications that may occur. One of these is swelling that appears suddenly. Though swelling is generally normal, what you should pay attention to is swelling that starts suddenly. Persistent or severe pain must be noted and be notified to the doctor immediately.

Any signs of a hundred degree fever indicate an infection. Stitches that have popped must also not be taken for granted. Discharge that is abnormal on the site where the incision was made must be thoroughly checked by the doctor.

All in all, it is best to see the doctor for necessary post-operative care to achieve immediate facelift recovery.

Before undergoing facelift surgery, it is advisable that you cease smoking for 3 weeks before and 3 weeks after the procedure. Nicotine has been shown to constrict small blood vessels, negatively affecting blood supply to the healing tissue.

Reduction of blood flow to the surgical site is indeed potentially damaging. It can reduce or slower healing of the surgical procedure, cause blistering of the skin, or in the worst cases, actual loss of tissue. If you have either stopped smoking very recently or have been unable to stop completely, you must accept these risks if you wish to proceed with undergoing facelift surgery.

If a problem occurs after surgery, Dr. and his staff pledge to care for you in resolving the problem to the best of their abilities. It is a necessity that you are truthful with us about your smoking habits.
