What is a Weekend Facelift?
What is a Weekend Facelift?
Jun 18, 2017
A "Weekend Facelift" is simply a less involved mini-facelift or sometimes a variant using suspension sutures. It refers to needing very little recovery or 'down time'. A mini-facelift may be ideal for some people, but the suspension suture techniques have had complications and typically only last a few months. I would recommend seeking consultation with a board certified Plastic Surgeon with many years of experience and with whom you feel comfortable.  
Is 40 Too Young for a Facelift?
Is 40 Too Young for a Facelift?
Jun 18, 2017
40 is not "too young" for a facelift in some cases. The real determinate are your anatomy and your goals. There really is no "Best Age" for a facelift. The "Best Time" is when the patient is dissatisfied enough with their appearance to seek surgical changes. Many people wait until there are significant signs of aging, while others opt to have smaller procedures earlier and more frequently to maintain a more consistent look. The latter seams to be a more popular choice.
