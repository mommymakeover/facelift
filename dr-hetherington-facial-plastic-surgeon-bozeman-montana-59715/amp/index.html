Fat grafting from ankles/calves/knees to breasts?
Not enough fat to be worth transferring
Oct 29, 2014
Liposuction of the ankles, calves, and knees will typically not yield enough fat to be worth grafting into the breasts. It is unlikely that the small amount of fat removed from these areas will make any noticeable volume enhancement of your breasts.

Asymmetric eyes after eyelid surgery. Should I be concerned?
Not to Worry
Oct 13, 2014
I wouldn’t worry about it at this point. Post-operative swelling and bruising often affects each eye a little differently and it’s not unusual to look more asymmetric in the early postoperative period. Your degree of asymmetry usually improves to a very acceptable level within a few weeks. That being said, it’s important to understand that some degree of asymmetry is normal. No one is perfectly symmetric before or after plastic surgery.
