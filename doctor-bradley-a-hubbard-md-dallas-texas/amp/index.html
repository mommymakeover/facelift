Statement: Based in Dallas, Texas, Dr. Bradley A. Hubbard, MD is a board-certified plastic surgeon who’s been in practice for eight years. He is focused on providing all his patients with outstanding care and natural-looking results. He specializes in body contouring, mommy makeovers, and breast enhancements.

Dr. Hubbard takes a straightforward, down-to-earth approach with his patients that sets him apart from other plastic surgeons. “In addition to their results,” he says, “I think this is one of things my patients appreciate about me the most.”

He is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS) and Dallas Plastic Surgery Institute.
