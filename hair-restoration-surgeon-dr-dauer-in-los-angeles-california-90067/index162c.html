How long after FUE hair transplant can I start taking Finasteride & Minoxidil?
Finasteride/Minoxidil post-op
Jun 15, 2023
I recommend not stopping finasteride at all with a hair transplant and stopping minoxidil for 1 week prior to the procedure and then re starting 2 weeks after the procedure
Can a cowlick be fixed by FUE?
Cowlick
Jun 15, 2023
I typically try to de-emphasize a cowlick when I am performing a hair transpant. Since the surgeon controls the angle and direction of the hair it is possible to do so.
How common is hair transplant deterioration after a few years?
Longevity of transplanted hairs
Jun 15, 2023
The transplanted hair should not fall out unless there are other medical issues casing this. Usually the deterioration of hair is from continued loss of native hair. This loss of native hair can be slowed down by using minoxidil and finasteride either topically or orally.
Eyebrow transplant on scar – Will I see results and will the hair grow correctly?
Eyebrow Scar
Jun 15, 2023
Yes transplanted hair will usually grow through a scar. It will have a lower growth rate than with healthy tissue, but a 50-60% growth rate through a scar can be a reasonable expectation.
How long does it take for swelling to reduce after an eyebrow transplant?
Swelling after procedure
Nov 3, 2017
Typically after an eyebrow transplant there is significant swelling and bruising around both eyes for approximately 4 to 5 days. Some individuals have slightly less swelling and bruising and some slightly more, but this is the average.
My eyebrow hair is long. If I get a transplant, can I grow the implanted hairs to the same length? (Phot
Scalp hair for eyebrows
Nov 3, 2017
Typically the hair for an eyebrow transplant is taken from the scalp. Because of that, the hair transplanted to the eyebrow maintains its color and character from the scalp. That also means that it will continue to grow exactly like scalp hair. After an eyebrow transplant using scalp hair, you can keep the eyebrow hair as long or short as you wish.
Using an eyebrows pencil after eyebrows transplant?
Pencil eyebrows Post -op
Nov 3, 2017
After two weeks post eyebrow transplant it is OK to use eyebrow pencil or powder. Regular facial cream may also be used in the eyebrow area after two weeks post procedure.
