malar bags 5 weeks post op
Oct 30, 2019
Do not be concerned as this is likely norma post operative swelling. It often takes 3 months or more to resolve, so be patient and stay in touch with your surgeon please.

Nose reduction and chin surgery
Oct 30, 2019
Ivan, you can achieve a much better profile balance from appropriate reduction rhinoplasty along with alar base narrowing, and that alone may obviate the need for chin augmentation. Computer imaging with your rhinoplasty surgeon would  be most helpful in consultation. 
Can alar rim grafts be removed?
Alar rim grafts.
Oct 30, 2019
Of course alar rim grafts can be surgically removed, but the question is whether you need them in the first place. Photos would be helpful, but as an alternative seek a second opinion from a board certified FPS or PS rhinoplasty expert.
Could I have a closed rhino or will a nose filler look good with my nose?
Rhinoplasty vs filler to improve nose.
Oct 30, 2019
I would advise strong consideration for rhinoplasty, as much more can be accomplished with permanent results. Fillers would mask the bump on your dorsum but only make the nose look too big anyway; and they are temporary! I advise consultation with a proven Rhinoplasty expert. You can discuss important aesthetic changes that you desire, such as reducing the dorsal hump, and others.
Would a facelift or a thread lift smooth out Sculptra bumps?
Bumps from Sculptra injections.
Oct 30, 2019
Neither procedure would be helpful, unfortunately. These are nodules of collagen, and may respond to needling to break them up, as well as injections of 5 FU and steroid mixed together. Multiple treatments may be needed, but they eventually shrink over time. Good luck!

Would I get the rhinoplasty result I desire?

Fat transfer vs repositioning vs fillers
Aug 20, 2019
All of the options you have been presented with are reasonable, but understand the surgeon will recommend the procedure that works best in his hands, most often.
