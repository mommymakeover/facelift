Nasal valve collapse – how long should I wait for a revision?
Revision Rhinoplasty
Nov 6, 2023
Hello! Thanks for your post and photos. I would agree with waiting toward 12 months before considering a revision. The nasal obstruction likely won’t improve, but as swelling comes down, other issues may need to be addressed. There are techniques to help open the nasal passageway so don’t feel like this is necessarily permanent. I would continue good follow-up with your surgeon to evaluate the surgical plan. If needed, seek consultation with others. Hope this is useful.
