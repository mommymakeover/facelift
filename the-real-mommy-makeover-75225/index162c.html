Name: Wesley Anne Brady, MD
Statement: Dr. Wesley Anne Brady is a board-certified gynecologist surgeon with nearly 20 years of experience. As the founder and medical director of Women’s Wellness Institute of Dallas, Dr. Brady has performed more than 3,000 cosmetic vaginal surgeries and helped thousands of women improve their sexual health.

She offers a comprehensive variety of cosmetic vaginal surgeries, including vaginoplasty and labiaplasty, as well as vaginal rejuvenation treatments like Votiva, ThermiVa, MonaLisa Touch, and the O-Shot, alongside bioidentical hormone regimens.

Dr. Brady takes pride in creating a safe space for women to explore and improve their sexual health. She personally tries all of the nonsurgical procedures that she provides through the practice, in order to better guide her patients in their decision-making process.

“While many surgeons offer vaginal surgery in addition to breast augmentation, liposuction, rhinoplasty, and other elective procedures, my practice specifically focuses on cosmetic gynecology,” says Dr. Brady. “I was the first doctor in North Texas to open a practice devoted to cosmetic vaginal surgery, entirely focused on enhancing our patients’ sexual health and well-being.”

Dr. Brady earned her medical degree from the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center in Dallas. She remained in the city to complete a residency as a gynecologic surgeon at Baylor University Medical Center. She later studied alongside Dr. David Matlock, a pioneer of laser vaginal rejuvenation and designer laser vaginoplasty, as well as Dr. Red Alinsod.

Dr. Brady serves as the president of the Laser Vaginal Rejuvenation Institute of Dallas. She is a full fellow of the American Board of Obstetricians and Gynecologists and an active member of the Texas Medical Association, the Dallas County Medical Society, the American Academy of Cosmetic Gynecologists, and the International Society of Cosmetogynecology.
