Facelift Surgery Philippines

Facelift in Philippines is a tightening of the muscles and skin of the face – youth, extended from 2 to 12 years, there is no alternative to the facelift.

Mini facelift restores a certain part of the face: forehead, cheeks or eyelids. With full facelift, the tissues of the temples, cheeks, chin, and neck are tightened at the same time.

The operation lasts up to 4 hours under general anesthesia. The first dressing is done in 3-5 days, the stitches are removed after about 7 days.

Operative edema on average decreases after 15-20 days, physical activity is contraindicated for 1.5-2 months.

The facial swelling also disappears within 2 months.

The procedure is performed in three ways:

  • tightens the skin of the face only, the effect persists for 2-4 years;

What is a facelift?

Facelift in Philippines involves an isolated or complex surgical treatment of the problem areas the skin of the face and neck. You may be offered an isolated lift of the forehead or eyebrows, lift the middle or lower third of the face, neck lift, the chin lift. Facelift is also achieved with the liposuction of facial areas, in which there is an accumulation of excess adipose tissue (cheeks, chin).

Types and methods of facelift surgery

A rejuvenating operation is also a chin lift. These operations can be carried out in complex depending on the indications. The essence of facelift is what the surgeon moves to a new position (usually up) and fixes soft tissues that have undergone ptosis.

Modern methods allow to achieve not only skin tightening, but also correction of the muscular aponeurotic system of this area (SMAS facelift). Today, these methods allow to guarantee to all patients an excellent result for a long time.

Facelift Manila Philippines

Previously applied facelift methods assumed the main effect on the skin, and the tension load applied to the seam area, reducing the effect time from the procedure.

Now, SMAS facelift allows the entire load to be distributed evenly over the superficial muscular aponeurotic system, and this provides postoperative scars, practically invisible to the outside eye and, last but not least, the duration of the effect up to ten to fifteen years.

All facelift operations are performed under anesthesia, which makes it as comfortable as possible. The first time after facelift surgery may cause swelling of soft tissues of the face and neck, small areas of bruising. However, these symptoms quickly pass if you follow all the medical recommendations.

Recommendations for the patient after facelift, neck lift.

Age: from 35 years old.

Preoperative measures:

  • Strictly on an empty stomach (do not eat, do not drink 4 hours before surgery).
  • Without makeup.
  • With clean hair.
  • Hair coloring, bleaching should be done before surgery and hair coloring is not desirable within a month after surgery.

Postoperative measures:

  • A tight bandage is applied for 48 hours.
  • In the first day to apply a cold compress for several minutes with a break of 30-40 minutes.
  • Lie in bed with a raised head.
  • You must comply with bed rest 10-12 hours, then you can get up, walk.
  • With pain and before going to bed take analgesics (analgin) + sleeping pills.
  • Diuretics are prescribed for the treatment of pronounced edema.
  • Do not take a bath (do not wash your hair, do not swim) 11 days, after use only light shampoo, easy make-up.

    • Macs Facelift Philippines

  • The dressing is done after 2 days.
  • You can not erase, clean, lift weights, play sports, run, jump!
  • Seams in front of the ear are removed on the 4-5th day. On the scalp for 7-10 days.
  • The sensitivity of the skin of the cheeks and neck improves gradually during the week.
  • Resumption of normal activities for 10 days.
  • Avoid sunlight for a month.
  • Scars in the beginning are easy to hide under the hair, after a few months they completely disappear.
  • After the operation, the patient looks younger for 10 years and the result persists for 7-10 years, and sometimes longer, depending on the patient’s skin and care.
  • The final result is evaluated after 6 months, when the scars heal.
