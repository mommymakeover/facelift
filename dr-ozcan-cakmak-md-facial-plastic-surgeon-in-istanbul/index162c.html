Best Facelift Procedure for a Smoker?
Safest facelift techniques for smokers
Oct 4, 2020
In deep and composite facelifts, the flap is undermined as a single unit -including skin, SMAS and malar fat- which provides excellent blood supply to the overlying skin. So, deep and composite facelifts carry much less risk of skin necrosis especially for smokers compared to all other facelift techniques having long skin flap dissection.
