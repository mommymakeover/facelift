How easy or difficult is it to accidentally move the wrong way and rupture internal stitches?
Rupture internal stitches?
Nov 12, 2018
It's not easy.  You would really have to twist your neck quickly and fully.  Doing some yoga poses too soon after surgery may pop a stitch.  Trauma to the area may rupture a stitch as well.  If a suture would break you may feel or hear a pop.  Also, if a suture broke you may see some relaxation in the area. Follow-up with your surgeon as soon as possible to assess the situation.
