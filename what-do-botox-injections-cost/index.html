What Do Botox Injections Cost?

In general the cost of botox injections to treat wrinkling will be determined by how much you need and can range from $400 to 1500. Ther are three FDA approved botulinum toxins in the USA: Botox Cosmetic, Xeomin and Dysport.

Most offices charge more for Botox Cosmetic and slightly less for the equivalent dose of Xeomin or Dysport. Some offices charge by area, others by unit, but I advise you not to shop around by price.

Instead pick the most qualified physician for the procedure. Remember you are not ‘buying’ the Botox, you are paying for the procedure. (Heidi A. Waldorf, MD, New York Dermatologist)

Nationally, most providers will charge anywhere between $10-16 per unit of Botox injections, with the national average being around $13 per unit.

The price of Botox is contingent really on two factors: 1) the price per unit of Botox; and 2) the number of units required to achieve the look you want.
