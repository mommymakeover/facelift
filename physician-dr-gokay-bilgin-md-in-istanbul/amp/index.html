Does finasteride ever just stop working or slowly lose effectiveness?
SWITCHING FINASTERIDE TO DUTASTERIDE
Jun 20, 2023
While finasteride can be effective for many individuals, it’s important to note that individual responses to the medication may vary. Some people may experience a gradual decline in effectiveness over time, while others may find that the medication continues to slow down their hair loss for an extended period.
In certain cases, finasteride may not work as effectively or may appear to stop working altogether. In this case you can switch to dutasteride without losing your hair.

HAIR GROWTH AFTER HAIR TRANSPLANT
Jun 15, 2023
Hello, it is normal that transplanted hair follicules will grow out weaker with less density at first but you will see that they will get thicker and stronger by time. You will start to see actual results after 6 months of hair transplant.
