Facelift Prices In Mexico

Skin aging is inevitable and inexorable. The tissues lose their elasticity – small, and then large wrinkles appear, the skin hangs.

You can reconcile with the age-related changes in the face or resort to the services of a plastic surgeon and turn the clock back.

Facelift and neck lift in Mexico

Facelift and neck lift is one of the most popular plastic surgeries, designed to eliminate or reduce the obvious signs of aging. Indications for its conduct are any age changes that you would like to get rid of – wrinkles on the forehead, over the bridge of the nose and at the root of the nose, “crow’s feet” around the eyes, bags under the eyes, lowered eyebrows, deformed facial contour and so on.

Dr Juan Carlos Fuentes Smas Lifting Patient

To restore self-confidence and sympathy for your reflection in the mirror will help operations performed using the technology of endoscopic facelift and other modern methods:

  • Neck lift;
  • Lifting of the middle zone of the face;
  • Circular facelift;
  • ligature lift via APTOS threads;
  • SMAS facelift

The doctor will help you choose the most appropriate type of face-lift depending on the desired results. Sometimes it is necessary to develop a whole complex of operations and procedures, allowing to achieve the best effect and keep it for a long time. So, often facelift is accompanied by liposuction of the cheeks and chin.

Endoscopic facelift

One of the most effective methods of facial plastic surgery is endoscopic facelift. This technology does not involve the removal of excess tissue, but consists of pulling them from the problem area to the unobtrusive areas.

Face And Neck Lift By Doctor Alejandro J. Quiroz

So, for example, the lift of the middle zone of the face allows you to move excess skin from the corners of the mouth, the angle of the lower jaw and the lower eyelid to the temporal region, in the hairy part of which small incisions are performed. An open facelift method involves making incisions in the area in front of the ear where visible scars may remain.

Endoscopic facelift is possible only with the availability of expensive medical equipment, so it is not performed in every Mexico clinic. The use of endoscopy can also make surgery less traumatic, reduce the risk of complications and reduce the rehabilitation period.

SMAS Facelift

SMAS facelift is a tightening of deep face structures used as an addition to other types of lifting to achieve the most stable result. SMAS is a fascial layer covering the muscles and nerves of the face. This type of facelift allows you not only to tighten your skin, but also to strengthen the original frame on which it is located. This is the reason for the effectiveness and durability of the result of the operation. But SMAS facelift surgery can only be entrusted to a highly skilled surgeon with extensive experience.

Aptos Thread Face Lift

Is a ligature plastic reconstruction or skin lift by intradermal thread. Is a worthy alternative to traditional plastic surgery. This type of facelift allows you to achieve a significant rejuvenating effect without complex surgical intervention and implant application, to improve the contours of the face and body. In this case, the result is observed immediately after the end of the operation, and scarring is not at all.

Strengthening the contours of the face or body with the help of APTOS threads allows to remove the need to address traditional methods of aesthetic surgery or is used instead of them for those patients who for various reasons can not resort to classical facelift.
