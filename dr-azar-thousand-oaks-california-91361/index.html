Kybella or chin liposuction?
Kybella vs. Liposuction
Jul 30, 2017
Without photos, it is hard to give you any kind of accurate recommendation. Liposuction is the gold standard for fat removal, but some patients either don't or can't tolerate surgery or the downtime that is associated with surgery. For them, Kybella is a great non-surgical option. It is extremely effective for the right patient but will take multiple treatments. 
Kybella for bra fat. What are the risks?
Kybella for bra fat
Jul 30, 2017
While Kybella is only FDA approved for under the chin, it is often used "off label" to treat the bra fat and is very effective. It will probably take more than one session so keep that in mind. The risks are minimal but you can expect to have some bruising and swelling following your treatment. See a really experienced injector to be assessed in person and discuss. 
Is a second treatment with Kybella a good option for lax skin left after first treatment?
Kybella not ideal for treating loose skin
Jul 30, 2017
Kybella does a terrific job eliminating excess fat, but it does not tighten loose skin. I would encourage you to refrain from getting another Kybella treatment before being assessed in person by a board certified plastic surgeon. You may need a non-surgical skin tightening procedure such as Profound RF or a surgical neck lift.
