Vartan Mardirossian, MD

Name: Vartan Mardirossian, MD
Statement: Dr. Vartan Mardirossian, MD is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon in Northern VA serving the Washington DC metro area. Dr. Mardirossian is best known for his work performing Facial Feminization Surgery, including rhinoplasty and other facial surgeries, deep place face lift, neck lift, facial implants, buccal fat removal and blepharoplasties.

After graduating summa cum laude with his bachelor and medical degrees from the University of Padua in Italy, Dr. Mardirossian completed his professional training at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Boston University.

He is board certified by both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. Dr. Mardirossian is the assistant professor of facial plastic and reconstructive surgery and otolaryngology at Florida Atlantic University School of Medicine.
