Facelift Cost New Jersey

Facelift in New Jersey is a kind of surgical intervention, which involves tightening the face and neck skin, as well as removing excess skin and fat.

The stress of everyday life, sun rays, genetic factors and gravity is a contribution to the changes that we see in the mirror when we age.

There are wrinkles, crow’s feet in the corners of the lips and eyes, on the forehead the lines turn into deep folds, cheeks drop, grooves in the nasolabial folds are formed, the muscles of the face and neck become weak.

If you want to regain youth and beauty of your skin, if you want to feel again beautiful and desired, the specialists of our clinic will be able to help you by selecting the optimal methods of facelift for you at an affordable price.

Doctor Jeffrey B. Wise Before And After Facelift

Indications for surgery

  • the formation of wrinkles in the area of cheekbones, temples, forehead;
  • the appearance of deep nasolabial folds and the “second” chin;
  • sagging skin and wrinkles in the neck area;
  • ptosis cheeks lower edge of the mandible;
  • drooping eyelids, mouth corners and outer corners of the eyes;
  • the appearance of wrinkles in the area the bridge of the nose;
  • the formation of deep vertical wrinkles on the cheeks.

Types

There is a lift of the upper zone (forehead lift), the middle part of the face, the lower one (the cheek and neck areas). Operations can be carried out either separately for each area of the face, or in combination.

Depending on the patient’s needs, the degree of age changes and the preferences of the plastic surgeon, one or another method is chosen which will be the most optimal choice for solving your particular problems. The most popular for today are:

Anesthesia

In most cases, it is performed under general anesthesia, but some operations can be performed under local anesthesia with a mild intravenous sedation.
Duration facelift surgery ranges from 1.5-2 to 3-4 hours. The patient is in the hospital about 1 day.

Preparation for surgery

One week prior to surgery, it is necessary to stop taking alcohol and blood-thinning medications (aspirin, hormones). 5-6 hours before surgery, you can not eat or drink anything.

Face Plastic Surgery With Dr Mark Glasgold

The recovery period lasts from a week to a month. You can return to work in 2 weeks. The final result can be estimated in six months.

The swelling of the tissues and bruises subside after a few weeks. Make-up can be applied after 10 days. Sutures are removed in some zones for 3-5 days, in others for 11-12 days.

Scars disappear after 3-4 weeks and 6-12 months are quite noticeable.

After operation

Wearing a special compression mask takes about 10 days. During sleep try to raise your head higher than usual. This helps to get rid of fluid and edema faster. It is not recommended to comb your hair for 10 days.

Do not strain, abstain from the load, but regularly go out for a walk, this increases the healthy blood circulation. During the month, a solarium and a sauna are contraindicated.
