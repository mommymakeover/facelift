Is a full facelift needed?
Facelift

For the best result yes.  Lifting the neck along with the jowl and cheek as one unit would be the ideal way to go about your treatment plan.  The brow and eyes would also need to be evaluated but from the photos provided I would suggest a full facelift.

Breast lift/reduction

Can a surgeon sculpt the lower abs and pubic area into a nicer shape?
Scultping the body
Aug 8, 2017

What’s the best procedure for my body?
Liposuction
Aug 8, 2017
My suggested would be SAFE Liposuction of the circumferential trunk and arms.  While non-surgical treatments exist none come close to a surgical result.  If you do decided to go with non-surgical cool-sculpting has good data to support.  However, the best result would be surgery.
Is the recovery for SAFELipo different from other types of lipo?
SAFE Liposuction
Aug 8, 2017
The recovery from SAFE Lipo is comparable to that of traditional liposuction. Recovery deals more with the volume and location of fat removal.
Implant exchange after 14 years- will I need a lift?
Mastopexy (breast lift)
Aug 8, 2017
The ideal surgical plan will likely include exchanging your implants and performing a breast lift.  The capsule around your current implant can be used to lift your implants but then the nipple and loose breast skin will need to be repositioned/tightened.  This is why the lift will be important for you to accomplish your goals.
Do I still need symmastia repair if I’m removing my implants?
Symmastia
Aug 8, 2017
You should not need the symmastia repaired if you are removing the implants.  If there is abnormal contour in the midline this may need to be corrected.  This can happen from irregularities of the pectoralis muscle.
I am planning to have a rhinoplasty 2 months before a wedding, is that enough recovery time?
Rhinoplasty recovery
Aug 8, 2017
You may still be slightly swollen at 2 months but most patients at week 2-3 are ok for social events.
