Facelift Surgery In Dubai

Years pass, muscles under the skin lose their elasticity, facial skin becomes flabby, wrinkles appear, and the most high-quality creams and masks help only for a short period of time.

Facelift in Dubai is a unique and effective method of tightening sagging skin of the face and neck, removing and preventing deep wrinkles, allowing to remove fat folds in the area of the face under the chin.

It should be noted that facelift is not only a surgical intervention, but also a set of exercises to prevent skin aging, or correcting problem areas in the initial stage of their formation.

Plastic surgeons, improving and changing the methods of facelift technology, have reached a level, that allows to perform such procedures with a minimum number of small tissue incisions, which makes facelift almost safe and painless operation.

In addition, modern plastic surgery performs a number of operations not in a complex manner, but corrects separately each of the problematic facial zones.

Recommendations for indications for performing facelifts in all plastic surgeons are approximately the same:

  • The patient’s age is 40-60 years;
  • Presence of clinical and laboratory examination of the general state of health;
  • Quality indicators and skin condition at the time of surgery;
  • It is clear that an aesthetic surgeon will be easier to work with the skin of a 40-year-old patient, still saturated with moisture and retaining elasticity.

The main reasons for refusal to a patient in facelift can be:

  1. Chronic heart and vascular disease;
  2. Diseases of the lungs and bronchi (in the stage of exacerbation);
  3. Functional features of the kidneys and liver;
  4. An allergic reaction to medications;
  5. Inflammation near the vascular tissues of the face;
  6. The course of radiotherapy in the face area.
  7. Obesity could be added to the list of contraindications, but it should be noted that this disease is rather not a contraindication, but one of the risk factors that can affect the undesirable and unsatisfactory result after the operation.

Preoperative period

During the first visit to the surgeon, it is necessary to correctly formulate your wishes and listen to his approach and recommendations for solving problems. As a rule, studying the face and structure of the skin, as well as drawing attention to the structure of the skull, a qualified physician immediately give competent advice, drawing attention to the way he is going to address the shortcomings in what sequence, and what else recommends.

Assessed risk factors such as poor blood clotting or uncontrolled blood pressure jumps. In addition, the need to inform about the courses of taking medications and taking alcohol and tobacco products, relieve unwanted consequences after surgery, or the troubles that may arise in its process.

The main recommendations of the surgeon are, as a rule, refusal from smoking and hair growing, to hide the scars behind the auricles after the procedure.

As it was said before, in the preoperative period the plastic surgeon determines what exactly will be corrected during the operation, and which problem areas of the person will be paid special attention.

Doctor Allen Rezai Facelifting Picture Results

Therefore, for each patient facelift passes differently, and, therefore, the duration of the operation for each patient is different. Facelift lasts about an hour and a half. And, however, on average, the duration of this procedure is from 2 to 3 hours.

The surgeon performs longitudinal incisions from the auricles to the parietal part of the head. The skin begins to exfoliate and gradually tightening it, the doctor fixes it in a new position. This procedure gives a positive result even in the very adult age of the patient, when age changes and skin aging are very visible. After a facelift, the skin is fixed, cosmetic sutures are applied, a bandage is applied.

Recovery period

The postoperative period is characterized by some discomfort in the face and neck area. There is swelling, bruising. The average period of rehabilitation is 20-25 days. On the 10-16th day, seams are removed, and the so-called “shrinkage” of the skin begins.

Dr Afschin Ghofrani Face Lift Tape

It can occur for several months, so do not rush to conclusions about the quality and need for the operation. The patient is observed in the clinic under the supervision of doctors during the day.

Noting the inconvenience to the patient during the postoperative period, attention is drawn to sleep. During sleep, the head should be slightly elevated.
As rule, facelift in Dubai is not a problematic procedure, that in a week the patients begin to apply makeup, after 14 days they can go to work, and a month later they begin to live a full life.

How long the effect of the operation will last depends on the state of health, heredity, and, of course, the way of life.

Facelift is facial rejuvenation with the help of gymnastics.

Dr Maria M. LoTempio Cosmetic Face Surgery

Facelift includes not only surgery and stay in the clinic. Facelift is also a gymnastics of a face that patients can easily master and regularly perform at home.

Regular exercise makes it possible to achieve an incredible effect, provided that this is the initial stage of the process of loss of tissue elasticity.

Facelift is gymnastics, as a rule, contains some basic types of exercises:

  1. Exercises to increase the eyes;
  2. Removal of puffiness under eyes;
  3. Prevention of wrinkle formation;
  4. Charging the muscles of the face with energy;
  5. Narrowing of the nose;
  6. Shaping the contour of the lips.

However, if there are significant problem areas, that require special attention and an individual approach, the complex of facelift exercises can be adjusted and fed individually for each of these zones.

When choosing facelifting in Dubai as an operative intervention, or as a complex of exercises for the face, it is always necessary to remember what goals are being pursued, and what result should be achieved. In any case, trust only competent, trusted plastic surgeons, relying on their experience and existing achievements.

The need for a re-operation occurs after 5-10 years, while you should not forget about massage, ointments, laser skin rejuvenation. These procedures will help considerably to postpone the repeated visit to the clinic of plastic surgery of the face.
