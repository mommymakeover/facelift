Does sunscreen cause Frontal Fibrosing alopecia?
Recent study finds that sunscreen does not trigger Frontal Fibrosing Alopecia (FFA)
Sep 24, 2023
Thank you very much for the excellent question. A recent cross-sectional study in 2022 found that FFA is not triggered by sunscreen use. But rather, patients with FFA were found to have higher levels of actinic damage, possibly explaining their increase use of sunscreens. So, in other words, people with FFA tend to use more sunscreen but the increase in sunscreen use does not appear to be the cause of their FFA. I hope that helps.

Is there any risk to having a hair transplant procedure 3 weeks after rhinoplasty?
No additional risk
Sep 12, 2023
Hi, there is no additional risk by having your hair transplant done 3 weeks after rhinoplasty surgery if it is done via the FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation) method. Unlike the FUE method, you do not need to be in a face down position at any point during the FUT surgery. This, however, might be a restriction if you plan to have your transplantation done via the FUE method though.
