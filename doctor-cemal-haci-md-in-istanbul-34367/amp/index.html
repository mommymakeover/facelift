What’s the best approach in fixing my nose, open/closed?
Open or Closed Approach
Jun 10, 2022
The most important feature of African American noses is that they are thick-skinned. In patients with thick skin, I choose open aprroach as my opinion. Because we get good results with techniques such as skin scoring and supratip suture for thick skin. These techniques are very useful to increase nasal tip definition. An open aprroach would be more appropriate for your nose in my opinion.
Cemal HACI, Otorhinolaryngologist, Istanbul/Turkey
Can I still do rhinoplasty after Bellafill on my nose 5 years ago?
Do filling applications affect the result of rhinoplasty surgery?
Jun 10, 2022
There is no harm in performing rhinoplasty after the elephant injection. With pre-operative injections, these elephants are dissolved and the remaining elephants are resected during the operation. Not all elephants can be resected in those who want to have closed surgery only. But when the open approach is made, there will be no problem.
Dr. Cemal HACI, Otorhinolaryngologist. Istanbul/Turkey
