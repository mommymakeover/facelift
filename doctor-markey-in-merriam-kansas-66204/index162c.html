Why are noses overly rotated up during/ after rhinoplasty?
Rotation after rhinoplasty
Jan 16, 2019
Great question - controlling the precise rotation of the tip after rhinoplasty is essential to getting a great result. No matter the technique, once the surgery is complete gravity has an effect and the rotation descends to some degree. Depending on the approach this may be a barely discernible amount to a large amount. Because of this, the nasal tip is often rotated higher at the conclusion of the procedure so that it will settle in the first 6 months to the ideal position.
When Did Swelling Go Down After Your Rhinoplasty Surgery?
Rhinoplasty recovery
Jan 16, 2019
The long recovery after an open rhinoplasty can be frustrating. The cast placed during the first week keeps the swelling down and the cartilage in place but once removed the nose expands. The swelling may go up and down as the weeks pass and you return to regular exercise and routine. It takes 6-12 months before you are able to clearly see the results.
