I Had Upper Eyelid Surgery 5 Weeks Ago and Still Have a Small Purplish Mark on my Cheek Bone, Wi
Eyelid Surgery Bruising Fades with time
Jul 22, 2012
Bruising and swelling from eyelid surgery is always at its worst for 2-3 days and mostly fades in 2-3 weeks. However, it takes at least 2-3 months to fully heal if not longer. Since the muscle around the eye is a circle, bruising from upper lid surgery always tracks down into the lower lid and cheek. And therefore it is often the last place to finish healing.
Will 6 Sessions of 20% Glycolic Acid Peel Reduce my Severe Dark Circles and Will 30% Be Safe?
Dark circles
Mar 24, 2012
Dark circles under the eyes have several causes, including shadowing from puffiness above, pigment in the skin and vascularity below the skin surface. Glycolic peels are safe at both 20 and 30% in trained hands, but typically improve skin texture and won't do much for most dark circles. Better to have someone assess your eyes to see why you have dark circles and treat them appropriately.
Botox Cosmetic- How Does It Work on Wrinkles?
Botox works by temporarily weakening muscles
