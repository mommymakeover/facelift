Vollure/Juvederm Vycross. Can I use these same fillers again?
yes you can use them again
Feb 21, 2021
Dental surgery is probably the most common cause of delayed filler reactions/infection in my experience. Probably what happens is bacteria enter the blood stream during the dental procedure and then hide in the filler like a biofilm. I would recommend melting ALL the old filler before you inject more so as to make sure there is no biofilm left. These delayed reactions are rare so you should be fine if you avoid more dental procedures.
Is it possible to have my nose de-projected without lifting the tip?
yes it is possible
Feb 21, 2021
The reason you often see the angle raise with deprojection is that a common technique to achieve de-projection is lateral crural overlay where the lateral crura are cut and overlapped…this causes increased rotation (tip up) as it deprojects. You can use tip sutures to minimize the rotation caused by lateral crural overlay, or perform a more intermediate crural overlap where the cut is closer to the dome (tip). Find a good, experienced rhinoplasty surgeon and you should be fine.
