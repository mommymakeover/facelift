Can stress during the first week of recovery damage my new grafts and affect the result?
Stress and Hair Transplant
Dec 16, 2021
In general, stress is one of the reasons which can cause hair loss. But in the case of hair transplant, the survivability of grafts may not be affected by the stress immediately before the surgery. Although, it may lead to an increase in blood pressure which will make the surgery more difficult. So, it is better to go for surgery in a cool, calm way to get the best of results

Is smoking cigarettes really going to ruin a hair transplant?
Smoking and Hair transplant
Dec 16, 2021
Smoking causes vasoconstriction of the vessels which usually affects the blood supply of the scalp. You should consider yourself lucky if you have got good results despite smoking. However, if you continue to do the same, the phenomenon of vasoconstriction will keep on continuing and it may lead to affecting the results in the long term.
