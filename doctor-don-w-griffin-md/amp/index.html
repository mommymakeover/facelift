How does the breast width diameter size affect the choice of implant size?
Breast base width and Implant size
Jun 10, 2022
Breast base width is one of the factors used to determine maximal implant size. There are other factors involved such as amount of native breast tissue and the tightness of your skin. Putting an implant that is significantly wider in base width than the breast width can lead to an unnatural look and create multiple problems. Implant base width does not have to match the breast base width meaning you can choose a smaller implant size and still get a beautiful and natural result.
