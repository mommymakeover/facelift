She is board certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology/Head and Neck Surgery.

Due to her vast experience with Rhinoplasty, Blepharoplasty and Facelift procedures, Dr V. is often sought by colleagues and patients for some of the most complex cosmetic cases in the DC metro area.

She continued her training in Internal Medicine at the University of California and returned to the University of Minnesota for her residency training in Otolaryngology (Head and Neck Surgery). Dr. V was then selected for a Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery Fellowship at Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center, Shreveport, LA.
