Can taking finastride for long term be fatal?
Long Term Finasteride usage
Jul 21, 2019
I would continue with finasteride at the recommended dose of 1mg/day as long as you are having no side effects. Any medication can cause an allergic reaction such as swelling, trouble breathing, anaphylaxis and death, but this is usually an immediate type of reaction to a new medication. You have already been on the medication for several years with no problems or side effects, so you should continue taking the medication.
