Am I suitable for a nostril reduction?

Rhinoplasty for wide nose?
Rhinoplasty
Nov 21, 2023
You have two options. One simple option would be t just narrow the nostrils which takes less than an hour under local anesthesia. If you want your entire nose narrowed a full rhinoplasty including breaking the bones to narrow them would be required
How many cases of fat embolism have there been after fat transfer?
Fat transfer
Nov 21, 2023
Fat embolism is much more of a concern with fat injections in the buttocks. The down side of fat transfer to the breasts is that most of the fat does not survive. I have answered many realself questions from women complaining that all the fat went away after a year
Facelift, fat transfer, or filler?
Facelift at twenty five.
Nov 21, 2023
You are too young for a facelift. Fat injections will make your face heavier and probably won’t last anyway. I think you need fat removed from your face. Buccal fat removal is an option

Rhinoplasty
Nov 21, 2023
To me the most noticeable problem is that you have a hanging columella ( the part between the nostrils) this is easily correctable with a minor procedure. I would not recommend any more steroids

Facelift
