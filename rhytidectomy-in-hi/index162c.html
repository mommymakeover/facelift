Rhytidectomy In Hawaii

With age, facial skin under the influence of various factors (due to gravitational ptosis, age changes, the formation of excess fatty deposits) is subject to aging processes. On the face there are wrinkles, the face oval loses the contour, becomes indistinct, diffuse, fatty platens, the skin sags, becomes flabby, uneven. These problems are eliminated by the facelift surgery in Hawaii.

The optimal age for this operation is 45-65 years, when there are the following indications for it:

– ptosis of soft tissues of cheeks;
– “jawls”;
– deep vertical wrinkles of cheeks;
– deep nasolabial folds with fat hanging over them rollers nasolacrimal furrow;
– “double” chin and pronounced wrinkles and creases on the neck
– omitted the outer corners of the eyes.

A Face Lift Patient With Doctor In Honolulu

The facial plastic surgery is carried out in different ways. You can just tighten the skin, you can tighten with simultaneous resection and fixation of SMAS (subcutaneous muscle-aponeurotic system), endoscopic facelift, Cheek Lift

The consultation discusses the advantages and disadvantages of each method. Like any other operation, facelift requires jewelry skill and high qualification from the surgeon, a good aesthetic taste.

Do not be afraid of scars! When a facelift performed by the endoscopic method, the incisions are in the scalp or on the mucosa side, which makes them invisible to the surrounding. With a checklift, the incisions are made according to the natural fold of the upper eyelid and the ciliary edge from below. After a while, the scars become white and become indistinguishable.

CheckLift is characterized by a short recovery period, an opportunity to perform an operation under local anesthesia. It is possible to perform a tightening and fixation of the outer corner of the eyelids, to shift the complexion of the cheeks muscles upwards, to obtain clear cheekbones, visual smoothing of the nasolabial folds, and to the younger skin around the eyes. People who value their time and the advantage of a beautiful young appearance choose a qualified literate specialist who uses the latest innovations in cosmetology medicine, remain attractive, successful and in demand.

The first day after a facelift.

You are on a stationary basis under the supervision of a plastic surgeon and nurses. At this time, it is important:

  • comply with bed rest, lie on your back, do not make any sudden movements. The head end of the bed can be raised slightly to make the position comfortable;
  • Do not change the position of the dressing yourself and especially not remove it;
  • Try to maintain a physically and psychologically calm state;
  • Take medication as prescribed by the doctor, in particular, analgesics and antibiotics;
  • Not to violate the sparing diet recommended in the first day and the following days;
  • ask for help if you have any sensations beyond the characteristic of the first postoperative day.

First month after a facelift.

Dr. Randy Wong Face Plastic Surgery

In the absence of complications and complaints from your side, you will be discharged from the hospital for a second or third day. During the following month you must:

  • Be at home for a week (highly desirable);
  • To limit physical activity, to exclude intimacy;
  • Take a hygienic shower not earlier than a week after the facelift, while head down not tilt;
  • to refrain from smoking and drinking at least two coming weeks;
  • On the 5th-7th day to come to the clinic for the removal of seams in the face (the dressing is removed the day before), at home to regularly treat the healing sites with disinfectants;
  • After another 3-5 days, again visit the clinic and remove the stitches from the scalp;
  • To refuse to visit the sauna, bath and other thermal procedures;
  • Do not sunbathe in the sun, or in the solarium for six months or more until the healing of the scars;

    • Face Lift Options With Dr Robert Peterson

  • Use special creams to reduce swelling and resorption of hematomas, improve the condition of the incisions, if they are recommended by your doctor.

Important!

Return to your habitual way of life should be gradual, so as not to interfere with the healing processes of the wound and the proper formation of sutures. If during the period of recovery after a facelift your state of health worsens, you should immediately contact your doctor.

In general, the rehabilitation period is fairly calm, if you do not violate the regime and follow medical instructions.

Painful sensations disappear or become much weaker already on the third day. Unpleasant swelling subsides by the end of the first month after a facelift in Hawaii, around this same time, hematomas disappear.
