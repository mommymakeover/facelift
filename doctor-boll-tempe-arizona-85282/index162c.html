Is it possible to recover from a labiaplasty 'alone' (without having someone else in the house)?
Healing "alone"
Jul 27, 2021
Hello,

If you are having your labiaplasty under general anesthesia then you will want to have someone with you for the first 24 hours. If you are having it done with local anesthesia and you will not be put under at all, it is reasonable to be by yourself. You will just be laying and icing and relaxing while you heal.

Good luck with your upcoming procedure.
