Should I try a hair transplant for the scalp burn and to correct my hairline?

Would it be a bad idea to have hair transplanted to the beard area if I have a chin implant
Beard transplant after chin/jaw implants
Aug 27, 2019
Chin/jaw implants are not a contraindication to having a beard hair transplant. Choosing a hair transplant surgeon who is familiar with chin/jaw implants ie a Plastic Surgeon might be advisable. You should always ask your hair transplant surgeon about post operative graft care and scalp hygiene including shampoos/conditioners as some doctors are very particular about what products can or can’t be used over certain timeframes.
