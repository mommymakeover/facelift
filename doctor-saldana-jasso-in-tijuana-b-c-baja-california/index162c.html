Is possible to have labia augmentation first and then later have a vagina reconstruction?
answer
Aug 20, 2020
If possible, first do a labiaplasty and then a vaginal reconstruction.
Labiaplasty is outpatient surgery and is performed on the outside of the genitals. Vaginal reconstruction is not an outpatient surgery, it requires more days of recovery and is performed inside the vagina. In the vaginal canal. These surgeries can be performed together at the same time,or separately at different times.
