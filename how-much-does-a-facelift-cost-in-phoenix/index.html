Unfortunately, in our body irreversible aging processes occur. First of all, these changes become noticeable in the face and neck area, first in the form of slight fine lines, and then deeper wrinkles.

In these areas of open skin, the most rapid decrease occurs in the amount of natural collagen under the influence of destructive ultraviolet rays. The skin becomes dry, loses its elasticity, wrinkles, creases and sagging appear.

Today, thanks to innovations in the field of medicine, there are several methods for combating premature wrinkles on the face and neck.

For example, the deep folds on the forehead that appear, if a person often frowns, are eliminated with the help of botox procedures.

Deep wrinkles in the nasolabial area of the face can be reduced or completely removed by popular non-surgical correction of the face with a biopolymer gel. Ugly folds on the neck and in the decolletage can be eliminated by the procedure of reinforcement with gold threads.

An increasing number of patients decide to perform a rhytidectomy operation (facelift), which completely eliminates wrinkles around the neck and around the eyes. This surgical operation provides patients with a lasting effect of rejuvenation.

The essence of rhytidectomy is to remove excess skin, the operation is performed under general anesthesia. Through small incisions behind the ears, near the scalp, tools are introduced to separate some areas of the skin to make it more mobile. Then the skin is tightened, as a result of which deep wrinkles are smoothed out. Formed excess skin is removed and superimposed neat, barely noticeable, cosmetic sutures.

Complications with rhytidectomy are extremely rare. The result, as a rule, depends on the qualification and experience of the plastic surgeon. The rejuvenating effect of this operation is maintained for 5-7 years, then you can do a re-operation. Sutures are usually removed on the 10th day after surgery, the swelling remains for 3-4 weeks, it all depends on the characteristics of the patient’s body. The entire recovery period lasts up to two months.

Facelift in Phoenix is a correction of the shape and contours of the face and neck. This concept includes both conducting surgical operations and various aesthetic procedures, the purpose of which is the effect of rejuvenation – removing wrinkles and returning the elasticity of the skin.

As with all surgical operations, facelift also has contraindications, when the patient can not undergo this or that procedure, and the surgeon has no right to agree to an operation in order to avoid complications in the client’s health.

For example, facelift is strictly prohibited at high arterial pressure, with serious cardiovascular and oncological diseases, as well as in a number of chronic diseases (various infections, allergies, poor blood coagulation, etc.).
