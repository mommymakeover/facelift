What does it mean when it says that the results for a cheek lift last 10 years?
Duration of the procedure and biologic aging.
Aug 29, 2022
Hi, MSH57! Thank you for your question.
It is difficult to say that a procedure lasts 5 years, 10 years or more. This procedure lifts the descended anatomy of your face and the standard aging process applies like for everybody. For some patients a secondary lifting or a fat injection may be needed after 5 years, but in some patients it stays just like it is done. So the aging process after the surgery acts biologically and it is not fully possible to tell how long the effect will be there.
