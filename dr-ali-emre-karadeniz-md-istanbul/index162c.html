If was evaluated at ~9000 FUT grafts, what’s the best estimation for total number of FUE grafts?
Total Donor Capacity
Nov 6, 2018
Graft numbers are very deceiving. A graft is a piece of skin. We could theoretically take an unlimited number of pieces skin from the scalp. The smaller pieces we take the more grafts there will be. However, the goal in hair restoration surgery isn’t getting grafts (pieces of skin), it is to get hairs for coverage. Therefore, we should estimate total donor capacity in number of hairs, not grafts. This requires a live examination calculating donor density, skin laxity, surface area etc….
