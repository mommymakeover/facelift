Doctors

Vishnu Rumalla, MD

Name: Vishnu Rumalla, MD
Statement: Dr. Vishnu Rumalla is a double board-certified plastic surgeon based in Fort Worth, Texas. He performs cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries of the body and face, with a special focus on the mommy makeover, breast enhancement, and body contouring procedures, like tummy tucks and liposuction.

His expertise also includes both surgical and nonsurgical facial rejuvenation procedures, such as facelifts, blepharoplasty, browlifts, and injectables, like Botox and dermal fillers. He also performs reconstructive surgery, including breast and skin reconstruction, as well breast reduction surgery.

When it comes to his patients’ experiences, Dr. Rumalla does not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. At his practice, Tarrant Plastic Surgery, which has locations in Fort Worth, Keller, and Southlake, Dr. Rumalla and his highly skilled staff make sure each patient’s surgery plan is fully customized for them. Dr. Rumalla also incorporates innovative technology and techniques such as computer imaging, minimally invasive surgery, and pain pumps to ensure incredible results and a comfortable recovery.

After earning his undergraduate and medical degree at the University of Miami, Dr. Rumalla went on to receive his general surgery training at the University of Medicine and Dentistry at Rutgers University in New Jersey. He then completed his plastic surgery fellowship at Indiana University. He is board-certified by both the American Board of Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery.

Dr. Rumalla is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and has been the recipient of many awards, such as the Susan B. Deak Surgical Research Award and the William P. Burks Surgical Humanist Award. He’s also been named a top doctor by Fort Worth Magazine.
