Will a Hair Transplant help with my hair loss on my crown?
Crown loss at age 22
Mar 3, 2020
Typically we do not transplant hair into the crown of men under the age of early 30’s . The concern is that the pattern of loss will expand around the transplants leaving an “island” of grafting hair. Additionally, this would use up a large amount of your finite supply of donor hair,leaving possibly an inadequate amount for transplants to the hairline and front when needed. The most important thing for your situation is prevention of further loss -you are already doing this with Finast…
What size are SMP dots? I’m scared they will look fake
Scalp Micropigmentation
Feb 27, 2020
If done properly the SMP should nicely conceal your donor area. The dots of pigment should be about the size of a ballpoint pen mark, and depending on the pigment used will gradually fade after 8-10 years.
I’m turning 23 soon and depressed about hair loss. What stage of baldness am I in and what can I do a
23 with hair loss
Nov 11, 2019
The most important thing for you to do is have a consultation with a Dermatologist or experienced Hair Restoration surgeon to evaluate your hair loss and create an appropriate treatment plan. Probably they will recommend adjuncts to prevent further loss and possibly thicken up miniaturized hairs. Those adjuncts include Finasteride (Propecia), Minoxidil (Rogaine), Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) and/or Low Level Laserlight therapy.
