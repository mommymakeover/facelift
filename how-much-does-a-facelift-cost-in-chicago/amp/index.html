How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Chicago?

One day they just appear. The skin in the corners of the eyes looks like a crumpled cloth, “pleating” appears above the upper lip, elegant cheekbones look like hamster cheeks …

Women have different views on age-related skin changes.

Someone accepts the appearance of wrinkles as an inevitable part of life, someone wants to prolong youth, using modern achievements of cosmetic surgery.

Today we will talk about the facelift and what lies behind this promising word.

Facelift in Chicago is a whole trend in plastic surgery and cosmetology, which includes a variety of procedures, united by one goal: to smooth out the face, eliminating wrinkles.

Dr Peter D. Geldner Cosmetic Surgery Face

The term “facelift”, as a rule, means surgical techniques. Modern plastic surgery uses numerous facelift techniques, each of which has its own advantages, disadvantages and indications.

This effect can be achieved surgically or non-surgically. The facelift in Chicago can be carried out on the upper part (eyebrows, forehead) or middle (cheeks, nasolabial fold, lowering the corners of the mouth) of the face, or combine interference in both zones, but do it differently.

The surgeon must be able to determine the nature of the changes and apply the best method of intervention given the age of the patient and indication.

Simple facelift, skin tightening is a short, obsolete and relatively inexpensive procedure. In its course made a few incisions in the skin are placed over.

It is excised and the incisions stitched. There are scars that will have to be masked with the help of hair or clothes, and the effect of this operation does not last long, a maximum of two years without changes, and, most importantly, it looks often unnatural.

Facelift Without Surgery In Chicago

Today, one of the most advanced methods of plastic rejuvenation is the SMAS facelift (superfacial muscular-aponeurotic system lifting).

The innovation of this type of facelift is that the SMAS-lifting is not only skin, but also other soft tissues are lifting and moving. The muscular frame, which forms the face and neck, is also put in order, which guarantees a more natural and long-term effect.

SMAS facelift in Chicago allows to eliminate the ugly and ridiculous consequences of rejuvenating plastic surgery – asymmetry, a change in the proportion of features, unnatural stretch of the skin.

Of course, this technique of deep facelift requires the exceptional skill of the surgeon.

There are various modifications of SMAS facelift, including an extended SMAS facelift, concentrating on the elimination of nasolabial folds, SMAS-plication, in its course the excess skin is not cut off, but internal folds are formed, which allows modeling the outline of the face, S-lift, in which neck tissue is not affected and the lift is performed with minimal S-shaped incisions (this operation is chosen by women under 40 years of age, the tone of the muscular skeleton of which has not yet undergone drastic changes).

Laser Facelift Chicago Before And After

Today, endoscopic facelift is extremely popular. This is quite an expensive operation, but its advantages look very tempting – since all manipulations are performed through micro cuts in the mouth and on the scalp, the scars remain imperceptible. Endoscopic intervention, as a rule, is combined with the use of SMAS lifting technology, so the result is worthy and long-term.

Mini facelift in Chicago (this, incidentally, refers to the above-mentioned S-lift) is a simplified facelift procedure. It is also called “partial facelift”, “French facelift”. The action of mini facelift is aimed at slightly improving the appearance of the skin, that began to age, or “pinpoint” to correct the deficiencies, for example, to tighten only sagging cheeks.

The advantages of a mini facelift are fewer and depth of scars, a shorter recovery period and reduced side effects.

Mini Facelift Chicago

Today, facelifts operations are performed very often. Usually patients are in the hospital for 2-3 days, after which they remain at home for some time, following the doctor’s recommendations concerning hygiene, nutrition, and regime.

Usually, 3 weeks after the facelift, the face already looks like it should, and after a couple of months it finally comes to form. In the case of mini facelift, these terms are reduced by about half. Unfortunately, like any surgical intervention, facelift can not be a completely safe procedure. The risks related to the facelift include:

  • bruises and bleeding
  • damage to nerves and facial muscles
  • impaired hearing, sense of smell, facial sensitivity
  • slow healing and poor scarring (threatens diabetics, smokers)
  • hair loss.

Neck Lift In Chicago Before And After

As a rule, if something is wrong, feelings and senses are restored, wounds are scarred, and hair finally grows within 6-12 months after the operation.

Surgical facelift techniques are often combined with non-surgical techniques.

These include the so-called invasive procedures, or injections, with the help of which local-paralyzing drugs (botox, dysport), fillers (wrinkle fillers, which also allow modeling cheekbones and chin) are injected into the skin, hyaluronic acid and collagen preparations, as well as patient’s own fat.

Of course, non-surgical techniques can be used separately. The less wrinkles are expressed and the outline of the face is changed, the more effective they will be.
