Will my nose become more slim over time? 16 days post rhinoplasty.
Healing after rhinoplasty
Jun 29, 2018
Swelling after rhinoplasty dose take a year or longer to resolve.  The swelling will appear as if your nose is wider/fatter and less defined than you want it to be.  The thicker the skin, the longer it takes for the nose swelling to go away.  It is a process that takes time, however you should try to focus on enjoying the improvements that you already have and let nature take its course.

Long-term the endoscopic forehead lift should not change your facial expression.  The procedure does raise your brows to a more elevated position, so yes that will change. However, the muscle motion should be the same as before once the swelling is resolved if the muscles are not cut during the procedure.

Bruising and healing can be quite variable from person to person.  We all heal along a bell curve.
