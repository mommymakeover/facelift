Dr. Ioannis Gkotsikas is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Thessaloniki, Greece.

He received his medical degree from the Medical School of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and begun his training in general surgery at the Sahlgrenska University Hospital in Sweden. Dr. Gkotsikas completed his specialist training in plastic surgery at the Plastic Surgery Department of the University of Thessaloniki and at the Reconstructive Plastic Surgery Department of the University Hospital “Karolinska” in Stockholm.

He continues to expand his knowledge and expertise in the field of plastic surgery by attending international training seminars and conferences. In the context of his doctoral thesis at the Medical School of the Aristotle University, Dr. Gkotsikas studied the effect of the use of topical negative pressure in the incorporation of skin grafts.

Since 2010, Dr. Gkotsikas is board-certified and a regular member of the Hellenic Society of Plastic Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgery (HESPRAS). He practices aesthetic and reconstructive plastic surgery and personally performs all non-surgical treatments at his private practice in Thessaloniki.

Can I have Lipo instead of a tummy tuck?
Liposuction vs tummy tuck
Dec 13, 2017
In my opinion, tummy tuck, maybe combined with liposuction, is indicated in your case. Liposuction alone will only make things worse. Skin laxity will probably accentuate and you will not be satisfied. My advice is to achieve your weight goals and then schedule an appointment for a consultation with a board certified plastic surgeon in your area.
is it best to wait for Dysport to wear off or go back and try to fix the issue?
Too early to judge Dysport treatment
May 11, 2017
It takes approximately two weeks to see the full effect of Dysport treatment. I always check my patients 15 days after the first injection and, if necessary, add small amounts of Dysport in order to fix any problem. I suggest that you wait another week and then call your doctor for a follow-up consultation if you are still not satisfied with the results.
