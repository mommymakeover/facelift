Atlanta Facelift Surgery

Facelift in Atlanta will help to look young to men and women at any age.

It allows for a long time to forget about wrinkles and visible age changes.

Indications for facelift and neck

– Stretched and saggy skin of face and neck in 40-50 years;
– Premature aging in 30-35 years;
– The appearance of the second chin;
– Deep wrinkles;
– Overhanging eyelids;
– Omitted outer corners of the eyes.

The list of contraindications

  • Presence of diseases: chronic, oncological, infectious;
  • Poor blood clotting.

The presence of diseases must be reported to the doctor. If contraindications are found during the tests, the plastic surgeon will refuse the operation.

Preparation for surgery

The preliminary stage begins with a plastic surgeon’s examination of the structure, color and age changes of the skin. Then the surgeon decides on the need for surgery for a detailed discussion. The doctor will necessarily consult about the recovery period and possible complications.

Recommended

  • If there is dependence on smoking, reduce the number of cigarettes per day 14 days before the operation;
  • Give up alcohol for 2 weeks before the procedure;
  • tell your doctor about taking drugs;
  • exclude hormonal preparations;
  • One day before the facelift in Atlanta, a “hunger day,” and not take liquid a few hours before the operation.

Analyzes and procedures:

  • General blood analysis;
  • General urine analysis;
  • Biochemical blood test:
  • Glucose;
  • Urea;
  • Creatinine;
  • Bilirubin;
  • Blood for HIV;
  • Blood on hepatitis markers;
  • Blood on the RW;
  • Electrocardiogram and examination and conclusion of the therapist (men after 45 years, women after 50 years);
  • blood type.

The tests are valid for no more than 14 days.

How is the facelift performed?

The incision is made with a scalpel from the temporal region to the border of the auricle. Then incision goes to the back of the head. The surgeon exfoliates the skin of the cheeks, temples, neck and chin, redistributes fatty tissues. Muscles and tissues are fixed by sutures. The skin stretches in the direction upwards and forwards, the excess is removed.

Result

  1. Wrinkle smoothing;
  2. Skin tightening on the border between the face and neck;
  3. restoration of facial contours.

Recovery

  • 2-3 days after facelift in Atlanta, it is necessary to observe bed rest in a stationary ward under the supervision of doctors;
  • For 7 days wear a compression mask on the face;
  • Removal of the front seams is done in a week, and the seams from the occiput region are removed after 10 days;
  • It is possible wash your hair and use makeup after 4 days, and dye your hair after a month;
  • Exclude physical activity, visits to the solarium and sauna;
  • swelling and bruising of the skin disappear after approximately 2 months;
  • A month later, you can take a face massage course as a rehabilitative therapy.
  • A full result is noticeable in 6-8 months after the operation. The term of facelift depends on the individual features of the patient’s skin, life conditions and the presence of bad habits. Under favorable conditions, the result can persist for 10 years.
