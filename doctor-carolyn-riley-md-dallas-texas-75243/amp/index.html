What labiaplasty techniques would be best for me?
Consider nonsurgical intervention
Nov 26, 2019
An in person evaluation is necessary to determine which intervention would be best treatment for you. There is a new non-surgical treatment that you may be a good candidate for. The procedure is done in the office with a device called Aviva (by InMode). It uses bipolar radiofrequency to reduce the size of the labia and clitoral hood without any incisions. The procedure can be done with local anesthesia and there is minimal down time!

Morpheus8, which is a RF microneedling device, works well for reducing the appearance of stretch marks. The number of treatments needed to get the desired results depends on the severity of the stretch marks.

Post Delivery Vagina
Nov 25, 2019
The impact of childbirth on the vagina has many variables (i.e. genetics, trauma during childbirth, etc.). Some of the effects can be long-term and can negatively impact sexual stimulation.
