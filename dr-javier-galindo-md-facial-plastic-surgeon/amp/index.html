Is it possible to deproject a nose without any placement of grafts?
Nasal tip deprojection
Mar 20, 2017
Of course, your nasal tip can be deprojected without any graft. Most nasal tip grafts are performed to increase nasal projection. In an overprotected nose, one or either way the length of the lower lateral cartilages must be shortened, and for that purpose no grafting is regularly needed. Look for a well trained surgeon that can fix your problem in the best way, because grafting or not is not the most important thing.
Is it normal to have breathing problems three months post-op?
Impaired breathing
Mar 20, 2017
After 3 months you should be breathing fine, but take in consideration that the inner nose can take up to 1 year to fully recover from surgery. If everything was done properly and your surgeon cared about the nasal function you should recover sooner or latter.
