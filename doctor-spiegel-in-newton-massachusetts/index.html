Would buccal fat removal make my jowls worse in the future, or will it make that place less sagg
Probably unrelated
Nov 19, 2023
I don't think that buccal fat removal will make jowls worse in the future. Jowls will form based on your genetics, lifestyle, and skin care. Buccal fat removal will make you look more angular, and the volume can easily be replaced with fat or a filler if necessary at any time. Chin implants, when necessary, are great at improving the jawline! Both can be done at the same time right in the office.
My deep plane neck lift has failed. Can the submandibular gland be removed to get a better resul
If it is the gland it's probably an easy fix
Nov 19, 2023
You can see if it's the gland by injecting the area with Botulinum (eg. Botox). This will shrink the gland and get rid of that mass. It could even be a permanent fix in some cases.

Nov 19, 2023
