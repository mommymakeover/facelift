Do I need cheek lift, any lift as I’m worried I look old?
Do I need a cheek lift?
Dec 15, 2020
I certainly don’t think so. You have full, youthful cheeks and I do not see a lot of gravity-related change. Even when you do start seeing gravity related change, the cheeks don’t necessarily need to be lifted. It can be easier and more economical to have either fat or fillers injected to provide some volume without having to lift anything.
