Facelift In Newport Beach CA

Facelifting in Newport Beach CA is an operation aimed at eliminating various signs of aging aging: wrinkles, folds, sagging of the skin, lowering the corners of the eyes and lips, double chin, etc. Facelift has different names: but behind all these names is an operation that involves excision of excess skin tissue and their subsequent tightening and fixation in a new position. Nevertheless, there are many methods of performing this operation, designed to correct individual parts of the face, or completely face and neck.

Facelift is not an easy operation, both for the surgeon and the patient. The main difficulty, however, is that a face is always in sight and can not be hidden under clothes, which means that the surgeon must ensure not only the result of rejuvenation, but also the preservation of the natural features of the face, the absence of noticeable scars.

The key to the successful result of facelift surgery in Newport Beach is a complete mutual understanding between the patient and the surgeon, in which the surgeon understands the wishes of the patient, and patient, in turn, is his real potential. That is why it is important to ask in advance all the necessary questions and get exhaustive and clear answers to them.

How do I determine what I really need a facelift?

Deciding on the need for anti-aging surgery is really difficult. Facelift is an age-related operation and, as a rule, in it is not necessary up to 40 years of age. If the skin is abundant in collagen and elastin, it is capable of dealing with many age-related changes. If you see in the mirror that there is a sagging of tissues around the mouth, cheeks, eyes, deep wrinkles and wrinkles, while you look gloomy and tired even when you are not really that, then usual skin care is not enough for you. It is worth turning to a plastic surgeon.

Best Facelift In Newport Beach

It should be noted that cosmetology procedures can not always be a full-fledged alternative to plastic surgery. Facelift is able to eliminate wrinkles and tighten the face, but not improve its condition, while cosmetic procedures can improve the quality of the skin, but can not fight the obvious signs of aging. The optimal solution is the combination of cosmetology and surgical methods.

What a facelift procedure is right for me?

You can find the answer to this question only with your surgeon. There are a lot of methods, some of them involve only a skin lift, others – a tightening at the level of muscle tissue. There are techniques that allow to adjust only certain areas, for example, the forehead lift allows to eliminate deep wrinkles and eliminate the overhanging of the eyebrows, without affecting or traumatizing the face as a whole.

Endoscopic facelift in Newport Beach is quite a popular technique today. It allows you to perform surgery through small incisions in places well hidden from prying eyes (for example, in the hair growth zone). Endoscopic facelift does not require a long recovery, does not leave any visible scars, and reduces overall trauma.

Nevertheless, it is worth discussing whether this method is suitable for you, since with pronounced changes, endoscopic facelifting is not carried out.

The purpose of the rejuvenating operation is obvious: facelift should not change your appearance, but rather return the old look. Therefore, it is best to take your picture when you are young, to help the surgeon understand what you are aiming for.
