Facelift In Tucson AZ

Facelift is today one of the most popular types of plastic surgery in Tucson.

Facelift surgery allows patients aged to restore the elasticity of the skin, eliminate its sagging, remove wrinkles and other signs of aging.

The choice of the method of carrying out a facelift is performed by the surgeon, based on the characteristics of each patient.

Results of facelift:

– Quality face rejuvenation
– Keeping the result for years to come
– Fresh and attractive facial skin without wrinkles

  1. The operation time is approximately 1-5 hours.
  2. A face lift is performed under general anesthesia.
  3. The patient is in the hospital for 2-3 days.
  4. The recovery period lasts from 10-14 days.
  5. The result is a face without wrinkles.

Indications for surgery facelift

  • Sagging of the skin of the forehead and brow, ptosis of the eyelids;
  • Deep nasolabial folds;
  • The appearance of wrinkles in the nose area;
  • jawl, double chin;
  • Presence of severe wrinkles on the neck.

Contraindications to facelift.

  • Severe diseases of internal organs;
  • Presence of tumors;
  • Infectious diseases;
  • Violation of blood clotting;
  • Acne.

  5. General, local or intravenous anesthesia, depending on the method of operation and the complexity of the operation.
  6. The operation lasts 1-5 hours, depending on the complexity and extent of the intervention.

  • During the week the patient wears a compression mask.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is it true that endoscopic forehead lift is better than other methods for tightening this area?

Optionally, for each type of face, the surgeon chooses the appropriate technique for him. The main thing is for the plastic surgeon to master all the main methods of facelift and to argue his choice of technique for your case.

How long does the face rejuvenation effect last?

Qualitatively carried out facelift is preserved up to 10-15 years. In some cases, operative and invasive procedures are combined to prolong the effect (fillers, botox, collagen preparations, etc.).

Where there will be post-surgical scars, and whether they are visible?

Modern techniques of facelifts allow not leaving visible scars. Postoperative scars are invisible, the patient can safely wear short hairstyles.

Does smoking affect the result of the operation and the healing process?

Doctors recommend to give up smoking, or at least reduce the number of cigarettes 2-3 weeks before and after a facelift. Nicotine promotes narrowing of blood vessels, which can affect the healing speed.

Do I need a haircut shortly before the facelift?

When carrying out the surgery for facelift, the hair is specially laid, there is no need to shave or cut hair.
