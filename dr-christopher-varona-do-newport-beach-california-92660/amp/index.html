Can my eyebrows grow back in the place of stitches?
Scarred eyebrow
Feb 14, 2019
Unfortunately the scar tissue usually replaces the hair follicles so there most likely won’t be hair growing back in the area. Replacing the follicles with a transplant can camoflage the scar tissue in the area where the eyebrow previous was and restore symmetry to the face. The angles of the grafts are critical for a natural result so its important to find a surgeon experienced with this specialized type of transplant.

Is this the final FUE result as per the grafts transplanted?
results
Feb 7, 2019
In general the transplanted grafts take 12-16 months to see the final results. You’ll see a big visual change typically between 6-9 months but the density will continue to increase to a lesser extent up to 12 months. Some of the characteristics of the growing hair like curl, color, and of course length will further mature between the 12-16th month. So I think its too early to say your current state is the final result.
