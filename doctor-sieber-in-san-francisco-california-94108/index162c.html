How do I get more upper pole fullness?
Implant profiles
Feb 12, 2021
Part of the issue is that a high profile implant isn’t the best choice to get more upper pole fullness. As you can see, the top of the implant is near the nipple. You need a wider/taller profile implant that can occupy more of that space in the upper pole to give you the fullness you desire.

In addition, your implants may have bottomed out slightly so fixing this will also bring the implant up a little higher and put it in a better position behind the nipple.

Best,
Dr. Sieber

Is BWD, implant width and potential impact for BAD, too-high-of-an-implant, or capsular contraction
Base width
Sep 4, 2019
This is a pretty advanced question! The short answer is no, base width does not have an effect on animation deformity. Having and animation deformity is more due to how pectoralis is released during surgery. Over or under release of the muscle can cause a more severe and dramatic animation deformity. Using an implant which is too wide or too narrow has more of an effect on final breast shape. Best,Dr. Sieber
