HIFU Facelift Before And After

HIFU facelift before and after are a photographs of a patient’s face before and after ultrasound treatment (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound).

Modern cosmetology is rapidly developing and already today it allows to effectively and safely eliminate age-related manifestations on the skin with the use of non-surgical techniques.

One of these procedures is HIFU facelift – a unique technology that acts on the deeper layers of the skin and facial muscles.

This device is an effective substitute for surgical facelift for patients who have contraindications to surgery or who are not ready to use cardinal techniques.

A Non-surgical Treatment To Tackle The Signs Of Aging

Due to high-frequency sound waves occurs simultaneously:

  • Tissue lifting;
  • Wrinkle smoothing;
  • Lifting of the contours of the face;
  • Improvement of the epidermis.

Acceleration of cellular metabolism triggers natural mechanisms of rejuvenation, which allows for a long time to preserve the effect achieved. The effect of the procedure is noticeable immediately after the session and has a cumulative effect up to 6 months. It is shown for 1 time in 1-1,5 years.

HIFU facelift is carried out on the basis of the SMAS-lifting technique. The innovative device generates ultrasonic waves.

HIFU Non-Surgical Face Lift

Several types of active sensors have a different frequency, which allows you to work on tissues at different depths. As a result of ultra-lifting occurs:

  • Disappearance of superficial wrinkles, bags and dark circles under the eyes;
  • rejuvenation;
  • Increased skin density;
  • The appearance of a clear contour of the face.

We will certainly acquaint you with the indications and contraindications to the HIFU facelift.

Correctly selected means, qualitative and thorough preparation will ensure achievement of the desired results without harm to your body.
