Does the scalp reattach to the skull after hairline lowering procedure?
Hairline lowering
Nov 6, 2020
Hello! Yes, the scalp is detached to move forward to lower the hairline but heals and reattaches quickly. It does not have any long term issues regarding the initial separation provided it is done correctly. There are, of course, other issues such as temporary scalp numbness, the visibility of the scar and possible weakness - temporary or permanent- that should be discussed. Good luck!

Does losing weight dissolve filler faster?
weight loss and fillers
Oct 28, 2019
Hello! Weight loss does not impact fillers. Fillers are naturally dissolved by an enzyme found in the skin, hyaluronidase. Tobacco use and ultraviolet light (sun exposure) have been shown to increase hyaluronidase but weight loss does not. A significant amount of weight loss (usually more than 10 pounds) can cause loss of volume in the face though, from natural fat loss but this is not a loss of filler volume. Good luck!
