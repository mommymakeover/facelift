14 days post op hair transplant, transplanted hair came out. Is this common?
Hair shedding after a Transplant
Jun 24, 2019
Most of the transplanted hairs will shed out and regrow. Clients often times become concerned when they see their hair shed out after the healing of the transplant, but the follicles are just going dormant under the skin and will regenerate a new hair.
Given these photos can I expect PRp treatment to give me back complete density?
100% Density
Jun 24, 2019
Patient results with PRP vary greatly from patient to patient. Most regrowth we find to be from hair that has been lost within the last few years. Typically 100% density is not achieved with PRP treatments.
Some of the transplant hair has not shed yet 5 months post op is this normal?
5 months post op growth
Mar 1, 2019
It is not unusual for some the the transplanted hairs to continue to grow after a transplant without going through a dormancy stage.
1 month post hair transplant. Is rogaine or minoxidil necessary for results?
Minoxidil after HT
Feb 21, 2019
The minoxidil is not necessary to use after hair transplantation to get good growth. I do recommend using it or another treatment such as finasteride to stop additional hair loss for maximum density.

Minoxidil hair loss
Feb 21, 2019
Hair that sheds from starting Minoxidil will typically regrow in a few months. Most patients will initialy shed some hair when they begin Minoxidil.
