Facelift Surgery In Colombia

Age changes such as the appearance of wrinkles, the severity of the nasolabial triangle, the redistribution of facial tissues, despite the progress in modern cosmetology, give the age of the patient, which can adversely affect the personal life, etc.

One way to “cheat time” is facelift in Colombia. The most frequently performed operations are a 2/3 facelift with SMAS, a full face lift with SMAS, etc.

Where to begin?

Before surgery should consult a plastic surgeon. The surgeon inspects the facial tissues, carefully prepares anamnesis, the patient gives all the necessary tests for hospitalization, the list of which will be obtained from the doctor.

It is especially important, in addition to the general condition of the patient, to find out whether there is a tendency to develop keloid scars.

The doctor will offer the most optimal variant of the future plastic surgery on the face.

After making a mutual decision by the patient and surgeon about the operation, an anesthesiologist is examined before the operation, which also assesses the operational and anesthetic risk.

Preparation for surgery

Preparations for surgery include failure or significant reduction in smoking 2 weeks before surgery, as this can lead to edge necrosis in the ear area as a result of the harmful effect of nicotine on the microcirculation of the ischemic skin, stop taking desaggregants (aspirin etc.) , which will lead to the development of interstitial hematomas.

In the presence of short haircuts, it is desirable for a woman to temporarily grow hair so that the postoperative sutures are invisible to others.

On the day of the operation, the hair is fixed with elastic scrunchy, marking is performed on the necessary areas, to prepare for the facelift procedure for the patient.

A facelift in Colombia is performed under general anesthesia. The incision is made along the scalp along the ear, bends around it and passes to the occipital region. After that, the skin separates from the underlying tissues. Then, to ensure a long-lasting facelift, the muscular-aponeurotic system (SMAS) is neatly allocated.

After that there is a moderate tension and fixation SMAS, with subsequent modeling skin and applying external and intradermal stitches in the ear area. Drainage is installed on both sides. The head of the patient is tightly bandaged with a special stretch bandage for this operation with the purpose of preventing bleeding and for tight fitting of the skin.

Results and effectiveness

After the facelift surgery, the patient is in the hospital for up to 3 days. The patient’s face in the postoperative period will be swollen, numb, taut, but this is a temporary phenomenon that will disappear in a week.

The surgeon will warn about this, so that the patient does not start to worry in vain and perceived this as a normal phenomenon. In two three weeks from the operation, your face will look much better, but for several months the skin will be dry and sometimes rough.

These temporary phenomena will also disappear. The result for which you came will be only six months or more, with all the tissues smoothing, the scars will be invisible and covered with the ear and hair. The result of the facelift remains for 5 to 10 years or more, but after this time you will still look significantly younger than your peers.

Indications for use

When you need to refer to a plastic surgeon for a facelift? There are several characteristic features that allow one to positively judge the possibility of using this type of operation:

  • ptosis of the eyelids
  • Mimic sagging the skin of the forehead or outer corners of the eyes
  • Nasolabial folds are a sign of aging and loss of moisture in the cells of the lips
  • Folds on the neck, as well as fatty sagging of the chin (the so-called “double” chin)
