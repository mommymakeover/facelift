Benefits Of Thermage

Thermage treatment is an increasingly popular cosmetic procedure designed to reduce the signs of aging by stimulating collagen production and tightening the existing collagen in the skin. If you want to learn more about Thermage, keep reading:

What are the benefits of Thermage treatment? Thermage treatment is a safe procedure that has been proven to help tighten and contour the skin, producing younger-looking and smoother skin. This type of treatment is minimally invasive and requires virtually no recovery time.

Plus, it can be used to treat people of all skin types. There is no surgery required or abrasive tools needed, which means you can achieve naturally younger-looking results in a short period of time.

What can I expect from a Thermage treatment? During your Thermage treatment, your cosmetic specialist will use a hand-piece that features a smooth, flat tip to apply radio frequency technology to the skin.

The radio frequency safely heats the dermal layer of the skin where collagen is produced. The heat stimulates collagen production, which can provide even skin tone, reduce drooping skin, and prevent the development of wrinkles. An increase in collagen production can also renew youthful contours of the skin and improve the smoothness and texture of the skin’s epidermis layer, or surface.

Within weeks, collagen will begin to regenerate, leading to tighter skin that looks younger, more evenly toned, and smooth. Results will be influenced by the areas of your skin that you have treated.

For example, if you undergo a Thermage treatment around your eyes, then you’ll experience reduced eyelid hooding and look less tired and aged. Thermage can be applied to nearly every area of the body. If you have wrinkled or sagging skin or cellulite, then a Thermage treatment can help tighten skin, reducing wrinkles, drooping, and dimpling.

What To Expect When You Receive A Thermage Treatment

Thermage is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure that can help contour, tighten, and smooth skin in order to create a more youthful appearance. Thermage works by transmitting heat energy throughout the deep, collagen-rich lower layers of your skin. This heat tightens existing collagen and stimulates the formation of new collagen. Read on to learn what you can expect during a Thermage treatment:

Pre-Treatment Consultation: When you meet with your physician, you will be able to discuss your desired areas of improvement. Together, you and your doctor can determine what results can be expected and whether you are a good candidate, as well as how long the treatment may take.

Thermage First Pioneered The Non-invasive Treatment Of Wrinkles Using Radiofrequency Energy

Experiencing Thermage: Thermage treatment is a relatively short process that takes an average of 45 minutes for the face and an hour and a half for other parts of the body. The length of time needed to complete the process depends on the size, location, and condition of the area.

During the procedure, your practitioner will place a small, hand-held device against your skin on the area to be treated. Each time the heat device touches your skin, you will feel a brief, cooling sensation, then a deep but short heating sensation, and finally another cooling sensation. Your physician will always ask for feedback on the level of heat used in order to balance your comfort with maximum results.

After Treatment: Thermage is a gentle, one-time procedure that requires no recovery time. Although your skin may be slightly red immediately following treatment, this should disappear within 24 hours. Results will develop in the two to six months after treatment, as your body renews the skin’s underlying collagen. Depending on your age and the condition of the skin that’s treated, Thermage can last for years.

How to Prevent Cellulite after Your Thermage Treatment

Cellulite is composed of small portions of fat that press against your skin from beneath, creating a dimpled look. Follow these easy tips to reduce the formation and appearance of cellulite and continue looking your best.

Treat The Deeper Layers Of The Skin

Quit Smoking

Smoking has many detrimental effects on the body, including damaging the skin. Habitual smoking promotes early aging and dehydration. Both of these factors affect the look of your skin and the health of your skin’s connective fibers.

Stay Hydrated

Hydration is extremely important for healthy skin. Drinking enough water and eating hydrating foods each day can help prevent the formation of cellulite. In addition, well-hydrated skin is plumper and better able to repair itself than dry skin. Staying hydrated will help keep you looking young and healthy after your Thermage treatment.

Exercise Often

You Look Younger, In A Natural Way

Exercise is great for all parts of your body, including your skin. Cellulite tends to form on the lower body as a result of long periods spent sitting in a chair. Activity promotes a healthier circulatory system, which reduces the risk of cellulite development. Exercise also helps maintain a healthy body weight and build muscle, which reduces the appearance of cellulite. Being active doesn’t require spending long hours at the gym—simply take frequent walks during the day or take the stairs rather than the elevator to promote healthy skin.

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet that is rich in nutritious, lean foods promotes a healthy weight and better circulation. Incorporate heart-healthy foods, such as avocado and salmon, into your diet and choose lean snacks, such as vegetables or fruits, to help keep your entire body looking great.
