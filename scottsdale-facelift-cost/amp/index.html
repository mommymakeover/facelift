What is a face lift in Scottsdale? What are the most important indications for Face lift?

Cosmetic surgery aimed at removing external signs of aging of the skin of the face and neck (wrinkles, flabbiness, sagging, folds) is called face lift.

Indications for face lift:

  • Wrinkles in the cheekbones, forehead, temples;
  • Lowering the corners of the mouth, eyelids, soft tissues of the cheeks;
  • Sagging of the skin of the frontal part of the head and in the eyebrow zone;
  • The formation of a “double” chin;
  • Presence of bags under the eyes;
  • Formation of pronounced nasolabial folds;
  • The appearance of deep wrinkles in the nose;
  • withering skin of the neck;
  • A noticeable change in the contours of the face.

Types of face lift

There are many techniques that allow for effective lifting, but the main ones are: circular face lift, SMAS, deep and endoscopic face lift. A brief overview of the facelifting methods will allow to determine the features and effectiveness of each method. Read more, about how to become younger without surgery with a face lift.

Circular face lift

Traditional plastic surgery to correct age-related changes, helping to recreate the right angle between the neck and chin, improving the contours of the face and smoothing the skin relief. The procedure is performed under general anesthesia with incision along the hair growth line in front of and behind the ears. After separation of the skin from adjacent tissues, the excess is cut off, the skin is tightened and the edges of the skin are aligned.

The stitches are superimposed, which are removed after 10-14 days. At the end of 3 weeks, the traces from the incisions are not very noticeable, and after half a year they disappear completely. Circular face lift is recommended for patients aged 45+ when cosmetic and hardware do not give positive results. The effectiveness of the method is obvious: the face looks younger for 10-15 years for a long time.

SMAS facelift

Aesthetic operation allows to smooth out wrinkles and get rid of such problems as nasolabial and cervical folds, ptosis of the eyelids, soft tissue of the face, “jowl”. The essence lies in a deep skin tightening with the gripping of dense tissues under it. A face lift is performed under general anesthesia. The incision is made from the anterior edge of the auricle to the occipital part, after which the muscular-aponeurotic system moves and is fixed in a new position.

Recovery is already on the 3rd month after the operation. Face lift is indicated to patients under 45 years of age – when age signs are clearly visible, but the skin is still capable of contracting.

The overall external effect is described as rejuvenation in average years by 10. Corrective procedure aimed at removing the inevitable, regular changes in the middle and lower parts of the face (in the zone of the cheekbone, neck, chin, jaw) and affecting subperiosteal tissues.

The probability of damage to the facial nerve is high when performing facelift surgery. The age group for such a serious surgical intervention is the elderly. The final result is visible after a two-month recovery course and lasts at least 10 years.

Endoscopic face lift in Scottsdale AZ is performed using a miniature video camera that reduces the risk of damage to blood vessels and nerve endings.

This type of operation has a lot of positive feedback, since it refers to minimally invasive methods that do not require large incisions of the skin.

The main candidates for the aesthetic procedure are patients aged 30-40 years, who need a tightening of the upper and middle part of the face. Visible scars are absent, and the effect of rejuvenation persists for 7-10 years.

Contraindications and recovery period.

Plastic surgery, like any other operation, has limitations related to the state of health.

Contraindications to facelift surgery include:

  • Chronic diseases in acute phase;
  • Oncological processes;
  • diabetes;
  • Pustular eruptions on the skin of the face and neck;
  • Pathology of connective tissue;
  • Violation of the coagulation capacity of blood;
  • Problems associated with cardiovascular activity;
  • Severe diseases of internal organs;
  • pregnancy;
  • lactation.

Patients, who underwent surgery in Scottsdale, remain in the hospital for the first 3-5 days after the operation under the supervision of the staff. The doctor prescribes analgesic therapy and taking antibiotics. A tight bandage of sterile surgical bandages is superimposed on the face, which is removed on the second day. Then, special compression masks are applied for 1-2 weeks. Stitches are removed after 5 days in some places, in others after 10-14 days.

The first 2 months after face lift are prohibited:

  • to visit the solarium, sunbathing under the sun;
  • stay in the bath or sauna;
  • dye your hair;
  • perform increased physical activity;
  • drinking alcohol and smoking.

The term of full recovery after a face lift directly depends on the method and complexity of the procedure: basically, the hematomas and puffiness of the tissues go down after 2-3 weeks, the traces of surgical intervention disappear about a month and the final effect is achieved after six months. Repeated operation is possible not earlier than in 5-10 years.
