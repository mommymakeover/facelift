Doctors

Arslan Musbeh, MD –

Name: Arslan Musbeh, MD –
Statement: Dr. Arslan Musbeh, MD, is a hair implant surgeon at Hairmedico, one of the hair transplant clinics in Turkey. A pioneer in follicular unit extraction (FUE) hair transplantation, he specializes in hair transplants, beard transplants, eyebrow transplants, scar restoration, micropigmentation, laser therapy, and other hair loss treatments. In his 16 years of practice, Dr. Musbeh has performed thousands of successful hair transplants using the most innovative methods, such as FUE, direct hair implant (DHI), and Sapphire FUE.

Dr. Musbeh has a stellar reputation for his personalized, hands-on approach and natural-looking results. He operates on only one patient per day and performs all surgical procedures himself, from graft harvesting to incisions, to guarantee the best possible care, safety, and results. He also personally trains Hairmedico’s entire medical team in a range of solutions, to help solve your hair loss problems.

Patients from all around the world seek out Dr. Musbeh for the quality of his care and results. “My aim is absolute satisfaction, since we believe that our patients deserve the best-qualified practitioner,” says Dr. Musbeh.

For Dr. Musbeh, the inspiration to pursue a medical career started early on. Finding his passion for hair restoration was only a matter of time. “Becoming a doctor was largely motivated by the values that my faith and my family had instilled in me,” says Dr. Musbeh. “It is a challenging profession.”

Dr. Musbeh received his medical degree at Odessa State Medical University in Ukraine in 2000. “After completing my general medicine degree, I decided to focus on aesthetic medicine and moved to France to attend classes,” says Dr. Musbeh.

He spent 12 years in France, specializing first in general medicine and later in alopecia and hair transplant surgery. “I began practicing nonsurgical aesthetics, particularly platelet-rich plasma therapy and mesotherapy to treat hair loss. At that time, I received many requests for hair transplants from my patients, so I decided to specialize in this field. Hair transplantation has become my passion.”

He received additional specialized training in aesthetic medicine and hair transplants at Faculté de médecine de Tours and Université Claude Bernard Lyon in France.

Today, in addition to his private practice, Dr. Musbeh strives to spread his knowledge in hair restoration through education. He is a Diplôme Inter Universitaire Européen teacher in hair loss treatments and hair transplants at Université Claude Bernard Lyon, in France. He also trains doctors through workshops and live surgery sessions in Istanbul, Turkey, and abroad.

Dr. Musbeh is a member of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery. He offers virtual consultations for patients considering hair restoration and looks forward to discussing your goals.
