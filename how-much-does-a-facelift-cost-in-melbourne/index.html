How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Melbourne?

Facelift in Melbourne is one of the most popular aesthetic operations of our time. Such a high popularity of the procedure is due to a number of its features. Facial Rejuvenation Surgery is the most efficient and versatile method of removing age-related changes.

Of all the aesthetic procedures, the facelift effect is the most resistant, and to save it does not need a complex beauty care. Facelift provides visible rejuvenation for 15-17 years, without the need to resort to surgery in the future.

Facelift in Melbourne is a complex and multifaceted procedure, putting forward special requirements for the competence of surgeons. The rejuvenating effect of plastic surgery is achieved not only by working with the skin, but also by lifting the muscle corset.

Acupuncture Facelift Melbourne Images

Experts for a long time successfully work in this direction, developing and implementing effective methods of surgical rejuvenation. Complex rejuvenation involves not only the restoration of the contour of the face, correction of the eyelids and periorbital zone, but also a neck lift. In modern aesthetic medicine, there are three main types of facelift:

  1. Circular facelift with removal of skin excess;
  2. SMAS facelift is the work with the muscular corset of the face, its movement and fixing in a new position;
  3. Endoscopic facelift is a low-traumatic intervention, allowing the operation to be carried out almost imperceptibly for others.

A various facelift techniques can be combined in a single operation to achieve the desired effect. This makes it possible to effectively modify various areas of the face, eliminating the most profound age changes and achieving the maximum lasting effect.

Doctor Carmen Munteanu Jowls Facelift Recovery

Surgical facelift is performed under general anesthesia. The duration of the operation depends on the degree of complexity of each specific case. As a rule, it lasts for 1-3 hours. Postoperative recovery in a hospital takes 1-2 days.

The next few weeks of rehabilitation take place at home. The preliminary result is evaluated after two months. Completely the effect of rejuvenating treatment is manifested in six months.

Aging is an inevitable process. And first of all signs of aging appear on the face – the skin ceases to be elastic, stretches, wrinkles appear, nasolabial folds appear, the “second chin” is formed.

Of course, all this looks not very aesthetic, especially on the female face. Fortunately, the possibilities of modern plastic surgery can significantly prolong the youth of the face, make it healthy and attractive.

The facelift procedure in Melbourne is aimed at eradicating the visible signs of skin aging – wrinkles, folds and so on. by removing excess fat tissue, tightening and redistribution of the skin of the face and neck. This procedure is usually performed at the age of 40-50 years. As a rule, indications for surgery are only pronounced skin changes, which are difficult to correct by cosmetic means.

Dr. Bryan Mendelson Suture Facelift

Such indications include deep wrinkles on the cheeks, on the forehead or on the bridge of the nose, the formation of the “second chin”, a noticeable lowering of the skin of the forehead and eyebrows. However, as with almost any other plastic surgery, the task of a facelift is aesthetic, “restore the former beauty”, “look younger for 10 years” want the majority of patients, so this procedure has no strict age limits.

However, there are a number of contraindications related to the general condition of the patient’s body. These include infectious and oncological diseases, serious diseases of internal organs, a violation of blood coagulability.

Before the operation, the specialist will conduct all necessary diagnostic and analysis complex to avoid unforeseen consequences. In addition, again, because a facelift is an “industrial “operation, the plastic surgeon tries to maximize the result to the wishes of the patient, describing the plan and the consequences of the procedure for a few days or even weeks before the operation.

Facelift Plastic Surgery Melbourne

Facelift surgery is performed under general anesthesia, usually it lasts for several hours. Surgical incisions are carried out in the natural folds of the skin, and after surgery, scarring is almost invisible. In addition, in modern plastic surgery, the use of endoscopic methods has become almost mandatory, when the incisions are microscopic and completely heal after a short time after the operation.

There are several different forms of facelift. This can be a facelift only for the face skin (this operation is simple enough, its price is low, but the effect lasts only 2-4 years), partial or complete tightening of the skin and muscles (masticatory muscles, neck muscles, the method is called SMAS facelift and gives a long Effect), often facelift is combined with other types of plastic operations, for example, blepharoplasty.

Laser Facelift Photos

The postoperative period lasts for a long time, depending on how clearly the patient performs the doctor’s instructions, the final result depends, therefore, such instructions should not be neglected.

After surgery, recommended hospital stay is usually 3-4 days, for a week – until the swelling subsides – you have to wear a special compression bandage. Sutures are removed after 7-10 days after the operation – sequentially: first in the area of the auricles, then in the scalp. Of course, during the first time after surgery, any physical exertion is excluded, even to wash your hair and dye your hair should be done with great care.

The final result of the operation is formed at least two months later, when edema subsides and the healing of scars begins. The procedure, which a few years ago only celebrities resorted to, has now become more accessible.
