Will narrowing the nasal bones cause face to look "aged?"
Narrowing nasal bones
May 17, 2019
From your pictures, your nasal bones do not appear to be wide. A smooth transition from brows to nasal tip are recommended. As a result, a more balanced appearance would be achieved by narrowing and creating a more harmonious tip. Only narrowing the dorsum would accentuate the width of your tip and not be desirable in my opinion. 
Will reducing my radix make my nose look too short?
Radix reduction
May 17, 2019
It is difficult to assess from your photographs. An in person evaluation would likely be most beneficial in your case. In the photo of you raising your eyebrows, the lump appears to be arising from the nasal bones, perhaps in the region of the rhinion not the radix. In this case, smoothing the transition between the nasal bones and upper lateral cartilages may be most beneficial. My recommendation is to see a trusted rhinoplasty surgeon near you for an evaluation. 
Do I have a long nasal spine? How involved is it to fix this?
Nasal Spine Question
May 17, 2019
Although the prominence of your nasal spine may be bothering you, simply chiseling this down will not improve the relationship of your nose to your upper lip. You have what is called a "tension nose" caused by tenting of the skin and soft tissues from the top of your nose and septum. To get relaxation of these tissues, you would likely need a reduction of the dorsum combined with tip repositioning. This will create more of an acute columellar-lobule angle.

May 14, 2019

They may want to wait a few more months until your nose is fully healed prior to any intervention.
Will Electric Stimulation Machines Help Firm Facial Muscles?
Facial muscles and their role in facial aging
Mar 3, 2019
Unfortunately, the role of facial muscle atrophy is quite small in relation to some of the other changes in volume and skin laxity. In fact, muscles actually work harder as we age in order to support and create expressions. This over activity is what creates the deeper lines around the mouth as well as in the glabella (between the eyebrows). If you are seeing some changes you want addressed, I would recommend considering a consultation to see what you would be a candidate for. Best of luck. 
Have you ever heard of stitches not being absorbed by the body?
Exposed sutures after necklift
Mar 3, 2019
Sorry to hear that you are having that issue. Prolene sutures are not absorbable and it is possible to have inflammation around a suture that is under the skin. Sometimes if permanent sutures are inflamed or infected, they made need removal in order to correct the issue. You would want to seek a professional opinion with an in-person evaluation to determine the best course of action for you.

Drooping tip following rhinoplasty
Jan 5, 2019

How can I reduce internal scar tissue after a rhinoplasty surgery?
Reducing scarring after rhinoplasty
Dec 12, 2018
Sorry to hear about everything you have been through. Unfortunately this is a tough problem without an easy solution. There is no data regarding prophylactic steroids and reducing scarring in rhinoplasty. I do treat scarring with injections of kenalog rather aggressively in patients that are forming scar tissue. As in every surgery, the risks of proceeding with surgery should be weighed with the benefits to determine whether another surgery is the right choice for you.
