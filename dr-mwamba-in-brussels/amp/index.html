How do you know if you have donor hair left for hair transplant?
Grafts for the future
Apr 7, 2016
A thorough consultation process should involve a proper donor area analysis and density check.  Donor area management is essential as donor resources for hair transplantation are finite.
Can I use unwanted eyebrow hairs to be transplanted into the desired eyebrow area?
Eyebrow hair
Dec 1, 2015
Every patient is unique and has unique needs. Hair transplant surgery is a permanent solution. Eyebrow hair can be removed and implanted to the eyebrow. Follicular unit extraction (FUE) surgery doesn’t produce the linear scar associated with follicular unit transplantation (FUT), however, all surgery produces scarring.My World Hair Transplant Center
I had a hair transplant on September 4. Can I do hard exercises?
Strenuous activity
Sep 30, 2015
Don’t engage in extreme physical exertion if you underwent FUT/strip surgery. Keep the recipient area away from tanning beds, chlorine in swimming pools, and extended sun exposure as well. You are fully invested to maintain a healthy environment for your newly transplanted grafts.Definitely follow the post-op instructions of your doctor.
Can a Hair Transplant Be Used to Create a New Hairline (Not One That Has Receded)?
New hairline design
Nov 24, 2014
Yes, you can achieve this effect by transplanting a new hairline. Each
case is unique, but reconstructing a new hairline will depend on your physical
characteristics (e.g. forehead shape). Some
individuals want conservative hairlines, and some individuals prefer more
aggressive hairline design. Generally
speaking, a new hairline should be designed to meet realistic expectations.
All hairlines should properly frame the face and be natural-looking.
