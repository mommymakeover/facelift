Doctors

Bradford S. Patt, MD

Name: Bradford S. Patt, MD
Statement: A leading physician in Houston, Texas, Dr. Bradford Patt is a board-certified facial plastic surgeon specializing in facial cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries. For 11 consecutive years, Dr. Patt has been listed as one of the “Top Docs®” and “Super Doctors®” in the Houston, Texas area.

With over 28 years in practice, Dr. Patt has become especially well-known for his exemplary results from rhinoplasty, facelifts, and eyelid lifts, as well as nonsurgical facial rejuvenation procedures such as Botox, dermal fillers, Ultherapy skin tightening, and nonsurgical rhinoplasty. He also offers NeoGraft FUE hair transplant surgery.

After receiving his undergraduate degree from Louisiana State University, Dr. Patt earned his medical degree from Louisiana State University’s New Orleans Medical School. From there, he completed two residencies—the first in otolaryngology (head & neck surgery) at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School in Dallas and the second in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery at the University of Illinois in Chicago.

Today, Dr. Patt is double board-certified by the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. He is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (AAFPRS) and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

He and his staff at Houston Center for Facial Plastic Surgery provide personal, individualized care to every patient that walks through their door.
