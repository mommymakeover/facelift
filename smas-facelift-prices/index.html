SMAS Facelift Prices

Here you will find a list of prices for SMAS facelift performed by plastic surgeons. These prices are taken from the reviews on the website realself.com (https://www.realself.com/SMAS-facelift/reviews).

SMAS-facelift is a radical and effective method of surgical skin tightening.

This is a plastic surgery, during which the deeper layers of the facial tissues are touched, than with traditional lifting. During the operation, the doctor not only tightens the skin, but also moves the muscle tissue.

The effect of rejuvenation is achieved at any age, while retaining a natural facial expression, the cut of the eyes and the line of the mouth.

How is SMAS facelift done?

First of all, the surgeon conducts an examination and analyzes which muscles on the face are stretched out, and which are lowered. The task of SMAS facelift is to return the fabrics to their original position. During the operation, the skin moves, and its excess is excised. SMAS facelift fixes fabrics in an ideal position.

SMAS Rhytidectomy Seeks To Improve Facial Rhytidosis

The result of this procedure:

  • Elimination of wrinkles and folds;
  • Restoration of volumes and contours of the face;
  • Struggle with excess skin and sagging of subcutaneous tissues.

Face lifting is performed under general anesthesia. The operation lasts from 2 to 4 hours. For SMAS facial lifting we use modern drugs and anesthetics that do not cause negative side effects. During the operation, the doctor makes a cut along the hair growth line and behind the ear. In these areas scarring is practically not noticeable.

Are there any contraindications to SMAS-face lifting?

Face lifting is contraindicated in people suffering from the following diseases:

  • Acute infections of various types;
  • Diseases of the cardiovascular system;
  • Malignant tumors;
  • psychical deviations;
  • diabetes;
  • Bleeding disorders.

How long does the recovery last after the face lifting?

SMAS Facelift Technique

In the first day after SMAS-lifting the patient needs to take painkillers. Within a few days after surgery, small bruises and swelling can be seen on the face. Ultrasonic, magnetic and microcurrent therapy will help to get rid of them. These procedures are carried out in our clinic.

In the rest, the recovery is quick and imperceptible to the patient. SMAS facelift recovery day by day:

  • On 3-4 days the bandage is removed. After this, a course of restorative procedures for saturation of tissues with oxygen and resorption of scars is prescribed;
  • Seams are removed for 7-10 days. This procedure is almost painless, conducted under the supervision of doctors;
  • In 2 weeks you can evaluate the results of the operation. Edema and bruises gradually go away, the contour of the face becomes delineated;
  • After 2-4 months, the maximum effect occurs. All traces of surgical intervention completely disappear. The timing of recovery depends on the characteristics of the skin tissues and the complexity of the surgical procedure.

SMAS Is An Abbreviation For A Tissue Layer Called The Superficial Muscular Aponeurotic System

SMAS-facelift is a complex procedure that can be entrusted only to a professional plastic surgeon. Under the layer SMAS are the main branches of the facial nerve, responsible for facial expressions. Therefore, any, even the most insignificant mistake of a doctor can lead to irreparable changes.
