How soon after having scalp micro-pigmentation can I have a hair transplant?
Information about SMP and Hair Transplant.
Dec 20, 2019
Scalp micropigmentation is a good way to cover FUT surgery scar in the donor area. As SMP requires more than 1 session to achieve a satisfactory result, each session requires a minimum healing time of 5-7 days in between due to the inflammation of the scalp.
If the SMP is performed on the scalp (not in the donor area to cover the FUT scar), my recommendation would be to wait at least 2 weeks, ideally 1 month, before undergoing HT.
