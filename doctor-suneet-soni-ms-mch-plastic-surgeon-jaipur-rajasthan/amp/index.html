How likely is that I have damaged the follicules of the transplanted hairs?
Shedding of transplanted hair withinf six
May 2, 2015
Shedding of transplanted hair is an almost universal characteristic of a
hair transplantation. This occurs because during hair transplantation a
graft is temporarily stripped of its blood supply. As a response to this, the grafts shed their hair but the root will remain inside. Shedding of transplanted hair is
noticed beginning one week following the procedure and continuing for up
to six weeks. A very small percentage of patients do not shed and the
transplanted hair continues to grow….
