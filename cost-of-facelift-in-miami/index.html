There are wrinkles, crow’s feet in the corners of the lips and eyes, on the forehead the lines turn into deep folds, cheeks drop, grooves in the nasolabial folds are formed, the muscles of the face and neck become weak.

Indication for the operation

  • the formation of wrinkles in the area of cheekbones, temples, forehead;
  • the appearance of deep nasolabial folds and the “second” chin;
  • sagging skin and wrinkles in the neck area;
  • ptosis cheeks lower edge of the mandible (jawls);
  • drooping eyelids, mouth corners and outer corners of the eyes;
  • the appearance of wrinkles in the areas of the bridge of the nose and temporomandibular;
  • the formation of deep vertical wrinkles on the cheeks

Analyzes

For the chosen type of anesthesia (usually – general).

Anesthesia

In most cases, it is performed under general anesthesia, but some operations can be performed under local anesthesia with a mild intravenous sedation. The duration of the operation is from 1.5-2 to 3-4 hours.

Hospital stay is about 1 day.

Preparation before operation

One week prior to surgery, it is necessary to stop taking alcohol and blood-thinning medications (aspirin, hormones).You can not eat or drink anything 5-6 hours before surgery.

The recovery period lasts from a week to a month. You can return to work in 2 weeks. The final result can be estimated in six months. The swelling of the tissues and bruises subside after a few weeks. Make-up can be applied after 10 days. Sutures are removed in some zones for 3-5 days, in others for 11-12 days.

Doctor Marcelo Ghersi Plastic Surgery Of The Face

Scars disappear after 3-4 weeks, and they become completely invisible for 6-12 months.

After operation

About 10 days require wearing special compression mask. During sleep try to raise your head higher than usual. This helps to get rid of fluid and edema faster. It is not recommended to comb your hair for 10 days. Do not strain, abstain from the load, but regularly go out for a walk, this increases the healthy blood circulation. During the month, a solarium and a sauna are contraindicated.
