My doctor has advised me to use Minoxidil after FUE. Should I stop in 8 months?
Minoxidil after Hair Transplantation
Sep 6, 2018
I usually advise my patients to apply minoxidil for six months after the hair transplantation. This increases the blood supply for the new grafts. Sometimes I urge some patients to keep applying it for many many years when I see scalp with decreased blood supply. In addition there are no side effects from the usage.
