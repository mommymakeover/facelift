Arm lift incision, is it possible to avoid the hair bearing skin and glands in armpit/axillary area?
Scars in armlift surgery
Jun 1, 2017
The scar in arm lift surgery extends longitudinally from the apex of the armpit down the inside of the upper arm. It hardly ever crosses the elbow joint into the forearm or reaches the armpit itself. However, following massive weight loss – due to gastric bypass or extreme dieting – all bets are off. In such cases the scars frequently cross these boundaries simply to taper the results and avoid unsightly dog-ears.
