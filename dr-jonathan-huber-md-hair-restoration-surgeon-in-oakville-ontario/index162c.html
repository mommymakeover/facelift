I had IPL beard removal and want my beard back. Is there any way to repair the hair?
You will likely need a transplant.
Jul 4, 2022
IPL uses light and heat to destroy hair follicles to prevent regrowth. If the follicles are successfully destroyed, there is no therapy that can regrow them. You will need a hair transplant to restore density to your beard.

General rule
Jul 4, 2022
As a rule of thumb, it takes about 150 – 200 grafts to lower your hairline by about 1mm. To lower your hairline by 1cm, therefore, might need in the vicinity of 1500 to 2000 grafts.
