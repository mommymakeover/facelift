How long does it take for Botox results to be seen? I got 27 units in my forehead and frown line
Time frame for Botox results
Oct 31, 2018
Botox typically start working within 24-48 hours. Full results are acquired at 2 weeks. If you still see movement at 2 weeks you should make a follow up appointment with your Plastic Surgeon.
How Long Before Botox Results Can Be Seen?
Full results of Botox
Oct 31, 2018
Patients see full results from the Botox injections at a 2 week period of time. Typically patients start to see changes within 24 to 48 hours however. Patients have been getting Botox injections for years with no long term issues. The recommendation is to make an appointment with your Plastic Surgeon for an assessment of short and long term goals.
Heavy lip filler lumps. Will they be resolved after 2 weeks?
Lip lumps as aresult of Dermal fillers
Oct 31, 2018
Dermal Fillers may result in lip lumps but should be rare. Massaging the area may resolve the lumpiness . After vigorous massaging a physician may inject Hyaluronidase to soften the area . The Hyaluronidase should resolve the issue.
