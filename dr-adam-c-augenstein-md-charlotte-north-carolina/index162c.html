Can I get lipo without tummy tuck?
Liposuction with tummy tuck
Oct 16, 2018
Based on the folds you have, liposuction alone would not be sufficient and would leave you with excess skin.  Combining liposuction to the entire midsection, front and back, with a tummy tuck, would be the only way to get the flat stomach you desire.  
What should I do about my big belly?
Belly question
Oct 4, 2018
Ultimately a tummy tuck and liposuction of your abdomen, flanks and love handles would give you a great result and flat belly.  Your best and safest starting point is weight loss prior to this procedure.  Meeting with a board certified plastic surgeon for a consultation can help you figure out the goals and where you need to be prior to surgery.  Best of luck in your journey.  
Would I need a BA or BA with lift to achieve perkier breasts?
Breast augmentation with or without lift
Sep 24, 2018
Thank you for your question...to achieve the result you are looking for you would benefit from both a breast lift and an implant.  Please see a board certified plastic surgeon to review all your options and select an appropriate implant and procedure to achieve your goals.  Best of luck.
Can I have a rhinoplasty with hypothyroidism?
Rhinoplasty and thyroid issues
Sep 24, 2018
Low thyroid levels would not affect a rhinoplasty but would be best to have your thyroid levels regulated appropriately when undergoing general anesthesia.  
Do I need an extended tummy tuck or fleur de lis tummy tuck?
Type of tummy tuck
Sep 24, 2018
An in office examination is beset to determine which type of tummy tuck incision is best to meet your goals, but based on the pictures I believe a fleur de lis type incision is likely unnecessary.  Best of luck. 
Can I still get massages 3 months post-op for lumps?
Massage after tummy tuck
Sep 24, 2018
You can get a massage 3 months after a tummy tuck, but there does appear to be some irregularity on your abdomen.  This won't improve with massage.  Best to be examined and discuss with your plastic surgeon.

Fever after tummy tuck
Sep 24, 2018

IV sedation or General anesthesia for rhinoplasty?
Anesthesia for rhinoplasty
Sep 6, 2018
Either can be used depending on the setting and the particulars of the procedure--in the end its what is safest.  Most of the time that probably is general anesthesia, but again its all about safety. Definitely discuss with your board certified surgeon.  
Is Double Bubble something that can be avoided entirely?
Double bubble after breast augmentation
Sep 6, 2018
Although always a risk when performing a breast augmentation, it can be minimized with appropriate planning and technique.  Lowering the fold definitely increases that risk; therefore, it should only be done in the appropriate circumstances as other techniques must be used in that scenario to minimize that from happening after surgery.  Larger implants definitely increase that risk.  
What type of plastic surgery should I get to fix my sagging stomach? Full/mini tuck?
Mini vs full tummy tuck
Sep 6, 2018
Ultimately it boils down to what do you want the skin around your belly button to look like?  If you want all the excess skin above and below your belly button removed then a full tummy tuck would be necessary.  An in office consultation would be best to examine you to determine the true extent of excess skin as well as muscle tone.  Best of luck.

Closed rhinoplasty
Apr 12, 2018
