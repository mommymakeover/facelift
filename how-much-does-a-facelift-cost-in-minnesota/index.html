How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Minnesota?

A variety of facelift methods in Minnesota are designed for those who want to get rid of the visible signs of aging.

When expressing emotions, talking, in the process of eating, there is a gradual contraction of muscles and, at the same time, stretching and contraction of the skin.

At a young age, until the skin is healthy, elastic and elastic on it there is no trace. Over the years, the skin loses its elasticity and sags wrinkles.

The following age changes can be observed:
– Flabby skin in the middle of the face;
– Notable wrinkles under the lower eyelids;
– Deep folds along the nose and corners of the mouth;
– Sagging cheeks due to loss of muscle tone in the lower part of the face;

– Double chin, which appears even in people with normal weight.

Facelift allows you to reduce wrinkles on the face, rejuvenate the skin around the eyes and improve the condition of the chin and neck.

The most effective are the traditional methods of surgical facelift, while other methods, such as endoscopic or laser facelift help to slow the aging and push back the need for surgical intervention.

The incisions are made during the operation for – or in front of the ears or in the scalp area, the excess skin is removed or contracted. The procedure is performed under general anesthesia. The modern technique of surgical facelift allows to obtain a reliable and durable result.

The most likely risks in facelift

Every year thousands of people turn to facelift surgery in Minnesota. Fortunately complications from operations on the face are rare.

Nevertheless, facelift, even when referring to an experienced plastic surgeon, involves certain risks that you should consider before you decide on an operation:

Doctor Richard H. Tholen Temporary Face Lift

– Adverse effects of anesthesia;
– Infection;
– Hematoma (blood clots under the skin).

It is necessary to follow all the recommendations of the treating surgeon before and after the operation to reduce the degree of risk. For example, treat wounds with an antiseptic, exclude physical stress for better wound healing, do not take medications that promote blood thinning.

Less invasive facelift

If the aging of the skin is not yet serious enough, the skin areas are not removed, and more gentle facelift is applied, for example, with a threaded needle or radio frequency waves (thermage).

Instead of a complete reconstruction of the skin area, Botox injections or fillers, laser resurfacing and microdermabrasion may be suggested. Facelift by the introduction of stem cells is also an alternative to a surgical procedure. All these methods give only a temporary effect and can not fundamentally change the skin condition.

For patients with a satisfactory skin condition, endoscopic and laser facelifts are also intended. During these procedures, skin areas are also not removed or contracted, reconstructive procedures are performed in the subcutaneous or deep layers using a probe or using laser technology. The effect is designed for natural elasticity of the skin.

Endoscopic facelift

Endoscopic facelift in Minnesota is recommended for those who do not have significant skin folds, and its condition is still quite good. This category refers mainly to patients under 50 years of age. Endoscopic facelift can also be useful in hard-to-reach areas (creases between the eyebrows, near the nose and mouth), where traditional plastic surgeries are powerless.

This is the last word in plastic surgery, the operation does not require large incisions, the wounds heal quickly, and the side effects are significantly less than with the traditional facelift. Before deciding on facelift, you need to do the following:

If you are going to lose weight, then this is better done before the facelift;
For a couple of weeks before the procedure should stop smoking.

Smoking reduces the flow of blood to the skin, slows the healing of wounds and increases the risk of infection.

Endoscopic facelift is performed using an endoscope – a tubular probe with a camera, which is inserted through a small incision. The image from the camera is projected onto the screen, and the surgeon observes the projection on the screen during the operation. In the operation, the subcutaneous layers are redistributed.

Dr. Michael C. Fasching Face Lift Is Designed To Remove Signs Of Aging From The Face

Endoscopic facelift surgery is performed under general anesthesia. In the operation, several small incisions are performed in the part where correction is not required. For example, with a neck lift, the incisions can be hidden under the chin and behind the ears.

With the help of the endoscope, not only facelift is performed, but also the implantation. Simultaneously with facelift, it is possible to perform additional procedures:

  • Removal of bags under the eyes;
  • Liposuction in the zone of the chin and neck;
  • Installation of implants in the cheeks or chin;
  • Laser skin resurfacing;
  • blepharoplasty the upper eyelid.

Although the operation takes three or more hours (especially with additional treatments) for most patients it is conducted on an outpatient basis. During the week, as a rule, the patient is ready to return to light work. Physical loads are resolved after a couple of weeks.

Bruising and swelling after face lifting are still possible, but to a much lesser extent. The risk of nerve damage is much lower, because the incisions are small. Sutures are removed after about a week.

Agreeing to the operation, it is necessary to remember that facelift is a surgical intervention, and it entails certain risks.

It is also inevitable that, although the endoscopic facelift surgery rejuvenates the face, it is unable to stop the time, and the skin after the procedure continues to age gradually.

Laser Skin Tightening

Laser Skin Tightening uses laser energy for facelift – a focused light flux. It can be produced in any areas, including hard-to-reach areas. This is a non-surgical procedure, therefore it has a number of advantages over other methods of facelifts.

Laser Face Lift With Doctor John Allan Ness

Advantages Laser Skin Tightening

  • The depth of treatment of the subcutaneous layers during laser facelifting can be controlled;
  • The laser simultaneously acts on the collagen matrix and improves the appearance of the skin;
  • Do not require general anesthesia and associated health risks;
  • No incisions are made, scars, bruises are virtually excluded;
  • The procedure time and recovery time after surgery are less.
  • The main complications can be considered a minor swelling, which quickly disappears; in some patients, hyperpigmentation may occur in the treatment zone.
  • It is recommended for a long time, up to six months, to avoid exposure to sunlight to reduce the risk of persistent hyperpigmentation.
  • Laser facelift is effective for patients with light slightly pigmented skin. Dark acne or sensitive skin, the presence of skin infections are a contraindication.
