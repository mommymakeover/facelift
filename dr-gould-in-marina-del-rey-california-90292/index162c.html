Statement: Dr. Daniel Gould, MD is a board certified plastic surgeon practicing in Beverly Hills, California. He artfully performs many surgeries, including rhinoplasty, breast enhancement, tummy tucks, and liposuction, but is best known for the natural and beautiful results of his deep plane facelifts.

He is a pioneer in Drainless Tummy Tuck a technique which gets rid of drains and decreases down time while providing superior results which look more natural because of the work done on the inside. He has written over five publications on this technique dating back to 2013- and continues to contribute giving talks at the national meetings on this subject and on deep plane facelift to teach other doctors how to perform them successfully.

What are the indications for either a Deep Plan or a SMAS?
Answer is simple trianing
May 25, 2022
Basically, Deep plane is always the approach I use. There are few exceptions, and I have trained in all methods. Facelift 3.0 is all about releasing the deeper structures for better results.
Will losing 20lb affect my deep plane facelift results?
Definitely should lose weight before
May 25, 2022
Weight loss before surgery is a good idea, in fact it will dramatically improve your recovery and it will improve your ultimate results at the ideal weight you ultimately maintain.
Congenital symmastia fix?
not really a good fix
May 30, 2021
Many of the options are not the best. Internal bras help lift and shape but usually they are used to push things in and up. The best solutions for your case are to recreate space in between.
Weight loss- before or after breast augmentation?
always before
May 30, 2021
ANY good doctor will always say losing weight makes surgery safer for so many reasons. You should always be at your ideal weight before surgery as it also makes the result the best.
Are my breasts saggy or tubular? Is there a way to fix them without an implant?
yes
May 30, 2021
A lift and a reduction will allow the reshaping of your breasts to make them more perky and better shaped.
Can I achieve more 'upper pole fullness' and a rounded shape. Can this be achieved with implan
hard to say
May 30, 2021
Your photos are at an angle which makes it hard to judge. You need to submit true lateral shots and a front shot from far away. This allows us to see the nipple position and to decide if it is at the level of the mid humerus and if the nipple is at or above the imf.
Am I safe to get my breast augmentation 1 month after having Covid?
no
May 30, 2021
Data shows you should wait at least 6 weeks, in order to reduce the risk of deadly complications from surgery. This is not something to risk!
Is dual plane placement still a possibility if I choose the inframammary fold incision?
Yes
May 30, 2021
Dual plane can be performed through ANY incision. However imf is the best to reduce the risk of capsular contracture.
Breast lift with implants or implants alone?
no other option
May 30, 2021
Sorry! the best option for you is to do a lift and augmentation. If you don't lift the breasts will be heavy and low and will look odd and make you look heavy. you need a lift and that requires a scar.
How much time does the nipple need to heal to close up from a removal of a nipple piercing bef
about two - four weeks
May 30, 2021
Depending on your body, most take between two to four weeks to fully heal on the outside tho healing continues on. the inside for up to three months. It is reasonable to remove the piercings before at least two weeks.
Can I do BA and Rhinoplasty 10 days apart?
Yes
May 30, 2021
I think it is ok to have two surgeries in a row, The breast augmentation should be first. Any Plastic surgeon that could do a rhinoplasty could do a breast augmentation at the same time. There are plenty of great doctors that do both and can do both at the same time. That being said it makes sense to do to breast first and then do the rhinoplasty.
Do I have tuberous breasts?
There is some degree of breast constriction
May 30, 2021
You don't have a tuberous breast however you do have some level of constriction of the lower pole. Your breasts have a short distance to the imf and relative to the nipple size it isn't quite correct.
Can I achieve full, round, natural breasts through breast augmentation?
absolutely !
May 30, 2021
A natural shape and size is possible! You need biometric sizing with an approach to measure your breast base width and to provide an appropriate shape and size for your body. I would suggest likely 255 MPP Sientra but any where from 180-265 cc depending on the base width.
Follow-up question: To lift or not?
lift
May 30, 2021
Lift will improve the shape and address the nipples but you could always do the augmentation first. It really is a personal question
What type of implant is best for lifting and adding upper pole fullness?
I agree with you
May 30, 2021
Maybe not high profile but moderate plus profile certainly. Shaped implants do not give any benefit and they potentially create complications If they rotate. I attached a scientific article supporting this concept. My advice is go with a round implant. the best way to get a lift is actually to have a lift though!
