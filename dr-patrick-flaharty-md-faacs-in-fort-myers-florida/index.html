Statement: Dr. Patrick Flaharty is a board-certified oculoplastic surgeon and the medical director of Azul Cosmetic Surgery Medical Spa, with locations in Bonita Springs, Naples, and Fort Myers, Florida.

Trained as an ophthalmologist and facial cosmetic surgeon, Dr. Flaharty specializes exclusively in procedures for the face, neck, and delicate tissues of the eyes, including eyelid surgery, facelift, brow lift, neck lift, laser skin rejuvenation, and Botox and dermal filler injections. Throughout nearly 30 years in practice, he has performed over 20,000 facial plastic and cosmetic procedures.
