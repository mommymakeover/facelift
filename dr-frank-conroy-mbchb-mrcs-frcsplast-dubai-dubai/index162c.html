PIH problem

I’m sorry to hear about your issues !

Has my anatomical implant turned?
Implant rotation
Dec 4, 2018
It does look as though the implant has rotated, although the deformity could be due to capsular contraction. Rotation of anatomical implants is relatively uncommon but is a recognized complication when using these implants, they also have an increased risk of BIA-ALCL due to the aggressive texturing used on them. For these reasons I do not use anatomical implants.
