How many syringes of filler is needed in the temple area?
Filler
Jul 8, 2019
There are many factors that can change the amount of filler you will need. Often we use 1-2 per side. If your injector is using a deep injection technique you will need more than a superficial technique with a cannula. Radiesse is not a filler we use in our practice in this area as it is not as safe as an HA filler. Best of luck to you.

Neck

Can I get the Halo procedure on my face for acne scars if I have a tan on my face?
Halo
Jul 8, 2019
We ask that our patients stay out of the sun for 2 weeks prior and 2 weeks after their laser treatment. If you do go out zinc/titanium sunscreen and hats are a must. I am on blood thinners and 80 mg aspirin. Will IPL bruise me?
IPL
Jul 8, 2019
Any time you are on blood thinners you are more likely to bruise. That being said, bruising is not a common side affect of IPL. The light/heat does not break the dermis, thus unlikely to bruise. Please be sure to let your provider know all the medications you are taking. Best of luck to you!
