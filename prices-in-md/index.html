How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Maryland?

Facelift is an operation known from ancient times and has a long history. The founders of facelift surgery are rightly German and French surgeons Lexer (1906), Hollander (1912) and Joseph (1921).Â The principles of facelift at that time were limited to subcutaneous preparation, elliptical excision and elevation (lifting) of the skin exclusively. As a rule, this was accompanied by a short-term results and, often, the formation of an explicit effect of the “operated face”. This, in turn, created a negative evaluation of the facelift.

The social renaissance of the 60s and 70s of the last century led to further progress and recognition of aesthetic facial surgery, which stimulated scientific developments in this field and the exchange of opinions leading to the improvement of surgical techniques and improvement of results.

Doctor A. Dean Jabs Face Plastic Surgery Candidate

The first major contribution was made by Skoog, P.Tessier (1974), and Mitz and Peyronie (1976) who discovered and described the superficial muscular-fascial system (SMAS). Since then, to achieve the duration of the results and a more natural face, many technical modifications of SMAS facelift have been developed.

The subsequent study of aesthetic analysis and proportions of the face made it possible to distinguish conditionally the upper, middle and lower third. Surgical progress and the development of the technical base have created the possibility of correcting these zones both in isolation and in combination.

Thus, in the list of rejuvenating operations on the face appeared such as: forehead lift (frontal lift), eyebrow lift and eye outer angle (temporal lift), midface face lift, neck lift, etc.

Modern facelift in MD is a large section of plastic surgery, which includes several stages. At the first stage, the surgeon needs to analyze the face, including age, sex, type of structure, ethnicity and personality of each patient.

Dr Dean P. Kane Plastic Surgery On Face

An important role is played by the definition of face proportions, symmetry, harmony, as well as understanding the age-related changes on the face.

The second stage, directly surgical, allows the surgeon, based on a carefully conducted analysis of the face, to choose the appropriate tactics, accesses and the amount of surgical intervention for each individual patient.

Facelift in Maryland is a large arsenal of surgical techniques performed as independent operations, and in combination with each other, or with other surgical and / or non-surgical manipulations.

Dr. Franklin D. Richards Mid Face Lift Photo Result

All this allows you to recreate the lost volume of soft tissues, eliminate age-related changes, recreate harmony and, what’s more important, preserve the natural look of the face.

The use of video endoscopy equipment allows for surgical interventions with minimal incisions on the skin, and the use of innovative methods of tightening and fixing soft tissues allows to reduce the operational trauma, shorten the recovery period and prolong the result.

Dr Ira D. Papel Smas Lifting Recovery

One of the innovative techniques of modern facelift is the Endotine device technology, based on the use of absorbable plates that have the property of multipoint fixation to soft tissues.
