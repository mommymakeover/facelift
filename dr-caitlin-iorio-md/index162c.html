Would fillers help? I feel like my cheeks have lost volume and are sagging
You’re an excellent candidate for cheek augmentation
Apr 19, 2023
You are an excellent candidate for cheek augmentation. The volume loss we experience is due to bone loss and fat loss. Fat can be liposuctioned from your abdomen or other areas of the body and can be injected to more permanently augment the cheeks whereas filler provides temporary relief but allows you to see what a permanent result would be like. The augmentation will also with the mild lines you see around your mouth as well. I hope this helps!
