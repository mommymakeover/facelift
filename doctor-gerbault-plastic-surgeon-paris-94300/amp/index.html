Tip of my nose hangs down when I smile – What procedure do you recommend to fix this?
Bulbous tip
Dec 15, 2021
Hello, your tip is mainly too bulky, wide, giving this bulbous appearance. Therefore, the tip, supratip, infratip and columella are unbalanced with the rest of the noseA tip reshaping combined with the restoration of a good tip support (to avoid distortion when smiling) should easily fix this appearance. Beware that trimming only the tip to debulk it may create deformities. Structural reshaping of the tip should be more appropriateGood luck for the future

How can I stop my nose from flaring when I smile?
Alar flaring and widening when smiling
Dec 6, 2021
Hi, alar widening and flaring while smiling, with a tip that drops and gets wider is a common phenomenon when there’s a lack of strong tip support. The muscle that contracts when you smile stretch the tip and the nasal base tissues, creating this widening and drooping.Botox can decrease slightly the consequences, but the best and long-lasting way to fix this is to reinforce the tip supports during a rhinoplasty.
