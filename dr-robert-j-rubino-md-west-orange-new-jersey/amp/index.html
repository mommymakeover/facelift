Can I talk to the doctor before labiaplasty surgery and ask to get the clitoris area tightened and
Don’t wait until the last minute
May 31, 2019
Any request to change the planned procedure should not be done the day of surgery if possible, especially for elective surgery. Proper protocol requires these changes are requested in the low stress setting in the doctor’s office. There true informed consent can be given. It is not fair to the patient or physician to make changes right before surgery.
I am considering vaginoplasty and /or perineoplasty. Is there an advantage to having these surgeri
Laser is more for less invasive approach
May 31, 2019
Laser treatment such as Femilift or MonaLisa are geared toward restoring the healthy vaginal tissue for patients with vagina dryness, painful sex, urinary frequency and in some cases of mild incontinence. Vaginalplasty and perineal place involve excision of skin with a scalpel, although a laser can be used as a ‘scalpel’ there is no advantage of one tool over the other during these procedures.

It may be a keloid formation

Based on your description, it sounds like you may be forming a keloid. A keloid is basically an over-formed scar reaction, where the scar is raised. Some individuals are prone to them. I would see your surgeon to evaluate.
