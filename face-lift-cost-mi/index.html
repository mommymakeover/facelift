Face Lift Cost Michigan

Facelift in Michigan is a plastic surgery aimed at smoothing wrinkles, nasolabial folds and other age-related changes by lifting the underlying muscles, redistributing the skin and excising its excess.

After this operation, the skin of the face, neck and decollete region is noticeably smoothed, the muscles will be tightened, and the aesthetic fat deposits will disappear.

General anesthesia is used during facelift surgery.

The main indication for facelift are age changes – loss of elasticity, wrinkles and sagging tissues.

With the facelift can be eliminate on the face of the following signs of aging:

  • Smooth nasolabial folds;
  • To eliminate wrinkles on the forehead, in the nose region and the so-called “crow’s feet” in the corners of the eyes;

    • Best Non Surgical Face Lift With Dr. Giancarlo Zuliani

  • Give the facial contours;
  • Remove the “double” chin that appeared with age;
  • Correcting the puffiness and flabbiness of the cheeks;
  • remove age overhanging eyebrows above the eyes;
  • Restore skin tone and remove wrinkles in the neck.

A facelift is technically complex, requires experience and skill, labor-intensive work. It can only be done by the most experienced surgeons!

  • Contraindications
  • Severe diseases of internal organs,
  • Infectious diseases,
  • Oncological diseases,
  • Poor blood clotting.

Facelift in Michigan gives the best and longest result when performed at the first signs of aging (slight flabbiness of the skin of the cheeks and neck, a few wrinkles), and they usually appear on the face after 35 years old.

Facelift is one of the longest operations. This is explained by the large number of tasks the surgeon has to deal with. On average, facelift lasts 2-3.5 hours.

Recovery

Postoperative recovery, depending on the volume of the correction done, ranges from 7 days to 4 weeks.

Facelift and neck: frequently asked questions.

Doctor Homaira (Myra) Danish O Therapy Facelift

Can I do a partial facelift? What are its features?

The surgeon can pull up individual areas of the face, where the tissues are particularly sagging. Features of a local facelift are that sagging of tissues is eliminated only where necessary. This is achieved by removing and tightening overgrown skin or adding lost volumes of soft tissues!

How to change facial features after a facelift?

Facelift is aimed just at eliminating age-related face changes! The common distinctive features of the face remain the same.

Is there a chance of paralysis, full or partial?

Such a probability exists in 3-5% of cases with a very deep suspender! An experienced surgeon always avoids interference in the areas of the facial and trigeminal nerves!

How many times can I do this operation?

At what age is this operation will be effective?
