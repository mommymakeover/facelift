2 months post-op forehead reduction. How long should I wait to get a hair transplant?
Hair transplantation after forehead reduction surgery
Jun 27, 2022
I have heard doctors say that 4 months is enough time to wait to have hair transplantation after a forehead reduction surgery. It is always better to know the final outcome of a surgery and the scar it produced before doing any corrective work in that same area. It would not hurt to wait another 6 months before planning a hair transplant following a forehead reduction surgery.
