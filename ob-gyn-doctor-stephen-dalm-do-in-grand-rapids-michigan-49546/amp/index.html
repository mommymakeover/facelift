How many diVa treatments are recommended?
How many treatments are recommended
Sep 21, 2017
The recommended treatment plan for patients who complain of vaginal dryness, bladder leakage, pain with intercourse and other similar issues is three treatments spaced at 4-6 week intervals for best results. However, each person is unique and may require more or less depending upon the issues we are addressing. For example, we see many cancer survivors who cannot use estrogen therapy for vaginal dryness. These patients may need more treatments to maximize results.
