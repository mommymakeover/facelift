Is 40 Too Young for a Facelift?
Is 40 too young for a facelift?
Mar 4, 2021
40 years old is not too young for a facelift if your aging changes will benefit from a lift. I have performed many facelifts, often mini-facelifts, in this age group. The results can be very natural and long lasting. Remember, however, that no one procedure solves everything in regards to aging. You may still need skin resurfacing procedures for wrinkling and photo-damage, and fillers for volume loss.

How Long Should Swelling and Numbness Last After a Facelift?
Swelling and numbness after a facelift
Mar 4, 2021
The majority of swelling after a facelift resolves in the first 2-3 weeks, however there is some residual swelling that persists for 4 months or more. This swelling often occurs over the cheekbone (malar) area and beneath the chin ( submental). The swelling from fat injections is often in the 4 month range. Numbeness for 4 to 6 months after a facelift is typical and entirely normal.
