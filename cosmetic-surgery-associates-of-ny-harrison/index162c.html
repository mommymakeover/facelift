Samuel Beran, MD

Name: Samuel Beran, MD
Statement: Dr. Samuel Beran is a leading plastic surgeon in New York who specializes in surgeries of the breast, face, and body. He is the founder of the Samuel J. Beran Cosmetic Surgery Associates of New York, which has locations in New York City, and in Westchester and Putnam counties, including Harrison, Valhalla, and Newburgh.

With over 20 years in practice, Dr. Beran is particularly noted for his expertise in breast enhancements—including augmentation, breast lift, reconstruction, reduction, and implant revision—and body contouring with surgical, minimally invasive, and noninvasive methodologies. This includes Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL), liposuction, tummy tucks, EmSculpt, and CoolSculpting.

In addition, he offers a broad range of minimally invasive and surgical facial rejuvenation procedures, from facelifts, rhinoplasty, and eyelid surgery to chemical peels, Botox, and dermal fillers.

“I truly feel what makes me unique is the personalized care, individual attention, and natural results I give my patients,” says Dr. Beran. “Every person is different, and their goals and desired outcomes from plastic surgery are unique to them. I take the time to thoroughly understand what each of my patients wants, and explain to them their surgery options and best possible results.”

Dr. Beran received his undergraduate degree from Union College in Schenectady, New York, and then went on to earn his medical degree at Albany Medical College in Albany, New York. Afterward, he completed two residencies: one in General Surgery at the Thomas Jefferson University Hospital in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and a second in Plastic Surgery at the University of Texas Southwestern in Dallas, Texas.

In addition to being board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, he is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS), and a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

With decades of experience and a comprehensive understanding and command of today’s most advanced techniques and technologies, Dr. Beran is able to deliver exceptional results. His patients have described him as “a doctor I can trust,” “detail oriented and thorough,” and someone who “works hard to make sure that your needs are carefully addressed and that you are comfortable moving forward.”

Similarly, his staff is noted for their professionalism, thoroughness, kindness, and eagerness to help make patients feel comfortable, educated, and wholly prepared for their treatment.

In addition to working with patients, Dr. Beran is a contributing author of Selected Readings in Plastic Surgery and has lectured on breast augmentation, liposuction, and facial rejuvenation.

To begin your aesthetic journey and finally address the concerns you’ve put on hold, schedule a consultation with Dr. Beran at one of his New York City, Westchester, or Putnam County plastic surgery centers.
