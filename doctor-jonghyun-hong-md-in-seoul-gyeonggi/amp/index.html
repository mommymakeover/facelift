What filler should I get for a fuller face?
Fat grafting is the answer.
Nov 4, 2021
Using filler on lip is totally fine, because even using Restylane, Juvederm like, longer last filler if you get it on lip, it will eventually absorbed. However I’ve seen so many complaints regarding under eye, forehead, cheek filler with change shape, leftover filler, infection and etc. Specially on lower eyes, it’s one of the common area where people dissolve injected filler due to dissatisfaction. This is why there is a procedure called ‘fat transplant’ as safer material, natural result…
