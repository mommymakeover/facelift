Is it operator error for my cheek implant to be floating in my face?
Implant migration
Nov 27, 2023
Implants are placed by creating a pocket and sliding the implant into that pocket over the desired area of augmentation.
Some surgeons place the implant into a snug pocket to avoid migration. If the pocket is too large, the implant can slide out of place. If it’s too small, there can be too much pressure on the area, and the implant can extrude through the incision site.
To avoid this, many surgeons fixate the implant with 2-point fixation to avoid migration and rotation.

What is the best option to fix sagging skin after neck liposuction?
Sagging Neck Skin
Nov 20, 2023
As we get older, we lose elasticity in our skin. Having liposuction alone, without any energy-based devices used, in patients (generally over 40, as a rule of thumb) tends to leave skin laxity.

Depending on the amount of laxity, you may have a couple options.
Less invasive options such as RF-microneedling may suffice.
Next level up treatment may include energy such as lasers or plasma to tighten the skin.
If there is too much laxity, then a formal neck lift may be required.
