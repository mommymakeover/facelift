I am interested in minimum neck lift but confused about all the different options. Advice?
Neck lift vs lower facelift
Jan 18, 2021
You are a great candidate for both.
Lower facelift scar starts in front of your ears and goes to behind your ears. It address your jawline, jawls and under the chin laxity.
Necklift scar is limited to behind your ears. It cannot improve your jawline or jowls but only the area under the chin and jawline.

Coolscilting multiple treatments, cost can add up to more than lipo but you can do it in increments of 1 hr a week.
