Do I Have Retracted Columella or Hanging Alar (Aside from the Bulbous Nasal Tip)? What Are the Be
Hanging ala easy to fix if done correctly
Oct 14, 2013
The photos are dark but it looks more like you have hanging ala than retracted columella. This is common in the Asian nose. Not that many doctors do it, but it is quite simple to fix the hanging ala with a direct removal of tissue in the rim of the nostril. This can be done with local anesthesia, and sutures are removed about 3-4 days. Scars are essentially invisible most of the time. This helps the balance between the ala and columella on the lateral view.

The most unusual thing about your problem is that you just have it on one side, because usually the earlobe shape is dependent on the surgeon, not on the healing.
