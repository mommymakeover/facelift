Doctors

P. Daniel Ward, MD

Name: P. Daniel Ward, MD
Statement: Dr. P. Daniel Ward is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon practicing in Salt Lake City, Utah. His expertise covers a wide range of facial cosmetic surgery with a specialized focus on rhinoplasty, face and neck lifts, and eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty). He is also well versed in facial reconstruction procedures.

“I chose facial plastic surgery because it combines artistry with science and engineering principles,” says Dr. Ward. “I love to be able to combine my artistic sense in this way, to allow a patient's natural beauty shine through.”

He chose to specialize in facial plastic surgery “because the face is our identity.” The goal of every procedure at Form Spa, is beautifully simple: “we help patients improve their lives by inspiring confidence,” Dr. Ward explains. “Whether we are taking down the hump on the nose in a rhinoplasty, improving jowls with a facelift, or treating droopy eyelids with a blepharoplasty, we are working to create the best possible version of the patient and allow their true self shine through.”

Dr. Ward takes pride in a level of aesthetic sense that sets his practice apart. He also points to their “down-to-earth approach, in combination with top-level service, results, and education, to provide the best possible experience for our patients.” This combination has fueled an international reputation for excellence that draws patients from across the U.S. and beyond.

After graduating from University of Utah Medical School, Dr. Ward was selected for a world-renowned program at the University of Michigan. He then went on to pursue fellowship training in facial plastic surgery at the University of Michigan, later serving on the faculty as a clinical lecturer. This is where he refined his skills in rhinoplasty, facelift, eyelift, and injectables procedures. Afterward, he returned to the University of Utah to join the faculty there, introducing various innovative facial plastic surgery techniques that had not yet been performed in or around Utah.

Dr. Ward is revered for his vast medical knowledge, artistic surgical skills, and passion for helping his patients find a renewed sense of self-confidence and happiness. He is a sought-after plastic surgeon in Utah, consistently placing in the top 1% of physicians in patient satisfaction.

He has received many awards and honors, including being named one of the Top Doctors in America, one of America’s Best Physicians for the past five years, and a Castle Connolly Top Doctor six years running. He has also received a Resident Research Award, been named one of the Best of Salt Lake City Plastic Surgery for the past six years, and been recognized as a RealSelf 100 Doctor.

Dr. Ward belongs to the University of Utah School of Medicine Alumni Board, the Founder’s Club of the American Academy of Facial Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, the Rhinoplasty Society, and the 1887 Society, which recognizes commitment to facial plastic surgery.

Dr. Ward also serves on various national committees via the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery. He is board-certified by both the American Board of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology, Head and Neck Surgery.
