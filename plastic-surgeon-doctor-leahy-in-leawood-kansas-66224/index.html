What is the difference between pseudoptosis and ptosis in regard to the breasts?
ptosis
Dec 4, 2019
Thank you for your question. Ptosis refers to "drooping" of the breast (or any structure, for that matter). Usually, "pseudo-ptosis" refers to a breast shape where a majority of the breast gland tissue has descended below the crease under the breast (inframammary fold), but the nipple itself remains above the majority of that line. They each have different ways of addressing the problem surgically. I hope that helps a little.
