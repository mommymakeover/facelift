hair growth at 6 months

Hair fall after FUE; is this normal?
loss of transplanted hair after a procedure.
Nov 7, 2016
Its normal to loose the transplanted hair 1-3 weeks after a procedure. The hair typically comes back 2-4 months after the procedure. The full result can be expected 9-12 months after the procedure.
