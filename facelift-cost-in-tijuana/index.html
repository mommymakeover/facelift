Types of facelift

  • SMAS facelift
  • Endoscopic facelift
  • Platysmaplasty
  • Rhytidectomy

SMAS facelift

SMAS facelift (Superficial Musculo-Aponeurotic System) is the most popular to date aesthetic operation on the face. It is performed through the incision in the region of the hairy part of the temple, in front of the auricle, along the border of the scalp in the neck area.

During operation, the surface myofascial layer exfoliates, moves to the desired position and is fixed. Excess skin of the face or neck is removed, after which intradermal sutures are applied.

The operation fixes the muscular frame of the face, which allows to save a new facial contour for a long time. The effect of SMAS facelift is a visual rejuvenation for 10-15 years.

Endoscopic facelift

Endoscopic facelift is performed through micro incisions in the area of the hairline and lower eyelids. It is used to tighten the upper or middle third of the face. Endoscopic facelift is a minimally invasive technique. After the operation is almost no trace. It is often combined with blepharoplasty.

Platysmaplasty

Platysmaplasty eliminates the sagging of the neck muscles (“turkey” neck). Platism is a wide paired neck muscle, the age stretch of which deforms the face oval.

The lift of the platism returns the natural form of the cheekbones, prevents the further ptosis of the soft tissues. The operation can be combined with liposuction – removing fat from the chin area.

Rhytidectomy

During the process of rhytidectomy, excess skin is removed, the skin is stretched, causing wrinkles to be smoothed out. It is carried out with mild ptosis. The drawback is the fragility of the result, because the correction is carried out only by the skin displacement, without affecting ligaments and tissue.

Contraindications to facelift.

  • Acute inflammatory diseases
  • Chronic diseases of internal organs in severe form

Features of the operation facelift

The operation is performed under intravenous and local anesthesia. The duration of the intervention is 3-5 hours. After the facelift, the patient is in the hospital for up to two days.

After facelift surgery in Tijuana, a bandage is applied to the face, which is recommended to be worn for 3-5 days. After the surgery, possible hematoma, swelling of soft tissues of the face, which spontaneously resolve within two weeks. The sutures are removed 10-11 days after the operation. The final result of facelift can be assessed 3-4 months after the operation.
