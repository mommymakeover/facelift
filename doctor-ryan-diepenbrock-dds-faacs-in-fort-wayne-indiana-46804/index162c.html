Chipmunk Cheeks Surgery – what other solutions are non invasive?
Don’t be hard on yourself.
Mar 6, 2020
Full voluptuous cheeks are an asset not a liability. Often times, one’s perception of full checks is the result of large cheek muscles (masseter muscles), excess cheek fat, a small chin, and flat jaw line.
Both surgical and non-surgical options are available. Surgical procedures can range from small to large depending on the issue.
Seek a consultation with someone extremely knowledgeable in facial harmony and balance.
Why Would A Surgeon Use An Endotine Fixation Device For An Endocscopic Brow Lift?
Endotine fixation
Oct 26, 2017
As mentioned elsewhere, there are numerous ways to fix an endoscopic brow lift. Sutures, bone tunnels, endotines, and screws. I personally use Endotines because they are broad. Suture fixation pulls and fixates one small area of the brow, while Endotines are wide and grab/secure more tissue. In my hands, Endotines provide a more natural look and are relatively easy to use.
It is a personal preference.
