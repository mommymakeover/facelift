Porcelain veneers or crowns? Which would be the best for me considering I grind my teeth in m
Functional and Aesthetic correction – balanced approach!
Jun 5, 2019
You should really think about gum correction. A smile looks great only if there is balance of white and pink. You would need gingivectomy (crown lengthening). Veneers is a very good option. It is suggested to check occlusion and do equilibration before bonding the veneers. Still would suggest you to wear a night guard after the veneer treatment.
Crowns are more invasive than veneers. Hence, not recommended in your case just for aesthetic correction.

I have badly stained teeth. Will bleaching remove stains on my molars?
Your molar tooth has cavity !
Jun 5, 2019
Your molar tooth that appears to be heavily stained is actually decayed. Please get an X-ray done for evaluation. If the decay has not reached pulp(nerve of the tooth), then a filling can be done.
You can also consider oral prophylaxis (teeth cleaning). Fissures of the back teeth (molars) usually have some stains, not necessarily it’s because of a decay.
I would not advice you to crown your front teeth. You can achieve a good smile with Teeth Whitening and veneers.
