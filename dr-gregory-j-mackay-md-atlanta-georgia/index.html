Removing the capsule from silicone implants and its relation to ALCL
Feb 12, 2019
The current lifetime risk for the development of ALCL is quoted as anywhere from 1/3,800-1/ 30,000, for women with textured implants. Common symptoms include breast enlargement, pain, asymmetry or a large fluid collection typically lasting more than a year. ALCL patients seem to develop an allergic reaction to the texturing around the implants. Therefore it is recommended that the implant and capsule be completely removed.

Neck Firming Options
sagging turkey neck
Jul 26, 2016
The sagging turkey neck is the result usually of both loose skin and and laxity in the muscles of the neck. The only way to correct this deformity is by surgical tightening of the muscles and removal and tightening of the skin.. There are no non surgical procedures to correct this deformity.
