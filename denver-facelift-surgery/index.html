It should be noted that a facelift is not only a surgical intervention, but also a whole prepared set of exercises, that are designed to correct problem areas, especially effective at the initial stages of their formation.

There are several subspecies of facelift, depending on the complexity of the work, the level of skin aging and genetic predisposition. There are procedures of varying degrees of complexity, some of them are performed even without surgical intervention.

Of course, the most effective is a traditional facelift in Denver, the effect of the procedure is usually maintained for 5-10 years, provided proper care and supportive procedures. The traditional procedure is a standard procedure: incisions are made before or behind the auricles, which can reach the parietal part of the head.

Contraindications for the procedure:

  • Chronic diseases of the cardiovascular system.
  • Allergic reactions to a certain group of medicines;
  • Inflammation of facial tissues, radiotherapy in the face.
  • Diseases of the bronchi and lungs, chronic diseases of the liver and kidneys.

The postoperative consequences, which must be taken into account before, include all kinds of hematomas in the face and neck area, infections, as well as the adverse effects of anesthesia. That’s why you need to comply with all the doctor’s recommendations, treat wounds with antiseptics, drink prescribed medications and not take medications that promote blood thinning, for example, aspirin.

Recovery period

Usually, the entire rehabilitation period is 25-30 days, but full skin shrinkage may occur for several more months, so do not make premature conclusions about your new appearance.

Usually, the sutures are removed on the 10-16th day after the operation, by this time all the swelling and bruises begin to come down.
