Types of facelift

  • SMAS facelift
  • Endoscopic facelift
  • Rhytidectomy
  • platysma

SMAS facelift

SMAS facelift (Superficial Musculo-Aponeurotic System) is the most popular to date aesthetic operation on the face. It is performed through the incision in the region of the hairy part of the temple, in front of the auricle, along the border of the scalp in the neck region.

During the SMAS facelift, the surface myofascial layer exfoliates, moves to the desired position and is fixed. Excess skin of the face or neck is removed, after which intradermal sutures are applied. The operation fixes the muscular frame of the face, which allows to save a new circuit for a long time. The effect of SMAS facelift is a visual rejuvenation for 10-15 years.

Endoscopic facelift.

Endoscopic facelift is performed through micro incisions in the area of the hairline and lower eyelids. Applicable for tightening the upper or middle third of the face. Endoscopic facelift is a minimally invasive technique. After the operation, there are practically no traces left. It is often combined with blepharoplasty.

Lift platysma

The operation eliminates the sagging of the neck muscles (the “turkey” neck). Platysma is a broad pair of neck muscles, stretching the age which deforms the shape of the face. Platysma lift returns the natural shape of the cheeks prevents further lowering of the soft tissue. The operation can be combined with liposuction – removing fat from the chin area.

Rhytidectomy

During the process of rhytidectomy, excess skin is removed, the skin is stretched, causing wrinkles to be smoothed out. It is carried out with mild ptosis. The disadvantage is the fragility of the result, since correction is carried out only by shifting the skin, without affecting the ligament and tissue.

Features of facelift surgery.

The operation is performed under intravenous and local anesthesia. The duration of the intervention is 3-5 hours. After the facelift, the patient is in the hospital for up to two days.

After facelifting in Las Vegas, a bandage is applied to the face, which is recommended to be worn for 3-5 days. Possible hematomas, swelling of the soft tissues of the face after surgery, which take place within two weeks. The sutures are removed 10-11 days after the operation. The final result of facelift can be assessed 3-4 months after the operation.
