I have no upper tooth display. Will a facelift help?
tooth display
Dec 7, 2021
Hello Genjam, your pictures show a nice and normal perioral situation. If people get older, the upper lips elongate and show no teeth when smiling. For those we can offer an upper lip lift (bullhorn), but your upper lips are not too long. If we would shorten it, a gummy smile could be the result, which people often do not like. Your face is beautiful as it is.
What can done for my droopy eyelids?
Correction of levator function
Dec 7, 2021
You should see an eye specialist to check the muscle function of your levator muscle. If you have a ptosis, this can be corrected by shortening of the muscle, so that it can elevate the lid again. This is a tricky operation which needs a lot of experience, that it is enough, but not too much. You should look for an experiences oculoplastic surgeon.
