What is a facelift? This is a special invasive corrective intervention, thanks to which physicians correct the defect of soft tissue ptosis on the face. It is about the s, bags under the eyes and other problem areas, which inevitably occur with aging of the body. It is important to note that facelifting has the following effect:

Facelift For Asian People In Seattle WA

  • Smoothes wrinkles;
  • nasolabial folds are removed;
  • Eliminates signs of gravitational ptosis.

It should be noted that in particularly serious cases, this operation may include redistribution of excess skin, which can result from sudden weight loss or as a result of traumatic injuries. As for additional indications, here we are talking about the following:

  1. deep wrinkles on the forehead;
  2. folds in the neck area;
  3. double chin;
  4. vertical wrinkles on the cheeks
  5. lowered corners of the eyes;
  6. problem skin in the bridge of the nose and in the temporal-malar area;
  7. nasolabial furrows;
  8. sagging of the eyebrows;
  9. drooping eyelids.

It is necessary to draw your attention to the fact that facial rejuvenation and facelift requires careful preparation of the patient, which includes a full medical examination, during which possible contraindications (for example, poor blood clotting) are revealed.
