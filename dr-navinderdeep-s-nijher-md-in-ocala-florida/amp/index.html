Statement: Dr. Navinderdeep S. Nijher is a double board-certified plastic surgeon with more than 16 years of experience. A native New Yorker, Dr. Nijher resides in Ocala, Florida, where performs a wide array of surgical and nonsurgical aesthetic procedures at Ocala Plastic Surgery, serving patients from across Central Florida and around the U.S.

He is particularly sought-out for his cutting-edge techniques in minimal-scar surgeries of the eyes, face, and breasts, as well as his body contouring expertise. He specializes in mommy makeovers, breast enhancement, and face and neck lifts.
