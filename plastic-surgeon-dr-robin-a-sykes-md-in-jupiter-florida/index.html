What Restylane filler is best for cheeks: longevity, look, side effects?
cheek filler using Restylane
Apr 16, 2021
In my opinion, I would recommend Restylane Lyft or plain Restylane in the cheek area. Both have a similar lifting capacity, which is what you need under the cheek tissues. Once it has been dissolved, then you can consider starting with filler again after a few more days.

I'm two years into Tamoxifen. Am I able to have lip fillers?
fillers and tamoxifen
Apr 16, 2021
There is no reason that I can think of why lip fillers could not be done while a person is taking tamoxifen. If you are concerned for whatever reason about your own case, it would be best to check with your oncologist.
In general, the lip filler is pretty inert, and stays in the area it is placed in. If you are also on a blood thinner or aspirin, I might recommend that you hold off on that for a few days pre-injection.
