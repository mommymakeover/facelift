Mini Facelift Perth

Mini facelift in Perth is a face lift of a new generation. If you are a woman or a man older than 40 years and want to rejuvenate your skin, you should familiarize yourself with the operation “Mini facelift”, this is a unique procedure that refreshes the face and removes excess flabby skin.

“Mini facelift” gives your face a youthful appearance while preserving its natural appearance. The term “mini facelift” has entered the lexicon of plastic surgery, due to the actively increasing demand from patients wishing to undergo surgery with a shorter period of healing and with a low probability of complications compared to more extensive facelift.

The fact is that a less voluminous operation, as a rule, does not differ from a standard facelift surgery – the scale of the procedure is shortened, but its essence remains unchanged.

Acupuncture Facelift Perth Image

Mini facelift in Perth is an aesthetic procedure, the popularity of which is steadily melting, is especially recommended for younger patients (or patients looking younger), as well as patients who do not want to undergo an operation under general anesthesia.

As a rule, this procedure is performed under local anesthesia. The operation affects the lower part of the face and neck. The procedure and the healing process last very briefly, and therefore there is no need for hospitalization.

Mini facelift is a local and purposeful procedure, which is a good solution for cases when the process of weakening muscles in the face and neck area begins.

It slows down the appearance of future wrinkles and ptosis of tissues.

As a rule, we are talking about patients with lowered neck skin, “bulldog” jaw, facial wrinkles and symptoms of premature aging. In this case, the upper part of the face – cheeks, eyebrows, eyelids and forehead do not need a lift. The solution is a “mini facelift”.

The surgeon makes small incisions to achieve this result – usually behind the ear, or in front of it – which allows to work more smoothly, while small scars remain almost invisible.

Description of the procedure “Mini Face Lift”

Mini facelift lasts about an hour and a half. In the beginning the patient takes a syrup for the dimness of consciousness. The surgeon then injects an anesthetic for local anesthesia into the area that must be operated on.

The incisions are made on the ear line or behind the ear, so that the scars are almost invisible. Causing the incisions, the doctor tightens the underlying muscles and tissues according to the plan of operation and the desired results.

The skin is tightened if necessary. If there is a significant excess of skin, this excess is removed. Then the surgeon sutures, returning everything exactly to its place. Suture are bioresorbable , so they do not need to be removed. In some cases, the surgeon also uses laser peeling to give the skin a youthful and healthy appearance.

It is important to remember that facelift is a full-fledged surgical operation, and therefore it is associated with a risk of bleeding, bloating, redness, pain, complications (wound infection during surgery, bleeding, thickening of scars, temporary or permanent sensitization of the skin around the scars).
In most cases, these phenomena disappear after a few days.

The healing time is shorter than with full facelift and is about a week, without significant pain. Surgeons say that the results of facelifts are preserved for at least 7 years.

“Mini facelift in Perth” allows to quickly return to normal life, it fits into the current trend to simplify plastic procedures and shorten the healing period. The results of the operation will become visible immediately after the healing process is completed. Minimal pain, quick result, natural and fresh look.

The results of a mini facelift affects the appearance of the line of the jaw and neck, as well as the lower part of the face.
