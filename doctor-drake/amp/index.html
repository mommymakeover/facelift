ThermiVa with Blood Thinners/History of Blood Clots

Loss of sensation with ThermiVa?
ThermiVa and Loss of Sensation
Apr 3, 2019
Most likely what is going on is that there is either a tiny bit of swelling of the tissues that will resolve within a very short period of time or this is a result of certain hormone changes that can happen with normal fluctuations. Wait a few days to see if this resolves. If it doesn’t, go back to see your ThermiVa provider (who I hope is a gynecologist) and be evaluated. Thank you and good luck.
Is ThermiVa safe to do if you have a ovarian cyst ? Cyst is non cancerous
ThermiVa and Ovarian Cyst
Apr 3, 2019
Excellent question. ThermiVa will not affect an ovarian cyst in any way. Just be sure you are having your cyst followed appropriately whether or not you have ThermiVa done. Good luck!
I have kids, but may would like to have more. Would having this effect getting pregnant in the future
ThermiVa and Future Pregnancies
Apr 3, 2019
ThermiVa will not affect your ability for get pregnant or carry a future pregnancy. If you are planning to do this soon, I would not get your series of three treatments done until you finish childbearing as you will probably need a series of three again after the delivery. But if you are just considering a future child and want ThermiVa done now, go ahead and do it without worry. Good luck to you!

Is ThermiVa recommended to treat vaginal tightness for women who have had three deliveries?
ThermiVa for Post Delivery Tightening
Apr 3, 2019
Absolutely! ThermiVa was originally developed for this exact reason. It also helps with other common post-baby issues such as painful intercourse due to dryness and bladder leakage. It works by increasing the collagen formation in the vaginal mucosa.
I would recommend going with a provider who is an OB/Gyn to make sure there aren’t any other issues, and to make sure your results are optimal. Thanks for asking this important question.
