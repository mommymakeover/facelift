You need to see a hair transplant specialist to review the type of hair transplant you can receive to achieve your goals.

1 yr post op FUE 800 grafts. I’m 26 looking to add more density. Do I have to shave recipient
You never have to shave for FUE
May 3, 2019
Shaving is always optional (at least in my office). We have a variety of different procedures that allow us get by without shaving. Recipient shaving is up to you and most people after the first procedure and when they have a hairline don’t want to shave any more. Even donor shaving is optional now.
