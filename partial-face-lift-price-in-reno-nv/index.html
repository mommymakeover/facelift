Partial Face Lift Price In Reno, NV

Face lift surgeries are nothing but cosmetic surgeries, also called Rhytidectomy.

Mostly, these surgeries are helpful in reducing wrinkles, fine lines, sagging skin correction and to help older skin look young again.

Typically, these surgeries have proved to be expensive depending on the procedure involved.

Generally, the pricing depends on two governing factors; what kind of clinic and who the doctor is. Normally it starts with a $3000 maximum as per the requirement and procedures involved.

There are various types of face lift surgeries.

Standard Face lift in Reno: With this surgery, the main concentration is the lower third portion of face. That means the neck and sagging skin in these areas will be tightened and particularly the wrinkles on the cheeks will be given treatment.

Bruising Face Lift Result Photo

Mid Face lift: As the name signifies, the concentration with this surgery is on the face. The lower eyelids and cheeks are given a facelift. The mid face lift help those particular areas of skin become smoother.

Mini Face lift: This face lift is comparatively cheaper and the recovery time is also quick. It could have a serious outcome in the form of improvement to sagging skin, fine lines and wrinkles on the face.

This surgery focuses on the parts of the face that the patient is interested in if the doctor says that it is alright.

Thread Face lift: In this procedure, to attain smoother skin on the face, small threads will be inserted into the skin. With the thought of insertion, comes the thought of scars, but this procedure will take place while the patient is awake so that the patient can see what is happening; and scarring is nominal. This procedure hardly takes 1-2 hour’s time to complete.

After summarizing about the various types of facelift operations, we must learn about the risks that are involved. Your knowledge is not complete until you know the risks.

Typically, the risks or side effects associated with these facelift surgeries are temporary. The one common side effect seen in all are swelling and fluid retention which normally recovers a short while after a face lift surgery in Reno NV. The troubling part is numbness and scarring and which in some instances could become permanent too. Sometimes nerve damages also could happen but normally is minimized in facelift surgeries with proper precaution and care.

All this information is helpful in comprehending what goes on before, during and after a facelift surgery, but it is always better to have a proper discussion with a doctor before going for any face lift surgery.

The Modern Face-lift

Mini Facelift Reno NV

21st century face-lifts do not pull tight on facial skin as this results in an artificial “pulled” or “wind-swept” appearance with an expressionless face. Instead the features of correctly done modern face-lifts are:

  • Muscle-tightening of face and neck.
  • Firm yet relaxed skin.
  • Scars that are virtually invisible.
  • Healthy glow to facial skin…..often laser-assisted.
  • Over-all appearance of natural youth and freshness.
  • Useful Information

A complete modern face-lift when done with meticulous care and precision takes between 3 and 5 hours, depending on each individual case.

We work under local anaesthesia with sufficient intravenous sedation to make you comfortable and unaware of proceedings …….. a very safe form of anaesthesia.

You would need to be at home for no less than 8 days for after-care. There is very little discomfort {more a feeling of tightness} but considerable swelling occurs in the first 5 days. It will take a full month for you to be able to socialise normally. You will receive skin care advice after surgery.

If you are considering surgery you will need to see the surgeon the day before surgery. If you are a smoker you must try to reduce to not more than 5 cigarettes per day for 3 weeks prior to your operation. This will benefit your facial blood supply.

If you are more than 9kg or 20lbs overweight you should delay surgery until your weight is under control, as this will give you the best surgical result.

If you have any health problems/illnesses you must give details to the surgeon well in advance of your surgery, also naming any medication you may be taking. All these details can be e-mailed to us.

Old age is not necessarily a problem, as long as you are basically well and not suffering from heart or lung disease or uncontrolled high blood pressure. If you have had a previous face-lift you should mention this in advance to your surgeon.
