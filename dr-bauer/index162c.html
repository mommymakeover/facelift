There is minimal research on autologous fat cell derived stem cells. Does this work or not?
Stem Cells
Oct 13, 2023
Although there is no clear cut data, there are over 4700 NIH-sponsored clinical trials underway to understand all the pathways and possible application of stem cell therapy. It’s obviously the new frontier of hair restoration. Unfortunately, those studies are hard to evaluate and compare because they are not standardized. However, the overall results seem to show potential. For now it is still experimental with no clear cut guidelines regarding technique.

More time

Every patient is different but we will not assess our post op results before 250 days. At about 8 months you can expect to be at about 80% of results but for 100% final eval you really do need to wait a year. The natural hair cycle is LONG and it takes a lot of patience but will be worth it.
Is there any risk to having a hair transplant procedure 3 weeks after rhinoplasty?
swelling
Sep 19, 2023
My one hesitation would be that about 20% of patients get significant swelling of the forehead, eyes and bridge of nose after a transplant. Ideally, if you can avoid doing a transplant until 90 days, (assuming no complications from your rhinoplasty) then you’ll be better able to deal with this, if it arises.
