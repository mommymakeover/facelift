The contact medium (glycerine) is applied to the cleansed skin of the patient, then the doctor performs various movements on the problem areas of the skin with the help of the applicator.

During the procedure, and before and after it, the temperature of the skin is measured with a contactless laser thermometer. The temperature of the impact on the dermis depends on the area of exposure: on the face skin no more than 42 degrees Celsius, the body – no more than 46 degrees Celsius. The procedure ends with the removal of glycerin from the skin by applying a cream on it, or wrapping is recommended.

Recommendations during and after the procedure:

  • During the radio frequency facelift the patient needs to remove contact lenses and any metal objects;
  • You can not take sunbathing (sunbathing) after the procedure for three days;
  • the day of the procedure is forbidden any other impact on the impact of RF;
  • It is forbidden to expose the treated skin to any mechanical action (not scratch, and so on);
  • Within seven days after the procedure, it is necessary to refrain from taking anti-inflammatory drugs;
  • Take vitamin C for half a year to maintain the process of collagen synthesis.

Before the procedure, you should refrain from visiting the solarium, bath, and peeling. In addition, the skin of the face and body immediately before carrying out should be absolutely clean, without make-up.
