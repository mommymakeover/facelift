Sexual function after posterior repair
Posterior repair
Jan 16, 2022
A posterior repair is a vaginal surgery that restores the skin and supportive layers which separate the vagina from the rectum. In addition to decreasing vaginal calibre (tightening the vagina) it may also help to restore normal bowel emptying. Over correction after a posterior repair (too tight) can result in pain with sex (dyspareunia) or challenges with vaginal penetration.
