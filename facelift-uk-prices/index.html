Facelift United Kingdom Prices

Facelift in United Kingdom is called surgical intervention, the purpose of which is to eliminate age-related changes in the skin of the face and neck.

The age at which experts recommend perfoming facelift: from 30-40 years of age and older. Often, facelift is performed endoscopically, because it has many advantages over the classic facelift.

Endoscopic facelift is the low traumatic (in comparison with classical methods) plastic surgery, which avoids large skin incisions during facelift surgery.

This method involves making small punctures in the skin through which an endoscope (a small camera) is inserted, which displays the image from the operated area on the screen, and the tools needed for the operation.

Endoscopic facelift can significantly reduce postoperative edema, as well as avoid complications and damage to nerves, vessels and hair bulbs.

Indications for facelift and neck lift:

  • Age-related changes the facial contours (fuzzy outlines of the face);
  • Wrinkles in the forehead and temples areas;
  • Wrinkles in the area of cheekbones areas;
  • Nasolabial folds;
  • “Double chin”;
  • Bags under the eyes;
  • Omitted corners of the eyes;
  • Omitting the corners of the mouth;
  • Overhanging eyebrows;
  • sagging skin.

Contraindications for facelift and neck lift:

You Should Stop Smoking At Least Two Weeks Before Facelift

  • Acute infectious diseases;
  • Diabetes;
  • Severe diseases of internal organs;
  • Poor blood coagulability;
  • Oncological diseases;
  • Endocrine diseases in chronic form;
  • Pregnancy and lactation.

Preparation before surgery facelift:

Approximately 14 days prior to surgery is necessary to stop taking drugs, blood thinners (e.g., aspirin, and ibuprofen);
One day before the operation is necessary to give up heavy meal.

The process of facelift surgery:

Facelifting is performed under general anesthesia, the doctor makes the necessary incisions on the patient’s skin (in the scalp, behind the ears, etc.), sometimes the facelift is combined with liposuction in the chin and neck areas.

A Facelift Works Better For The Lower Half Of The Face

Endoscopic facelift:

Facelift by endoscopic method is carried out in all areas of the face: upper, lower and middle. Facelift surgery by this method requires a lot of experience of the surgeon, so we are glad that we can offer our clients facelift by the endoscopic method.

The upper third of the face:

When performing surgery on the upper face area, the surgeon makes a series of small incisions in the scalp. The endoscope is inserted into these incisions under the skin.

This is a miniature camera that sends an image to the screen in the operating room, as well as the instruments using which the doctor separate the soft tissues from the bone. This technique allows not to injure hair follicles and nerve endings. This facelift will allow to tighten the skin of the forehead, raise the eyebrows and eliminate wrinkles around the eyes. Endoscopic lift the upper zone of the face is performed under general or local anesthesia, the duration of the operation is an average of one and a half to two hours.

The middle and lower third of the face:

Surgery for skin tightening in the middle zone and lower zones is performed in order to improve the contours of the lower part of the face that have changed with age, to restore the volume inherent in young people, to eliminate nasolabial folds. The surgeon cuts the skin in the zone of the temples (in the scalp), under the upper lip (in the oral cavity).

After this, the surgeon separates the skin from the bone tissue and fixes it in the desired position. This type of facelift is performed under general or local anesthesia and lasts from 1 to 3 hours. Lift the middle and lower third of the face allows to make the contour of the face more clear, get rid of nasolabial wrinkles, lifts the skin of the dropped cheeks.

Recovery after a facelift

After the facelift in United Kingdom, the patient, as a rule, remains in the clinic for 2-3 days. At the same time, the post-operative swelling on the face is most pronounced. During the first week after the operation, it is necessary to wear a dressing bandage, and dressings and wound treatments are also required.

Facelift Can Be Combined With Other Operations Of All Sorts

The first month after surgery, you must exclude heavy physical activity. The result from the operation can be observed after 4-5 weeks (if an endoscopic facelift was performed) and 5-6 months if the face and neck lifts was performed in the classical way.
