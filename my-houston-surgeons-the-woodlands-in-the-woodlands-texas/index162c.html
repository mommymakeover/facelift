Scott Reis, MD

Name: Scott Reis, MD
Statement: Dr. Scott Reis is a board-certified plastic surgeon in Houston, Texas. Dr. Reis specializes in breast enhancement, tummy tucks, mommy makeovers, and body contouring, as well as eyelid lifts and other facial plastic surgery procedures. He also regularly performs hair restoration surgeries such as FUE hair transplant. He and his staff also offer a wide range of nonsurgical aesthetic treatments, from Botox and dermal fillers to laser skin rejuvenation and RF skin tightening.

At his private practice, Dr. Reis ensures that every procedure is done with the latest techniques and the highest level of care and safety. He is passionate about helping each patient achieve their aesthetic goals with natural-looking results.

Dr. Reis also has a special interest in helping patients who have experienced an injury or in need of reconstructive surgery after cancer treatment.

After graduating from Rice University with degrees in both finance and economics and biochemistry and cellular biology, Dr. Reis collaborated with plastic surgeons on a project that aimed to improve ear reconstruction techniques for children with congenital ear anomalies. This experience inspired Reis to seek a career in reconstructive and cosmetic surgery.

Dr. Reis earned his medical degree from the University of Texas, where he was accepted into the prestigious Alpha Omega Alpha medical honor society. This was followed by a six-year plastic surgery residency at NYU’s Institute of Reconstructive Plastic Surgery in New York City, where he trained under some of the most respected leaders in the field.

Dr. Reis is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He has contributed to various medical journals and is a member of prestigious organizations, such as the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the Texas Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgeons, the American Medical Association, the American College of Surgeons, among others.
