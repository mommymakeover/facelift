Tummy tuck vs. liposuction
Sep 22, 2021
Hi, thank you for your question. Liposuction alone will reduce the fat deposit but will increase the skin excess of the lower abdomen that is overlapping the pubic mount. Only a tummy tuck can address both, the fat and the skin excess.

Localized fat vs. abdominal wall weakness
Sep 22, 2021
Hi,thank you for your question. For this localized fat tissue you might be a perfect candidate for liposuction. Scars will be minimal, hidden in the belly button. Before liposuction a consultation with examination by a surgeon is necessary to rule out an abdominal wall weakness (diastasis recti) that needs to be treated differently.
