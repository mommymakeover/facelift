Making a hair transplant via FUE without shaving the head will be less effective?
No-Shave FUE
May 23, 2023
No-shave FUE is a great option for individuals looking for a smaller procedure who want minimal recovery time (i.e want to look "normal" as quickly as possible). No-shave FUE is just as effective as your standard FUE procedure, however, given that less of the donor is exposed we tend to harvest less grafts in each session. It's ideal for a patient looking for a hairline touch-up or transplanting a smaller region.

Topical Finasteride Post Transplant

Native vs. Transplanted hair

Can you damage a graft by applying pressure?
Transplanted Hair Risks
Feb 23, 2023
You cannot damage grafts by applying pressure 7-days post procedure as that is the amount of time it takes for the grafts to heal. We recommend that our patients apply pressure when massaging the transplanted area in circular movement so that scabs and redness/itching on day 7 post procedure, but to never pick any scabs. We recommend that you start finasteride and/or minoxidil to assist with regrowth and maintain results.
