There comes a time when the use of cosmetology techniques becomes insufficient to correct age-related changes. Then the face lift comes to the rescue.

What is a face lift? In our clinic in Bellevue you can be sure of an excellent result: the knowledge and experience of plastic surgeons of the clinic, as well as modern operational techniques are guarantee you a chic appearance.

Face lift in Bellevue is a procedure that can adjust almost all age changes. During the operation is performed correction of the skin of the face and neck, as well as the removal of excess skin and fat. You will look younger, on average, for 10 years.

Indications for facelift

  • Ptosis of soft tissues of the face;
  • Pronounced nasolabial folds;
  • The second chin;
  • The lowering of the eyebrows, the flabbiness of the skin of the forehead and neck.

Methods of facelift

Currently, it is possible to perform the following facelifts:

  • Full facelift ( “the circular facelift”);
  • Lift of the upper third of the face with an endoscopic eyebrow lift (these are the so-called modern minimally invasive techniques, since several small incisions are made in the scalp, hair (hair follicles) does not suffer, and the incisions heal practically without a trace);
  • lift of the middle third of the face;
  • Lift of the lower third of the face with / without liposuction of the chin area;
  • Surgery to eliminate the sagging neck muscles.

Preoperative preparation

The question of the need for a facelift (“circular facelift”) and its volume is decided on the consultation by a plastic surgeon. Quite often a face lift is combined with an operation to rejuvenate the eyelids – blepharoplasty (upper and / or lower) and lipofilling.

Examination is performed before the operation. A face lift is performed under intravenous anesthesia in combination with local anesthesia. The duration of the operation, depending on its volume, on average takes from 2.5 to 4.5 – 5 hours.

Facelift: preparation for surgery

Readiness for surgery is determined not only by the person’s internal mood for surgical intervention, but also by the general state of health, psychological and emotional stability. Preparation for facelift surgery consists of several steps:

Consultation with the surgeon is an important and crucial moment. The patient and the surgeon must find a common vision of the problem. Listen to the advice of a doctor: an experienced doctor knows not only how to make a facelift, but also understands the specifics of skin types, take into account the physiological characteristics of the organism in each specific case.

A facelift in Bellevue should be planned in a stable state of health. The body of the patient must be prepared for the upcoming changes. This means that in the near future you need to eat right, get all the necessary vitamins and minerals, useful substances.

For example, if you are planning a global weight loss, the result of the operation can be reduced to zero. Any significant change in weight leads to sagging of the skin, so the weight should be stabilized before the operation, so as not to lose the effect of its conduct.

You need to undergo tests and take tests. Facelift, like any other plastic surgery, requires the passage of standard medical examinations. Patients make a fluorography and ECG. We need the results of a clinical analysis of blood and urine, as well as a biochemical blood test.

It is mandatory to make the analysis of a blood clotting determine group and Rh factor, the absence of infectious and sexually transmitted diseases. You need to get rid of bad habits.

Facial Rejuvenator Conductive Gel

Strongly advised to quit smoking 14 days before surgery, not to drink alcohol and to balance your nutrition. The admission of medications diluting the blood is also excluded in the preoperative period.

The body should be ready for surgery to minimize the risk of various complications. Prepare the face and body for surgery. On the eve of the operation, you need to remove all cosmetics, do not use creams and other cosmetic products, wash your hair, take a shower.

A few days before the operation, a diet is recommended, and you can not eat or drink on the day of the face lift surgery. All the preparatory measures allow performing the operation without complications and will facilitate the course of the postoperative period.

Postoperative period

A special bandage is applied after a face lift in Bellevue, which must be worn for several days after the operation. Sutures are removed on the 12th – 14th day. You need to be prepared for the fact that the postoperative period is accompanied by the appearance of bruises (bruises) and swelling on the face, which take place within 2 – 2.5 weeks.
