What is a facelift?

In short, facelift in Sydney is a tightening of the skin and facial muscles. Of course, if you take care of yourself from an early age, the lift effect can be sustained long enough and successfully maintained without surgery.

Endoscopic facelift

You can often hear the question: what type of facelift is performed in 30 years of age, and why do it, because at this time there are only the first age-related changes?

The answer to the question “why” is very simple:

  • To ensure that these almost imperceptible changes are not noticeable;
  • Low-traumatic mini facelift allows to delay the need for a deep facelift for a long time.

Vampire Facelift Sydney Before And After

Endoscopic facelift is performed using a special device – an endoscope. With its help, you can eliminate the first visible manifestations of age-related changes in the face: shallow facial wrinkles, omission of the corners of the mouth, sagging cheeks, second chin.

The incisions during endoscopic facelift are tiny, less than 1 cm. They are usually located in the scalp or behind the ears, where they will subsequently be unnoticeable. Endoscopic facelift in Sydney, as a rule, lasts about 4 hours, and the results will please at least 7-10 years! Endoscopic facelift is perfectly combined with other plastic surgeries, because this operation is not highly traumatic and does not require a large recovery period.

Deep facelift

Deep facelift is required for patients with pronounced age-related skin changes. Such procedure is recommended in 40-50 years of age, but if you decide to do facelift surgery after 60 years old and you do not have any contraindications to it, there are no obstacles to it.

Dr Robert Drielsma Facial Face Lift

During the deep facelift of the face, the doctor separates the skin and deep covers (the so-called SMAS), if necessary cuts out excess skin and fixes the SMAS in a new (or rather, well-forgotten old) position. The incision, as a rule, is hidden behind the auricle or in its internal part.

After surgery, the patient is in the clinic for 1-3 days. Two weeks after a deep facelift, the edema subsides and the sensitivity of the facial skin is restored. Seams are removed on the 10-12th day, and a month later they become completely invisible.
