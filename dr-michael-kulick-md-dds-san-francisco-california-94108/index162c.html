Statement: Dr. Michael Kulick is a board-certified plastic surgeon in San Francisco. He specializes in a range of surgical procedures, including facelifts, neck lifts, eyelid surgery, rhinoplasty, lip lifts, tummy tucks, and breast augmentation.

A pioneer and advocate of technology, Dr. Kulick offers his patients the entire spectrum of these advances, from IPL photorejuvenation and laser hair removal to laser-assisted facelifts.

He also provides reconstructive procedures to help patients return to their normal life. “A healthy level of self-esteem contributes to an emotionally satisfying life, and a successful procedure will make you feel more confident and give you a self-esteem boost for years to come,” says Dr. Kulick.

After graduating from Northwestern University, Dr. Kulick obtained a DDS (doctor of dental surgery degree) from Northwestern University Dental School, graduating magna cum laude.

He then earned his medical degree from Loyola University Chicago’s Stritch School of Medicine, graduating magna cum laude. He went on to complete residencies in general surgery and plastic surgery at Stanford University Hospital, followed by a fellowship in hand surgery.

Dr. Kulick is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, as well as a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons.

He lectures nationally and internationally on the topic of cosmetic laser treatments, and has written numerous articles about the safety, effectiveness, and integration of this technology for surgical procedures. His book, Lasers In Aesthetic Surgery, has been published in the United States and internationally.

Over the course of his distinguished 36-year career, Dr. Kulick has received many honors, including being inducted into the RealSelf 100 & 500 Hall of Fame.

In addition to his exceptional surgical skills and artistic vision, Dr. Kulick is known as an honest and caring provider. “To have surgery is a significant decision in a person’s life, one that requires trust that’s facilitated by communication and follow-through,” says Dr. Kulick. “We are always here for our patients.”
