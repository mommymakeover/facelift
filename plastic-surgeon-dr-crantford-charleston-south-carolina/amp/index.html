Can Buccal fat pad removal go wrong?
Complications
Mar 14, 2019
Thank you for your question. Any time you have surgery there are associated risks. Buccal fat reduction is a relatively safe procedure with a low incidence of complications. The possible complications include asymmetry, swelling, nerve injury, parotid duct injury and infection. I recommend consulting with a board-certified plastic surgeon to discuss your concerns. Best of luck.
