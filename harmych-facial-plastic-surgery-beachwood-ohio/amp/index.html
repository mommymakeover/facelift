Doctors

Brian M. Harmych, MD, FACS

Name: Brian M. Harmych, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Brian M. Harmych, MD, FACS is a double board-certified facial plastic surgeon with nine years of experience based in Cleveland, Ohio. Focusing exclusively on surgical procedures of the face, head, and neck, he specializes in rhinoplasty (nose job), revision rhinoplasty, facelift, neck lift, eyelid surgery (blepharoplasty), and Botox.

Dr. Harmych received his medical degree at Wright State University Boonshoft School of Medicine in Dayton, Ohio. He went on to complete a residency in Otolaryngology – head and neck surgery as well as a fellowship in facial plastic and reconstructive surgery at the University of Cincinnati in Ohio.

Today he is an active member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and a Fellow of the American College of Surgeons. Dr. Harmych is also on staff at the Cleveland Clinic, one of the world’s most renowned hospitals, at the Cleveland Clinic Hillcrest Hospital in Mayfield Heights, Ohio.
