Do injections help maintain a facelift?
Staying young
Jun 27, 2018
There is no question that a facelift can provide one of the most significant rejuvenations for your face.  But what is also important is the ongoing maintenance that keeps things going in the right youthful direction.  Comprehensive simple effort makes a big difference.  Skin care, facials, microneedling, botox, fillers, all help to maintain youth. Would Fractional Laser treatment help my deep acne scars?
Some improvement
Jun 18, 2018
Deep acne scar changes are difficult to get resolve completely, however, a deep fractionated CO2 laser treatment can provide some decent improvement.  The expectation is for improvement, but do not expect that the marks will gone.  Often the more superficial scars can be improved the most.  
What do I actually need done? I want to just fix my eyelids, but was suggested a face and neck lift
Many options
Jun 18, 2018
Of course you don’t need to have anything done as this is a cosmetic treatment.  But, in order to look at this in a priority fashion, I favor upper blepharoplsty as the best option to achieve the best bang for your effort.  Next, a facelift with liposuction would provide a good result. Third most important would be a lower blepharoplasty.  In my experience, the more treatments that you do the greater the satisfaction when all has healed.

Do all facelifts include Platysmaplasty?
Most facelift surgery requires a platysmaplasty for optimum results
May 6, 2018
Not all individual patients who undergo facelift surgery are provided a platysmaplasty, however, the majority of patients would benefit from one in order to achieve optimum results.  The platysma bands noted in the pictures will only be rectified if a platysmaplasty is performed.  In most instances this requires a small incision under the chin for access to the platysma.
Neck lift lower facelift: Is tightness normal 4 days post-op?
Hardness is normal
May 6, 2018
Yes this is normal.  Underneath the skin the deeper plane of fascia and muscle were tightened and at a 2 to 7 day period post-operatively the tissue can become somewhat hard.  This is due to the healing undertneath, and due to the overall healing process.  After a few days the hardness will subside.  Ultimately the tissues we soften and appear and feel like normal soft tissue.
