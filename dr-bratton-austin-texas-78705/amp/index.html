My eye bags are just getting worse. What would be the most cost effective approach to reduce them?
Lower eyelid bags
Apr 20, 2020
In my opinion, a transposition lower eyelid blepharoplasty would be the best option here and most cost effective long term. With this method, the lower eyelid fat is redistributed to hide the tear trough rather than simply removing fat, which can sometimes make this area appear more hollow.
