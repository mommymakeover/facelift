How Long Does Botox Last?
How long does Botox last?
Dec 24, 2017
Depending on which facial muscles were injected, how much Botox was placed, and the particular patient's physiology around Botox, the effects last 3-5 months. Botox works by blocking the motor nerve's ability to release the "neurotransmitter" the triggers the corresponding muscle to contract. The nerve must then sprout new neurotransmitter "releasers" - and this takes a few months.
