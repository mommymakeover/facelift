Non Invasive Facelift vs Mini Facelift

Sometimes its really difficult to make up your mind on if you should spend thousands of dollars on a facelift surgery to buy back 10 to 15 years of your age. And when you have finally made up your mind, exactly which facelift surgery to go for becomes another tough choice.

In the present facelift surgery industry, aside from the typical full facelift, there are another two very popular types of facelift surgeries Mini facelift and non invasive facelift.

Differences of Non Invasive Facelift and Mini Facelift

For non invasive facelift surgeries, there are no incisions and long healing time involved as it is done with only injections. Fillers of various types are injected underneath the face to tighten up the loose skin and erase off the wrinkles. Whereas a mini facelift requires small S shaped incisions which are well hidden. Vs. the non invasive facelift, a mini facelift requires a minimum of 7 to 10 weeks for the incisions to fully heal and there may be minor post surgery complications involved.

Examples of Non Invasive Facelift and Mini Facelift

One of the most popular non invasive facelift surgeries in recent years is called the vampire facelift. It is named in such a way because of its unique method of injecting a mixture of the patients own blood and a liquid called Selphyl back to their face.

It is very popular among people with busy work schedules due to the fact that it requires no healing time and the whole process only takes around 30 to 45 minutes to complete.

The very idea that it is using patents own blood as under skin fillers makes it less likely to cause complications and the blood will also act as a agent to promote collagen growth underneath the skin.

Vampire Facelift Is Actually Not A Surgical Facelift At All

Beside the advantages of the vampire facelift, the down side of it is that it will need constantly reinforce injections to keep the youth effect every 9 to 12 months.

The Quick Lift

The Quick Lift is well known in the mini facelift category. It is a improved version of S-Lift which was invented 20 years ago. Designed for men and women aged between 40 to 65, the Quick Lift is able to significantly improve the appearance of lower to mid face as well as tightening the skin of lower neck to give patient a nice jaw line.

The advancement of Quick Lift is that it not only tighten the skin but also suspends the underlying tissues on a patients face, making the results last a lot longer than other types of mini facelift surgeries.

If taken care properly, the effect of a Quick Lift can usually last for about 3 to 5 years, depends on individuals lifestyles. Do note that smoking and alcohol addiction are some of the main factors that destroy your youthful look.

The disadvantages of a mini facelift is that it will take about 7 to 10 weeks for the incisions to fully heal and during which there might be complications like headache and swollen skin. And revision surgeries maybe needed if the incisions cannot heal properly.

The invention of vampire facelift has really made a lot of people happy. It is a non surgical facelift that is able to get rid of small wrinkles on your face within one 45 minutes to 1 hour. And it does not require any recovery time and hospitalization like many other traditional facelift surgeries.
