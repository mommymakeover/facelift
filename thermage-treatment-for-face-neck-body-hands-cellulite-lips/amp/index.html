Thermage Treatment For Face & Neck, Body, Hands, Cellulite, Lips

Are you looking to diminish the appearance of wrinkles? Do you want an alternative to surgery? Do you have two spare hours? Thermage could be the solution for you.

Known as the “lunchtime face lift,” Thermage is a short, non-surgical procedure that works gradually over time.

Unlike surgeries where the effect is immediate or procedures that require many visits with minimal results, one Thermage treatment effectively diminishes the appearance of wrinkles over a six-month time frame.

Its popularity has been widely recognized by the media: Oprah and other programs have featured specials on the procedure. Thermage treatments are completely non-invasive: a session requires no scalpels or needles and leaves the patient with no bruising or swelling.

The Thermage Skin Tightening Treatment

One session lasts approximately one to three hours with no additional downtime. Results are gradual and appear natural so you’ll never look like you’ve “had work done.”

The skin and the tissue below are rejuvenated, leaving skin looking smoother and firmer, and you looking younger.

Here’s how Thermage works:

Controlled heat is applied to the collagen while the top layers of your skin are protected with a refreshing cooling spray. The heat causes the collagen in the lower layers to contract and your skin tightens immediately.

This effect continues for up to six months as the stimulated collagen continues to be revived and grow, rejuvenating your skin to give you a much more youthful appearance long after the procedure is over.

Because Thermage treats the collagen in the lower layers of the skin, it can be used on virtually any part of the body. It began as a facial treatment but has since grown into a rejuvenating treatment for the neck, abdomen, arms, legs, hands, lips, as well as to eliminate unwanted cellulite. Thermage Treatments for the Face and Neck

Are you just beginning to notice the signs of aging? Looking for the benefits of a facelift without the surgery?

A Face and neck Thermage treatment could be right for you.

Thermage is a non-surgical, non-invasive procedure that promotes the growth of collagen already present under the skin while encouraging new collagen formation.

For the face and neck regions, Thermage is perfect for patients who want tighter and younger-looking skin.

Some of the conditions Thermage treats include:

  • Early jowling
  • Cheek folds
  • Sagging brow and eyelids
  • Loose skin
  • Acne scars
  • “Crow’s feet”
  • Wrinkles and creases around the eyes, nose and mouth

Thermage works by emitting a specific radio frequency that heats the collagen cells below the skin’s surface. The immediate reaction to the heat causes the collagen cells expand, creating the appearance of fuller, younger looking skin on the surface. Unlike surgery, one Thermage treatment offers the added benefit of continuing to promote collagen growth and expansion for at least six months after the session.

While not as extensive a treatment as a facelift, Thermage for the face and neck offers several advantages over a facelift procedure:

  • Non-surgical
  • No downtime
  • Immediate results
  • Continued improvement over time

The Thermage suite of procedures works on any type of skin on the body, but is perhaps best suited for the delicate face and neck area.

The FDA approved Thermage for the reduction of face and neck wrinkles in 2002, and since then over 20,000 procedures have been performed in the United States and Canada.

Because Thermage doesn’t involve surgery and results improve gradually you will never look like you’ve “had work done.” Initially you’ll look more rested and rejuvenated while results improve subtly over time.

Body by Thermage Treatments

In 2002 the FDA approved the Thermage system for reduction of wrinkles on the face and neck. The response was so positive that in 2006 the Thermage suite of treatments received additional FDA backing for use over the entire body. The resulting product was Body by Thermage.

Thermage For Arms Reviews

The best candidates for Body by Thermage treatments are people with loose body skin. New mothers who have an excess of skin after pregnancy make great candidates. People who have just experienced considerable weight loss resulting in loose skin are also excellent candidates for Body by Thermage, as well as older patients unhappy with the natural loosening of the skin that accompanies aging.

Other typical applications for Body by Thermage procedures are lax abdominal skin, “bat wing” arms and sagging thighs and buttocks. In all cases, Thermage treatments offer a quick and long-lasting alternative to surgical procedures.

The Thermage system works by emitting a radiowave into the lower layers of the skin while the top layers are cooled with a rejuvenating mist. The radio waves heat the collagen cells under the surface of the skin, causing them to expand and the skin above to take on a firmer and more youthful appearance.

A positive side effect that accompanies the heating of the collagen cells is the wounding of the collagen, which heals and regenerates for the next six months and prolongs the tightening effect.

In comparison to a surgical procedure, Body by Thermage has many advantages. Perhaps the most positive aspect of Body by Thermage is the relative absence of downtime.

Patients can typically return to their daily lives immediately following the procedure, experiencing only minor swelling and redness.

Unlike surgical procedures, the results of the Body by Thermage procedure are gradual and improve over time. With Body by Thermage results are immediate and the healing of collagen cells prolongs the rejuvenating effect of the procedure.
