Thermage Price List

Thermage is a noninvasive procedure that works to firm your skin in one session with no injections and no surgeries.

This one-of-a-kind technology has only been available since 2002, but it has already helped thousands of women achieve younger-looking skin. Read on to find out more about how Thermage works.

How It Works

With age, the collagen fibers in the skin will inevitably break down, leading to sagging and wrinkled skin. Thermage works to reverse this process by heating the body’s collagen with radiofrequency waves, which cause the fibers to contract, stimulating the development of new collagen throughout all three layers of your skin.

Before applying the radiofrequency waves, the doctor will draw a grid on the patient’s face to ensure accurate application. During each radiofrequency pulse, which can last between two and seven seconds, the Thermage machine also cools the top layer of the skin to prevent discomfort. After the procedure, which lasts about 45 minutes, patients can go back to their daily activities with no recovery period.

How Does Thermage Work

Effectiveness

Patients usually see results soon after just one Thermage session, but it can take two to six months to see the full effect. The smoother, tighter skin will last between six months and four years, and repeated treatments will build on one another to further repair the skin’s collagen network. Thermage has also been shown to reduce cellulite and loose skin on other areas of the body, though these results may not last as long.

If you’d like to reduce the signs of aging you see in the mirror, Thermage can help. Without invasive surgery or painful injections, your face can be firmer and more rejuvenated!

Early Thermage treatments were effective but not always comfortable for clients, who sometimes required pain medication. Thermage CPT has changed this, making Thermage treatment a painless and highly feasible method of rejuvenation.

Tips for Maintaining Your Newly Regained Youthful Appearance

Thermage treatments are a wonderful way to restore your skin’s vibrancy and your youthful appearance. Following a few simple skin care tips will help ensure that your treatment lasts for a long time to come. Protect your youthful glow by:

Wearing Sunscreen: Sun exposure is one of the most detrimental factors for skin health. Limit your amount of exposure by wearing sunscreen on a daily basis. Think of sunscreen as your foundation, and don’t leave home without it.

Investing in a Good Moisturizer: Your skin needs moisture just as the rest of your body does. Dehydration can cause aging to occur more quickly and allows wrinkles to develop more readily. Apply a good moisturizer to your skin at least once per day to keep it nourished, supple, and smooth.

Thermage CPT Provides Improved Results With Even Less Pain

Exfoliating Regularly: Your skin is constantly renewing itself – as old skin cells die, new cells are born. Sometimes your skin needs a little extra help shedding the old skin cells so that the new ones can shine through. Gentle exfoliation on a regular basis will remove dead cells and keep you looking radiant.

Living a Healthy Lifestyle: Your diet and lifestyle play a huge role in the appearance of your skin. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits and vegetables and take the time to get some exercise every day. Avoid using tobacco products or drinking alcohol, both of which can dry out and damage the skin.
