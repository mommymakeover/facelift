How Much Does A Facelift Cost In Virginia?

Every woman sooner or later faces the problem of obvious signs of aging. And each time the moment of their appearance you want to put it off as far as possible.

The popular facelift procedure in Virginia allows you to maintain the beauty and youth of the face effectively and at an affordable price.

With age, a second chin, sagging of the cheeks, wrinkles appear in the corners of the lips and nasolabial folds appears on the face.

These problems can not be cope with the help of conservative methods. These problems can be solved with facelift.

Facelift is performed in the following way: firstly, specialists make small punctures in the upper part of the forehead, a special instrument is introduced through the punctures, by means of which the excess skin is peeled, pulled upwards, and then fixed in this position by means of special threads; In the ear area, another incision is made, then again tighten the skin and focus in a tightened position.

This operation is minimally invasive, so postoperative recovery lasts no more than 10-15 days. In addition, after such an operation, the woman does not have any stitches.

Facelift in VA is a complex of surgical operations, the purpose of which is to eliminate age-related changes, correction of deep structures of the face and neck. During such a procedure, lifting of the upper, lower and middle parts of the face is performed, the external parts of the eyebrows, the zones around the corners of the mouth, the anterior and lateral parts of the neck are corrected.

Description of the procedure

Each patient should visit a specialist for examination before carrying out a facelift. A plastic surgeon assesses the condition of the skin of the face and neck, determines the degree of manifestation of age-related changes.

The specialist determines which method should be used in each individual case. Such nuances are always discussed with the patients before face and neck lift. Consultation of the surgeon makes it possible to evaluate the expected result, to discuss possible complications, to determine the cost and conditions of preparation for surgery and recovery.

Indications for the procedure

Indications for the facelift include:

  • Ptosis of the skin in the eye area, on the chin, on the cheeks;
  • jawls;
  • Flabbiness of the skin on the neck;
  • Loss of skin elasticity in the eyebrows and forehead.

Facelift gives the opportunity to return the skin to youth and radiance, and to yourself – confidence in its beauty and good mood.

Contraindications to facelift

The procedure is safe, but there are a number of contraindications that need to be considered before you contact a specialist. These include cancers and diseases of an infectious nature. When preparing for facelift surgery, it is necessary to refrain from smoking and taking medications.

Possible complications

Negative effects of facelift can be associated with the appearance of hematomas, tissue necrosis, and pigmentation spots. However, with a professional approach to carrying out skin plastic surgery, such manifestations are minimized. The results of our work you can find out from the reviews of our patients.
