Why do some doctors only offer FUE? Isn’t FUT better?
FUE vs. FUT
Feb 8, 2019
Most experienced transplant surgeons offer both surgeries. There are specific indications of why one may be superior to the other. Apr 2, 2018

Nasal valve collapse
Oct 12, 2017
Based on photos alone I cannot tell if you have nasal valve collapse. It would require an examination.
