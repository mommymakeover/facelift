If I have had Laser Hair Removal on the beard area and regretted the decision but only required a
laser hair removal
Jan 13, 2017
If you regretted your decision to have laser hair removal, then you should stop doing treatments and touch ups. You will need to give the area at least a year without any laser to see what grows back, before considering doing any transplant to the area. Yes, you can do FUE to the beard, but would need a consultation with a hair restoration surgeon to be evaluated.
Are FUE/FUT procedures more rigorous to preform on people of African descent than other racial cou
African descent hair
Jan 12, 2017
The short answer is yes. Patients of African descent have varying degrees of “curl” in the follicles that can be difficult to cut from a strip of tissue, or harvest in FUE. Be sure to select a surgeon who has experience with African hair to ensure you have the highest quality hair transplant.

You should double check with your surgeon, but at this point it should be fine, I usually have my patients start at the 14 day mark post op.
