Statement: Dr. Anastassakis is a distinguished board-certified Head & Neck surgeon specializing in surgical hair restoration and the founder of Anastasakis Hair Clinic in Athens.
Dr. Anastassakis brings a wealth of expertise and advanced training in the intricacies of head and neck anatomy to deliver unparalleled results in reconstructing and enhancing the appearance of both hair and scalp. Surgery is performed in person by Dr. Anastassakis and his experienced team of 11 assistants. Anastasakis Hair Clinic offers a comprehensive range of treatments such as, FUE hair transplants, repair, and revision of failed hair transplants (hairline in particular) and scars, Follicular Unit Strip Surgery (FUSS) with trichophytic closure, Body hair transplant (BHT) and more. What sets Dr. Anastassakis apart is the extensive collection of real-life before-and-after videos showcasing his remarkable outcomes.
As a pioneer in specialized techniques, Dr. Anastassakis is recognized for his artistry and commitment to patient comfort, implementing innovative methods to enhance local anesthesia.
Boasting recognition as one of the 220 board-certified surgeons by the American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery, Dr. Anastassakis has held prominent positions in the ABHRS Board of Governors from 2017 to 2021, underscoring his leadership in the field. Additionally, he is one of the 139 Fellow Members of the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery (ISHRS) and actively contributes to the scientific community through conferences and workshops on various aspects of hair transplantation. Driven by a dedication to patient safety, he serves in non-profit roles within ISHRS committees and conducts live surgery workshops to mentor aspiring hair transplantation practitioners.
Notably, Dr. Anastassakis is the accomplished author of the medical textbook “Androgenetic Alopecia from A to Z,” a comprehensive work with international acclaim, leading to its English publication by Springer in 2022-2023. This three-volume masterpiece covers every facet of the condition, from biology and pathophysiology to diagnosis, etiology, and related disorders, making it an invaluable resource in the field.
Throughout his illustrious career, Dr. Anastassakis has maintained a hands-on approach, personally performing all hair transplant surgeries in his clinic, leading three surgical teams, and providing guidance to thousands of hair loss patients for over a decade.
Experience the pinnacle of surgical hair restoration with Dr. Anastassakis, where expertise, innovation, and a commitment to excellence converge for the benefit of each patient.
