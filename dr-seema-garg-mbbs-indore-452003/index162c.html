Is hair transplant viable if I am 26 (F) with oily scalp and dry hair?
Female hair loss
Sep 5, 2019
First you shall take medical treatment for hair loss, Female responds very well with the minoxidil application and vitamins. Continue your treatment for at-least 4-6 month, if your density is improved you may not need hair transplant.
Can previous PRP injections cause issues for hair transplants due to scarring?
PRP and Hair transplant
Sep 5, 2019
PRP is injected by very fine needle and does not cause any scarring, so you can go for PRP sessions without any fear before hair transplant. At some of the hair transplant centre PRP is used during hair transplant procedure to promote the growth of the hair follicle, although level of evidence is III and studies are in progress to know the role of PRP in hair growth and Hair transplant but definitely it is supportive for hair growth.
Can Minoxidil still work after a laser hair removal treatment?
Beard restoration
Sep 5, 2019
Minoxidil acts on live hair follicle to stimulate the growth of hair and laser destroys hair follicle so it is not possible to get all the hairs back, after laser hair reduction, but you may get some of the hairs which were not destroyed by laser.
If you want good density of beard than you have to go for beard hair transplant. In this procedure hair roots are taken from back of the scalp and implanted on the beard hair.
