How long are drains usually left in after mommy makeovers? (Tummy tuck)
Drains following Tummy Tuck
Jan 6, 2021
On average drains will be removed 5-7 days after surgery. Sometimes the drains can be removed as early as 3 days, and rarely they can stay in for 10-14 days. Drains are removed based on the volume they collect. Wearing a tight compression garment, and limited physical activity will promote early drain removal.
