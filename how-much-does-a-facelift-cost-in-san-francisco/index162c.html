Unfortunately, age affects not only the internal organs of man. The first signs of aging are primarily manifested in our skin.

The most noticeable of them appear on the skin of the face. The aging process of the facial skin can be accelerated by various diseases, harmful environmental influences, harmful habits, stress.

Therefore, mimic wrinkles in the mouth area, eye, forehead, nasolabial and infraorbital furrows are formed by 35-40 years old under the influence of a whole complex of causes, the facial countours can change and then it becomes clear that cosmetology care is not enough.

In our time, a well-groomed and young face with elastic skin is the basis of success in almost all areas of activity.

What are the most important indications for Facelift?

Typically, a facelift is most in demand at the age of 40-50 years, when the skin of the face and neck is largely susceptible to sagging and stretching.

Sometimes, appearance correction is required at an earlier age of 30-35 years (premature aging). The operation allows successfully to eliminate any, even strongly pronounced manifestations of age-related changes.

Indications for facelift in San Francisco

  • Sagging skin of the forehead and eyebrows;
  • Formation of wrinkles in the nose region;
  • Lowering the outer corners of the eyes;
  • Deep vertical wrinkles on cheeks;
  • Deep nasolabial folds;
  • Ptosis of soft tissues cheeks below the edge of the lower jaw;

  • Ptosis of the eyelids;
  • “Double chin;
  • Forming wrinkles and wrinkles around the neck.

Contraindications to facelift

  • Severe diseases of internal organs
  • Infectious diseases
  • Oncology
  • Diseases of blood clotting.

Endoscopic facelift

Endoscopic facelift in San Francisco is a modern technique. All surgical manipulations during endoscopic facelift are performed by thin optical-fiber endoscopes. Endoscopic facelift, as a rule, is used for lifting the skin of the upper third of the face.

Anesthesia

Circular facelift and neck is a long operation, conducted under general anesthesia. Lifting of individual areas of the face can be performed under local anesthesia. Circular facelift will help patients look younger for 5-8 years. Of course, the result depends on various factors, for example age, skin condition, the presence of bad habits, lifestyle and work characteristics, as well as nutrition. Permanent care of a professional cosmetologist, mesotherapy procedures, lymphatic drainage massage, masks fix the result and serve as a preventive measure for the re-emergence of soft facial ptosis and wrinkles.
