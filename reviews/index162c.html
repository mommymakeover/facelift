Facelift Reviews

Are you tired of looking old when in fact you feel energetic and young? When you look in the mirror, who do you see there: yourself or an unfamiliar elderly person? What are you going to do?

Youth is the inner state of man, and not his external image. Unfortunately, in due course appearance does not correspond to our internal state.

The purpose of facelift is to keep time, to reconcile the external and internal state of a person!

Facelifting, or rhytidectomy, rejuvenates the neck, face, cheeks, eyes, and forehead. The concept of “rhytidectomy” comes from the Greek word “rytis”, which means “wrinkle”, plus “ectomy”, which means “incision.”

Restore A Youthful, Smooth Look To The Neck And Lower Third Of The Face

In fact, this concept includes a group of rejuvenating procedures that, due to professionals, will not make you change beyond recognition, but will give you a rejuvenated, rested look.

The essence of facelift is:

  1. redistribution of the skin;
  2. truncation of skin excesses;
  3. strengthening the muscles of the face;
  4. the formation of a new facial contours.

The neck and cheeks become tight, the line of cheekbones and chin sharpens, the skin acquires elasticity and youth.

Indications for facelift

Deep Plane Facelift Before And After Pic

  • deep lines that connect the wings of the nose and the corners of the mouth (nasolabial folds);
  • fuzzy contours of the chin or sagging oval face;
  • the sagging of the cheekbones and deep creases on the cheeks;
  • flabby skin, wrinkles,
  • excess fatty tissue in the neck.

During facelift, the plastic surgeon uses a scalpelto make incision starting at the temporal region and continuing to the front of the auricle. After reaching lobe of the ear, the incision bends around it, going out to the occiput. Then follows a broad disruption of skin of the temples, cheeks, chin, neck, with subsequent redistribution and excision of skin excess.

The surgeon moves the skin, fat tissues, muscles in such a way as to eliminate the sagging parts of the face, and then fixs the muscles and connective tissue with the seams (SMAS-lift). The skin is tightenedback and up, and excess skin is removed.

As a rule, face and neck lifting is performed during one operation. In addition to tightening the skin around the neck, the muscles of the neck, as a rule, are fixed through a small incision under the chin.

Thus, due to the tension of the skin, as well as the muscular-aponeurotic layer (SMAS), it is possible to:
  1. Firstly, wrinkles smooth out, making its almost invisible (which looks particularly impressive in the area of the cheeks, neck),
  2. Secondly, to recreate the aesthetically correct, harmonious contour of the angle between the lower jaw and neck,
  3. and, thirdly, to a large extent restore the facial contours, the omission of which primarily worries the patients.

Related procedures

Very many men and women make a circular facelift. This is the second most common surgery after eyelid surgery. Almost always facelift is combined with other procedures, such as:

Lower Facelift Also Trims Excess Skin, Smoothing It Down To Eliminate Facial Creases

  • elimination of heavy eyelids,
  • removal of bags under the eyes,
  • Rejuvenation of the forehead, removal of the overhanging eyebrows,
  • tightening of heavy cheeks,
  • formation of the neck and chin contour,
  • injection cosmetology,
  • laser liposuction of the chin and neck,
  • chemical peeling.

Despite the fact that, forehead lift is not an obligatory part of the circular face lift or neck, but there is, in fact, a separate operation, but sometimes it makes sense to discuss with the surgeon the possibility of performing it simultaneously with facelift. It is recommended, if possible, to combine a facelift and neck with a forehead lift (especially since the latter does not make the postoperative period worse). But you will definitely be satisfied with the result.

Male Full Face Lift

With the help of an endoscopic forehead lift, you can smooth out the deep horizontal wrinkles of this area, and also lift heavy “drooping” eyebrows, which will largely transform and rejuvenate the face. It is possible to perform blepharoplasty simultaneously with a circular facelift. Plastic surgeons with the help of microsurgical methods of suturing achieve remarkable results: incisions are practically not visible.

An integrated approach to solving the aesthetic problems of the patient, planning an operation with the expectation of maximum effect with minimal risks to health is the professional credo of our clinic.

Recommended age for a facelift

Midface Lift With HA

The age range of patients varies from 40 to 60 years old. It should be noted that facelift is successfully tolerated by both 70-year-olds and 80-year-old patients. Most plastic surgeons believe that the ideal candidate is “a middle-aged woman with a strong bone skeleton and a minimal amount of fat tissue.” A facelift is best performed when the face and neck already have signs of sagging, but the skin is still elastic.

Postoperative period

The stitches are removed a few days after the operation. This does not lead to the divergence of the edges of the wound (which many patients fear), since special strips are placed in place of the removed stitches.

Short-scar SMAS Face Lift

As a result, just a month after the operation, the seam is almost invisible. In the first 2-3 days after surgery, the patient is usually concerned about the swelling of the face, concentrated in the eye area. It should be noted that painful sensations occur extremely rarely and, if they occur, are easily removed by the simplest analgesics. Sometimes, unpleasant sensations in the seams area caused discomfort, which is quite acceptable in the first 2-4 days after the operation, because the body undergoes some changes. Subsequently, there may be a loss of sensitivity of some areas of the skin, a slight tingling. All this over time passes.

The degree of puffiness should in no case be projected onto the final result: you just need to be patient and wait until the end of the recovery period. In the first days after surgery, the patient is under the supervision of a surgeon who monitors the nature and individual characteristics of the healing process.

Recovery After Cervicofacial Rhytidectomy

A mandatory condition for adequate healing is full compliance with your surgeon’s recommendations and the performance of prescribed rehabilitation procedures. This is a wearing a compression bandage after a facelift. Some time after the operation you need to wear a special bandage. This will gradually release the swelling of the soft tissues of the face. After removing the bandage, the patient can return to a full life.

The final stage

The final result appears only a short time after the operation. The timing of complete recovery is very individual and depends on the depth of surgery and the characteristics of the organism: from a few weeks to two months.
