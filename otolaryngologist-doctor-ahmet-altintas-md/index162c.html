Statement: With over 19 years of experience, Istanbul-based otolaryngologist – head and neck surgeon Dr. Ahmet Altıntaş, MD has performed thousands of surgical and minimally-invasive facial procedures. He is particularly known for his expertise in performing first-time and revision rhinoplasty, facelifts, blepharoplasty (eyelid lifts), chin augmentation, and injectables, including dermal fillers and neurotoxins.

Dr. Altıntaş received his MD from Istanbul University Cerrahapaşa Faculty of Medicine in Turkey, then completed his residency in otolaryngology – head and neck surgery before going on to study maxillofacial and facial plastic surgery. Passionate about high quality patient care, Dr. Altıntaş is genuinely dedicated to providing every patient with an individualized treatment plan and a warm, considerate bedside manner.

In addition to operating at his Istanbul plastic surgery clinic, he is a frequent speaker at domestic and international professional seminars, regular contributor to scientific journals, and an instructor at Istinye University. Dr. Altıntaş holds hospital privileges at Memorial Bahçelievler Hospital and is a member of both the Turkish and European Society of Plastic Surgery.
